Purchase Machu Picchu tickets at the Machu Picchu Cultural Center in Aguas Calientes. Machu Picchu means "Old Mountain" in Quechua, the language of the Incas. The Spanish probably never knew about Machu Picchu, either, because they pursued the Incas in the opposite direction. Bingham found Machu Picchu -- purely by accident -- on July 24, 1911, while heading a Yale University expedition to Peru. Reaching the mountain Trains take explorers to Aguas Calientes, a neat and tidy town at the base of the mountain where Machu Picchu rests.
