Venezuelan Foreign Minister Elias Jaua accused the U.S. officials of using a visa program as a cover to meet with youth organizers at private universities "for training, financing and creating youth organizations through which violence is promoted in Venezuela. For weeks, Venezuela has been mired by deadly protests. Authorities are investigating the death of Jose Ernesto Mendez, elected official Cesar Rincones said Monday. A 17-year-old was hit by a truck and killed during a protest in Carupano, Venezuela, a government official said. And on Monday, the government said three U.S. diplomats have 48 hours to leave the South American country, accusing them of conspiring against its government.
