One person, identified as Robredo's aide, was rescued from the crash site, civil aviation chief William Hotchkiss told PNA. -- Philippines Interior Secretary Jesse Robredo is among three missing after a small aircraft crashed off Masbate Bay, the state-run PNA news agency reported, citing civil aviation authorities. Authorities were searching for Robredo and the two pilots, PNA reported. Robredo is a popular minister who is known for the transformation of Naga City into a premier place under his leadership as local chief executive, according to his official biography. Robredo was headed to Naga City to attend a political event, the agency reported.
