The problem is that unlike other states, Nebraska set no age limit when it passed its safe haven law a few months ago, so people are literally dropping off kids of all ages at Nebraska hospitals. No change in Nebraska's law will deal with the fact there are clearly a lot of parents out there willing to walk away, or they're so overwhelmed they don't know what else to do. In some cases, they are driving to Nebraska from other states to abandon their children. The laws are intended to allow parents to leave an infant at a hospital anonymously and not face prosecution.
