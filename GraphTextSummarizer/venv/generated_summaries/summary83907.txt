So in 2010, Lewis made it her mission to help other teenage parents become college graduates. Her Washington-based nonprofit, Generation Hope, provides tuition assistance, mentoring and life skills training to dozens of teen mothers attending area colleges. Less than 2% of teen mothers finish college by 30, according to the National Campaign to Prevent Teen and Unplanned Pregnancy. Fighting the stigma For Lewis, it's about helping teen parents beat the odds. Many of the students in Lewis' program are the first in their families to go to college.
