The bodies of Hannah Witheridge and David Miller were discovered early on September 15 on a beach on Koh Tao, a small southern island in Thailand. DNA match On Friday, Police Commissioner Gen. Somyot Poompanmuang told CNN that DNA in semen taken from Witheridge matched samples taken from the two men. Somyot said the two Burmese suspects would reenact the incident for police on Koh Tao later Friday. The deaths have shaken residents on the idyllic island, which takes up only 21 square kilometers (8 square miles) amid bright blue waters in the Gulf of Thailand. The island is popular among tourists for its diving locations, white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife.
