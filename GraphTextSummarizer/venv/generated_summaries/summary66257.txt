Billingsley, whose second and third husbands predeceased her, is survived by her two sons, Drew Billingsley of Granada Hills, California, and Glenn Billingsley of Phillips Ranch, California. "She will be missed by all of her family, friends, fans and most especially by me." Tony Dow, who played Beaver's brother, Wally Cleaver, also reflected on Billingsley's legacy. In the 1980s, Dow appeared with Billingsley in "The New Leave it to Beaver." Billingsley is related by marriage to actor/producer Peter Billingsley, known for his starring role as Ralphie in the seasonal TV-movie classic "A Christmas Story," according to the Internet Movie Database. Asked once to compare real-life families to TV families, Billingsley responded, "I just wish that we could have more families like those.
