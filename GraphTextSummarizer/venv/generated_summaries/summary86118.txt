Last week she released her third album, "100 Proof," an 11-song set of traditional country music reflecting the potent ballad style of classic stars like Dolly Parton and Tammy Wynette as well as a rowdy side on a few numbers. Pickler: I loved Dolly Parton, I love Loretta Lynn and Tammy Wynette. The first thing I ever said when I went into the Sony building was "I love Tammy Wynette, I love Dolly Parton, I love Loretta Lynn." Look, times are tough for record companies and I get that they have a formula and have to play certain things safe. CNN recently spoke with Pickler about her new album, ditching a pop sound and writing with Taylor Swift.
