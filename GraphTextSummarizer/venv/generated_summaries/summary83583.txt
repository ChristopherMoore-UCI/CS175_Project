Councilman Jose Huizar was fined $2,100 for accepting tickets to the 2007 Academy Awards. The city ethics commission fined Councilman Herb Wesson $3,900 for accepting tickets to the BET Awards in 2007, 2008 and 2009. Councilman Eric Garcetti was fined $4,800 for accepting tickets to the 2007 Emmy Awards and the 2007 and 2008 Oscars. Councilman Tony Cardenas was fined $2,500 for accepting tickets to the 2008 Emmy Awards. "While I paid the full cost of the awards show tickets," Garcetti said, "I messed up when it came to the dinners afterward.
