Bulone put Marcus Mauceri under oath, and according to court transcripts, Marcus swore that he was Matthew. According to court officials, 40-year-old Matthew Mauceri was due in court Tuesday morning for the beginning of his trial on scheming-to-defraud charges. So, Marcus Mauceri showed up in the courtroom of Judge Joseph Bulone, with Marcus claiming he was Matthew, according to the officials. In the judge's chambers, defense attorney James Thomas told Bulone he had represented both brothers previously, and that the right brother might not be present in court. Later, Matthew Mauceri appeared and was cited for failure to appear and contempt of court.
