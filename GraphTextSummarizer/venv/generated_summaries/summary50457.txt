This may not be Howard's final World Cup. Team USA goalie Tim Howard on one hand. He wasn't really that good when he started out When Howard began his pro career as a 22-year-old goalkeeper for the New York/New Jersey MetroStars of MLS, he was known as a great athlete who could make great saves. Facing the relentless onslaught of Belgium's offense, Howard made 16 saves -- setting a World Cup record. Howard has yet to say whether he wants to be in the net when the team tries to qualify for Russia, host of the 2018 World Cup.
