Watch how Kanye West is mourning his mother » Donda West was indeed proud. » At the MTV Video Music Awards in September, she and a gaggle of girlfriends were perched in the VIP section at a party for Rolling Stone magazine. On "Can't Tell Me Nothing," a track from his latest album, "Graduation," he rhymes about his mother's attempt to discourage him from spending money on jewelry and "Louis V." On "Touch the Sky," from his 2005 album, "Late Registration," he recalls how his mother drove him from Chicago, Illinois, to New York in a U-Haul van. Long before her son became an international superstar, Donda West was an English professor at Chicago State University. When asked if her son would sell more records than 50 Cent (both rappers released their albums on September 11), she answered coyly.
