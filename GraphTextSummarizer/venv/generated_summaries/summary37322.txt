-- Glastonbury festival held in Somerset, England every year is the world's biggest open air arts and music festival. After the clear up, the site will return to a working dairy farm until next May when preparations for Glastonbury 2010 will begin. Among the highlights this year were the newly reformed Brit-pop band Blur, 'The Boss', Bruce Springsteen, dance queen Lady Gaga and The Prodigy among many others. Past performers include rapper Jay-Z, David Bowie, Rod Stewart, Paul McCartney and Shirley Bassey. A fan gets into the spirit of the Glastonbury music festival.
