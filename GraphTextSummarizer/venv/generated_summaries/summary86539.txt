And, if nothing else, Taxi's newfound Internet fame has added an interesting bit of education about seizure alert dogs. So, they warn people to exercise caution when considering this type of canine service. Thus, Taxi never graduated to calmly lead blind people across the street, for fear that he might actually lead blind people across a tree. When Rachel takes Taxi with her to school, he's there to warn of oncoming seizures and to also assist her in the event of one actually happening. When Taxi's big, serious face appeared in the Hector Garcia Middle School yearbook and hit the Web, people just couldn't resist.
