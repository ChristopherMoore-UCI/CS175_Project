Dr. Rajendra Pachauri The IPCC was the joint winner -- along with Al Gore -- of the 2007 Nobel Peace Prize. -- As head of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC), Dr. Rajendra Pachauri is at the forefront of global efforts to combat climate change. In 1999, Dr. Pachauri was appointed to the board of both the Indian Oil Corporation Limited and the Institute for Global Environmental Strategies (IGES) in Japan. Pachauri -- an economist and environmental scientist -- has been Chairman of the IPCC since 2002.
