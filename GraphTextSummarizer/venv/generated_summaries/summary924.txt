His latest invention is the brain computer music interface (BCMI) which allows people to create music using just their eyes. Ever since musician Eduardo Miranda met a patient with locked in syndrome 11 years ago, he has been on a mission to create a way for the paralyzed to make music. Motivation Speaking of his inspiration for the project, Miranda says: "I wanted to create something to enable people with severe disabilities to make music. "Our brain is producing electricity all the time," says Miranda, head of the Interdisciplinary Centre for Computer Music Research at Plymouth University. Flashing icons representing different snippets of music appear on screen and you can make a selection, just by staring at one.
