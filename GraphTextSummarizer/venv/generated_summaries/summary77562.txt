Your guide to fibroid fixes Severe bleeding Ten million American women have heavy bleeding, also called menorrhagia. Killer cramps Virtually all women in their childbearing years have period pain (or dysmenorrhea). While mainstream docs are mostly neutral on the idea of increasing omega-3s to fight period pain, some think it makes sense. Many women, perhaps 95 percent, have period issues -- bleeding like Sutherland's, debilitating pain, out-of-control PMS, or annoyances such as constipation or diarrhea. And a recent study found that after seven years, more than 95 percent stopped having periods.
