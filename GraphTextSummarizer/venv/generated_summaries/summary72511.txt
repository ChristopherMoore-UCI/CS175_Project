South Korea is calling off trade with the North, closing off access to its waters for North Korean ships, and setting up a new military plan with regard to the North. North Korea says that it's considering the situation as the first phase of a war, and that if the South does anything, the North will respond. President Obama ordered military commanders to work with South Korea to prevent any aggression from the North.
