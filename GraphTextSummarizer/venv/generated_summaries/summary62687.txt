Yes, many skeptics predicted terrible consequences, but six months after legalization of recreational marijuana in Colorado, the sky still hasn't fallen. Applications to attend Colorado schools have increased -- to no one's surprise. Colorado has struggled with problems linked to marijuana edibles. Colorado travel is up. People who have eaten or ingested too much of a marijuana-laced treat have become nauseous and extremely anxious; half of the 79 calls to medical facilities concerning marijuana in the first four months of legalization regarded edibles.
