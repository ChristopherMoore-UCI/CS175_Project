In other matches on Sunday, former Danish legend Michael Laudrup is still waiting for his first victory as Real Mallorca coach, after his side lost 2-0 at Sporting Gijon. Italian striker Giuseppe Rossi helped himself to a double as Villarreal secured their first Spanish Primera Liga win of the season with a 4-0 thumping of Espanyol on Sunday. Midfielder Borja Valero and Brazilian international Nilmar were also on target as Villarreal warmed up in style for their Europa League match against Dinamo Zagreb next Thursday. The Madrid-based side, who finished sixth in La Liga last season. secured their first win of this campaign with a 4-1 thumping of promoted Levante.
