IKEA desks and chairs can be the height of style in these work spaces, and many don't build in a budget for much decor beyond that. The philosophy: Startup employees are working a minimum of 10 hours each day, so why shouldn't they enjoy their surroundings? Tech startups are notoriously known for being scrappy and bland when it comes to office decor. LinkedIn Though well-established LinkedIn is no longer a startup, Krista Canfield, a senior manager of corporate communications, has decorated her space in true startup style. Her personal office is called the "Classic" room and is inspired by the Classic Chic fashion styles that she says many Stitch Fix customers favor.
