"We started receiving complaints from residents about water shortages since the end of July," said Liu Zhongmin, head of the Water Resources Office in the Department of Water Affairs of Haidian District. Growing demand The Shuimo Community in Haidian district has grown rapidly and authorities have been unable to cope with the growing demand for water. They lift tiles off the pavements and dig until they hit ground water. "Who would dig up wells if there's enough water? "We're aware of local residents digging wells to get water. The community has grown from 1,000 residents to more than 8,000 in recent years, and new residents have occupied roads where pipelines are buried under their houses, according to Liu.
