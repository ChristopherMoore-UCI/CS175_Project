Indeed, many creators of fantasy games and stories today probably cut their teeth on D&D as kids, or teens. Video game creator Ken Levine didn't have friends to play D&D with when he discovered the game in 1978. Universally considered the first modern role-playing game, D&D set the benchmark for many future RPG games -- either the table-top or computer variety.
