-- For many Americans, the scariest phrase in the English language is "changing demographics. In the early years of the 21st century, many white Americans are trembling at the thought that -- within three decades -- they'll be the minority in the United States. Now comes this bit of news, which is likely to send more Americans scrambling for their blood pressure medicine. Where some Americans look at changing population figures and see calamity, I only see opportunity. Specific to what will be happening in the schools, we can also expect greater demand for bilingual teachers and more resistance from older taxpayers to having to approve additional property tax bonds to pay for what will be a necessary expansion of school facilities in the years to come.
