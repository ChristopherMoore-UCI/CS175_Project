The Supreme Court has supervisory responsibilities over such court buildings in the Philippines, the PNA cited Prosecutor General Claro Arellano as saying. The official Philippines News Agency (PNA) reported that the gunman "went berserk" at the Hall of Justice the city of Cebu. Besides the gunman, the other person wounded in the attack was Maria Theresa Casino, a prosecutor who is in a critical condition, according to the PNA. The incident is likely to add to the debate over stricter controls on firearms in the country, which has been rattled by a series of recent shootings. Cebu police officials weren't immediately available to comment on the matter.
