Held in the picturesque cliffside town of Diamante in southern Italy, the marathon is the eye-watering highlight of an annual Chilli Pepper Festival that celebrates obsessions with super-heated seasoning in the surrounding Calabria region. Calabria, the region surrounding Diamante, is Italy's kingdom of chilli and is famous for its tangy cuisine. Chilli pepper is Calabria's brand. Life without chilli is plain boring," says Monaco. Last year Capocchiano gulped down 560 grams (19.7 ounces) of diavolilli, the so-called "little devils" that are typically the hottest peppers found in Italy.
