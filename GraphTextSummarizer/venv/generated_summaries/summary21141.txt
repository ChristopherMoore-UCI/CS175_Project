MADRID, Spain  -- Sevilla midfielder Antonio Puerta has died after collapsing during a Primera Liga match on Saturday, Spanish media reported on Tuesday. Earlier on Tuesday, a statement from Seville's Virgen del Rocio hospital had indicated that Puerta's condition was worsening. He was able to walk from the pitch but doctors said that he collapsed again in the changing rooms and had to be given cardiac resuscitation before being taken to hospital where he remained on a life support system. Puerta is the third high-profile footballer to die after collapsing on the field in recent years. Sevilla midfielder Puerta was able to walk from the pitch but collapsed again in the dressing room.
