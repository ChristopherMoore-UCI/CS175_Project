Lu Stout makes her home in Hong Kong, China, a city as stunning by day as it is by night -- and memorable for its harbor, shiny skyscrapers and "unique blends of Eastern and Western traditions," in the words of the Hong Kong Tourism Board. Have a walk around the interlinked Hong Kong Land buildings in Central to have an idea of just how convenient Hong Kong is.
