Gwen Stefani, 2003 She's sported pink and blue hair, but it's Stefani's signature platinum locks and bumped bangs that made the Tragic Kingdom singer a pop icon. Her thick thatch of bangs, cut straight across the forehead, became a popular look with flappers seeking to defy society's vision of beauty. -- Bangs may be a modern red carpet staple, but they actually have a long history (hair-story?) in Hollywood. Whether wispy or thick, feathered or blunt-cut, bangs are the go-to hair choice for celebs who need a new coif. Jane Fonda sported bangs and a defiant glare in 1970, because, well, it's important to leave Barbarella alone.
