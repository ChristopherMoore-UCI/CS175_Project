Known as the "lady" in Myanmar, Suu Kyi has been compared to former South African President Nelson Mandela, who spent a chunk of his life in jail for fighting apartheid. Aung San Suu Kyi is the very embodiment of Myanmar's long struggle for democracy. In an interview with CNN, Suu Kyi, in fact, likened Myanmar's plight to South Africa's former brutal race-based system. Over the years, Suu Kyi has repeatedly challenged the junta and discouraged foreign investment in Myanmar. Over the years, Suu Kyi has made it clear that she remains devoted to bringing democracy to Myanmar.
