Mark Fuller accepted "pretrial diversion" offered by Atlanta prosecutors. Whether the Rices and Fullers stay married is their business; but whether Ray Rice and Judge Mark Fuller committed crimes should be a question for prosecutors, and ultimately, juries to decide.
