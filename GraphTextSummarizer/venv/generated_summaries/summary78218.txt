What you need to know about synthetic drugs 6. Other kinds of synthetic drugs can be sprayed onto plant material and smoked, such as synthetic marijuana. Almost all the chemicals in Molly and other synthetic drugs come from laboratories in China. The DEA says only 13% of the Molly seized in New York state the last four years actually contained any MDMA, and even then it often was mixed with other drugs. Someone who buys or takes Molly now is probably ingesting dangerous synthetic drugs that have not been tested and are produced in widely varying strengths.
