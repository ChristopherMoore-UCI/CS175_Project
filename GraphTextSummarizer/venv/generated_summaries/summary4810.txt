A car bombing killed five people and wounded 43 others in a Shiite Turkmen neighborhood in Tuz Khurmato. -- At least 19 people were killed and more than 100 were wounded Tuesday in bombings in Iraq, police said. On Tuesday evening, five people at a busy outdoor market on the western outskirts of Baghdad were killed by a car bomb. The latest attacks come amid a recent uptick in Sunni-Shiite violence in Iraq that has killed at least 266 people in the past three weeks, according to a CNN tally. Yet another bomb in southern Baghdad injured four people.
