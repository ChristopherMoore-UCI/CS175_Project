Copy editors "merely" prepare content, some publishers say, and don't we have Web editors to do that? Perhaps the most misguided reason for not having copy editors is "reader engagement." Another stated reason for not having copy editors is that readers don't care about typos. Besides, emerging research shows that readers view edited news more positively than they view unedited news. Copy editors provide a safety net for a publication, catching most of the problematic stuff dropped from above.
