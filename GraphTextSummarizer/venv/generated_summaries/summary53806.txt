Bob Marley's music used to help famine victims in Africa Other famous folk who have had a species named in honor of them include President Obama (a lichen), Comedian Stephen Colbert (a bug) and Elvis Presley (a wasp). You may get a chance to see these Marley creatures yourself; Sikkel and his team have sent some to the American Museum of Natural History in New York. Remembering Bob Marley Sikkel describes his research in the June 6 edition of Zootaxia, a journal for animal taxonomists (folks who classify species).
