The court banned sales in South Korea of Apple's iPhone 4 and iPad 2, Samsung's Galaxy SII and Galaxy Nexus smartphones and Galaxy Tab and Galaxy 10.1 tablet computers. Similar patent cases between Apple and Samsung are being heard in Germany, the United Kingdom and Australia. -- A South Korea court ruled that both Apple and Samsung violated each other's patents, in a case that mimics legal battles between the electronics giants around the world. Samsung denies any wrongdoing and is counter suing Apple for $519 million, also for patent infringement. Apple accuses Samsung of copying the design of the iPhone and iPad, and is suing Samsung for $2.5 billion.
