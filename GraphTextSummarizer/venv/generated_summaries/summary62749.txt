A suicide bomber rode a motorbike up to a security checkpoint a third of a mile from the consulate and detonated 22 pounds of explosives attached to his body, police spokesman Mohammad Faisal said. Violent attacks occur frequently around Peshawar, which is in Pakistan's northwest near the border with Afghanistan and adjacent to Pakistan's tribal region. It has been the site of recent clashes between Pakistani security forces and militants. Along with those killed, the strike also injured 31 others, Pakistani military officials said.
