The growing popularity of vegan bake sales makes Loewenthal think about the future of the Worldwide Vegan Bake Sale. More than 120 groups across six continents are holding vegan bake sales from April 24 through May 2. When Loewenthal started researching vegan bake sales in 2008, a Google search yielded few results. In late January, more than 25 U.S. cities held vegan bake sales to raise money for Haiti hurricane relief. Some people become vegan for animal welfare reasons.
