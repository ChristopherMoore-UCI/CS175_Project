" Gaza Music School opened toward the end of 2008, but within months it had been destroyed in the Israeli invasion of Gaza. "We hold a bi-annual national competition and the Gaza students participate by video conference. Many instruments, such as the piano, were destroyed." Reopened in a new building in a different part of Gaza, the school now has 130 students and is so oversubscribed that only 15% of applicants are accepted. This is one of many logistical problems faced by staff and students at Gaza Music School, the only music school in the 360 square kilometer strip of land controlled by Israel.
