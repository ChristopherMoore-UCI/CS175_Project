The advertisement showed Argentine field hockey star Fernando Zylberberg training in the streets of Port Stanley in the Falklands. The Argentine government in recent months has stepped up its rhetoric over the Falklands, saying that the U.K. is exploiting the resources of the islands it calls Las Malvinas. The Falklands have been under British rule since 1833. The video ends with the slogan: "To compete on English soil, we train on Argentine soil." Short accused the Argentine government of misleading rhetoric that implies that there is local support for Argentine rule.
