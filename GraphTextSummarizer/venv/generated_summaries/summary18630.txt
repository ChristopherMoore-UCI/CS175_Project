"New Jersey smells bad," "New Jersey drivers are the worst!" Jersey City Go on a food tour of diverse Jersey City. West New York, New Jersey Hungry after the hiking and biking? You'll find authentic and affordable Mexican food at Taqueria, a savory crepe at French restaurant Madame Claude's, new American food using seasonal, local ingredients at Thirty Acres, and don't miss Marco and Pepe's for brunch (three words: croissant French toast). Cape May Finally, for a real getaway, keep driving south until you reach the southern tip of Jersey and charming and quaint Cape May, which stakes a claim as the nation's oldest seaside resort.
