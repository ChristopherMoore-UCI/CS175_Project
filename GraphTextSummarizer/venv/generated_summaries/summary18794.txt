For now, videos can only be shot from within the Instagram app, and there is no way to upload existing video from your phone's camera roll. But the coolest feature may come after you shoot the video: 13 brand-new filters, created by an artist specifically for Instagram video. The changes were announced by Instagram co-founder and CEO Kevin Systrom during a press event at the headquarters of Facebook, which bought Instagram last year. Vine, Instagram and the rise of bite-sized video On Instagram, the videos can be between three and 15 seconds long and include any number of individual video clips. The new video feature was made available to all iOS and Android Instagram users around the world Thursday as a free update to the existing Instagram app.
