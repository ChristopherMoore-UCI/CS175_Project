Just before halftime, Carroll caught United goalkeeper David De Gea after going up for a corner -- a challenge that infuriated Ferguson, who later said it should have been a red card. Chelsea cruised to a 3-0 win over Fulham to strengthen their claim on a Champions League spot and move into third place. West Ham retook the lead ten minutes after the break as Mohamed Diame fired a low shot into the corner past De Gea. Manchester United edged closer to a record 20th English Premier League title despite having to rely on a late equalizer from Robin van Persie to rescue a draw at West Ham. In Wednesday's other English top flight game Chelsea won 3-0 at local rivals Fulham to move back into third place in the table and edge ahead of London rivals Arsenal and Tottenham in the battle for the European Champions League places.
