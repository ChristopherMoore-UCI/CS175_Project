U.S. President Barack Obama issued a statement Monday expressing "deep condolences for the tremendous loss of life and devastation." Survivors in the hardest-hit areas are contending with no electricity or clean drinking water. Water-logged bodies from washed-away villages floated at the shoreline on the northwestern coast of Mindanao island. One woman in Cagayan de Oro collected murky brown floodwater in a bucket, just meters away from where a destroyed vehicle was submerged. Tropical Storm Washi affected more than 167,000 people, the council said in a report Monday.
