Complementary currencies are colorful slips of paper designed with images of local scenes or landmarks. BerkShares are a form of local currency developed in the Berkshire region of Massachusetts two years ago. Paul Glover, the founder of the Ithaca Hours complementary currency for use in Ithaca, New York, has been bombarded with requests to help set up local currencies around the country. Printing and distributing local currency isn't illegal. Finally, any income received in local currency must be taxed as if it were federal dollars.
