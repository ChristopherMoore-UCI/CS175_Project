His research and teaching interests include American religious history, African American religions, hip hop, popular culture and the arts. Recent public debates around black liberation theology often portray black churches as occupying an alternative universe. Many prominent black cultural institutions and artists began in churches. Black churches have always been a place where people have come to sway, to dance, to shout and -- value judgments aside -- to be entertained. Rather, they show black churches to be exceptional even as they are quintessentially American.
