"Meizu, OPPO and Xiaomi are poised to differentiate themselves as strong mid-range smartphones, like a sedan," says Josh Ong, China Editor of The Next Web. China is forecast to overtake the U.S. in smartphone shipments and become the world's leading smartphone market this year, according to research group IDC. Break it down by marketshare and, according to IHS iSuppli Research, in the first half of 2012, three of the top five smartphones sold in China are local Chinese brands: Lenovo, Coolpad and Huawei. The mainland China smartphone market has been partly fueled by the demand for lower-cost handsets. The fan base for China's sleekest smartphones will remain in China for now.
