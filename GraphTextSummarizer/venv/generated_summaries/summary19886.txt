In a new survey from Zagat, Southwest Airlines swept several satisfaction categories, including best value among domestic airlines for the third year in a row. Forty-five percent of people try to avoid flying airlines that charge for checked luggage. This is Continental's third year winning the premium seating category among large domestic airlines. Continental Airlines and JetBlue Airways topped the survey for premium seating and economy seating, respectively. Singapore Airlines was the top-rated international airline for both premium seating and economy seating, receiving a survey-high score of 28 out of 30 for its premium seats.
