GirlUp -- United Nations Foundation is a campaign inspiring American girls to take action for girls in countries such as Ethiopia, Guatemala and Liberia who face a lack of access to education and are susceptible to child marriage, high maternal death rates and HIV infection. -- CNN Films' "Girl Rising" tells the stories of girls across the globe and the power of education to change the world. Yasmin, the girl in Egypt in "Girl Rising," was part of a Plan International USA program helping to keep street girls safe. Plan International USA tries to give girls around the world access to food, water, education, financial security and protection from sexual violence and exploitation.
