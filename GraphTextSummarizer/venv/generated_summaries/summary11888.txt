McCourt is survived by his wife, Ellen, and other family members, including brother Malachy McCourt, who is an actor and writer. The author was born to Angela and Malachy McCourt on August 19, 1930, in Brooklyn, New York, to Irish immigrants. Author Frank McCourt, whose tragic childhood became creative grist for his first book, the Pulitzer Prize-winning "Angela's Ashes," died Sunday, according to the Web site of his publisher, Simon & Shuster. In the book, McCourt described a desperate childhood marked by severe poverty, malnutrition, neglect, infectious disease and loss.
