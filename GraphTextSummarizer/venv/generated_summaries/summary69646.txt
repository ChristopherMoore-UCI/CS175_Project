Keeping family together Javian and her group consult with social workers from three hospitals to identify families who need them the most. " From one heart to another Since 2009, Harboring Hearts has helped cover more than $150,000 in services for families, Javian said. After her father passed away in 2008, Javian teamed up with a friend to co-found Harboring Hearts. The New York City nonprofit provides financial and emotional relief to families battling heart disease. Within 48 hours, Javian's group was able to find a hotel for the family.
