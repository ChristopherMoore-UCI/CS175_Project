-- Famed Indian spiritual guru Sri Sathya Sai Baba died Sunday of respiratory failure Sri Sathya Sai Baba was hospitalized last month in his southern India hometown of n Puttaparthi. The guru claimed to be a reincarnation of Sai Baba of Shirdi, one of India's greatest spiritual figures, who died in 1918. The orange-robed spiritual leader with a puff of dark hair is considered a living god by his followers in India and overseas. The guru has set up various health, education and charity organizations.
