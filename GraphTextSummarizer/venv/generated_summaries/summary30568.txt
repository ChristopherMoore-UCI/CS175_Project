-- While an art student at Dumlupinar University, Turkish illustrator Murat Palta watched "Star Wars". Cult classics reimagined as Ottoman miniatures "I kept on trying to imagine what 'Star Wars' would look like if it was placed in the Ottoman era, so I decided it would be good to illustrate it," he recalls. "I think next I'll illustrate 'Lord of the Rings' and '2001: A Space Odyssey,' or maybe 'Pan's Labyrinth.'"
