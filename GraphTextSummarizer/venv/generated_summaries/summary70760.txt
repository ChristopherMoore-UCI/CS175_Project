While other Minutemen engaged in a dialogue, Moscoso remembered Forde shouting insults at the protesters. Eventually, Forde's tactics alienated even the most stalwart proponents of border security. Despite "her cloudy reputation" in Minutemen circles, Stonex said, he and Forde got along well.
