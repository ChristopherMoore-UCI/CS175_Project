Hong Kong's leadership has warned pro-democracy legislators against the veto, saying the city might never again receive a chance for political reform. Hong Kong Democratic Party chairwoman Emily Lau told CNN. Hong Kong has become increasingly polarized over the last few years as the city fights over what kind of political system it should have as a Special Administrative Region of China. Hong Kong and Chinese leaders have repeatedly said there is no room for the proposal to change. A controversial Beijing-backed election proposal tabled Wednesday that pro-democracy legislators have already sworn to veto, describing it as "ridiculous.
