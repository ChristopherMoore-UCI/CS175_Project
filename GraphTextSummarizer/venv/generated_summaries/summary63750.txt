Canada -- where on Thursday Obama made his first foreign trip as president -- is one country where opinion may be shifting in America's favor. Then-candidate Barack Obama gave a major speech in Berlin, Germany, in July of 2008. Last year, when Bush was president, only 24 percent of Americans believed world leaders respected the U.S. president, according to a recent Gallup poll. But on Thursday, President Obama got a different reception when he crossed the northern border: adoring throngs of Canadians shouting, "Yes we can!"
