Among famous men who have been treated for prostate cancer are former Secretary of State Colin Powell, former Kansas Sen. Bob Dole, former New York Mayor Rudy Giuliani and actor Robert De Niro. Radio show host Clark Howard says doctors diagnosed him with prostate cancer in its early stages. -- Consumer advocate Clark Howard revealed to listeners of his radio show on Wednesday that he has prostate cancer. If PSA levels are up, the chances of prostate cancer rise, and Howard's levels were such that his doctor gave him PSA tests every 90 days to monitor antigen levels. The fourth and most recent biopsy found a "very, very small isolated pocket of cancer," Howard said.
