-- Five young children were pulled from a car Thursday after it plunged into an icy pond in St. Louis Park, Minnesota. The children -- ages 1 through 7 -- suffered serious injuries, according to the Minnesota State Patrol, which received a report of the car in a retention pond early in the morning.
