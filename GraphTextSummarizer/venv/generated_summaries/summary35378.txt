The technical challenges that Monza presents have never diminished the excitement or quality of the racing there -- not least Moss's first victory in 1956. For all the attraction of Formula One's growing band of new circuits, it's places like Monza that get motor sport enthusiasts pulses racing. "Nowhere else in the championship can you reach top speeds." Liuzzi, who has completed two Monza Grand Prix for Toro Rosso, says the first time you race at Monza can be daunting. Monza hosted its first race in 1922 and has been the spiritual home of Italian motor sport and Ferrari ever since. One of the greatest drives ever seen at Monza wasn't a win but a third place finish by Scotland's Jim Clark in 1967.
