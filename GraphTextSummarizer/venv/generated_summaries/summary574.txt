Dr. Laura Stachel discovered several years ago that many women and babies were dying during childbirth simply because there wasn't adequate light to treat them. At night, Stachel said, the doctor would take the solar suitcase outside and use it to tend to his patients and treat them with intravenous medications. Nominations are open for 2013 CNN Heroes Stachel says she cried when she heard the story, and she hopes her nonprofit can continue to help more people in need. "Every man, woman and child who came to his clinic over the next 30 days were treated day and night," Stachel said.
