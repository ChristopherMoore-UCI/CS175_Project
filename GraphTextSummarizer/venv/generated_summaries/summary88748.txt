Candidates like Amr Mousa, Abu el Fotouh, Ahmed Shafeek and Hamdeen Sabbahi have said "there is no objection to a woman becoming ..." then finishing the sentence by referring to positions such as vice president or some other senior function. Our candidates have also promised women access to senior executive positions. Women are given sugar, rice and other staples so that they choose the generous candidate. Throughout the official 21 days of presidential propaganda, we the public have learned that all the candidates "value" women and believe that women are half of the Egyptian society and therefore should be respected and honored.
