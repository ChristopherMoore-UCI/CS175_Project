She's been first lady of two nations: Before she got married to the South African anti-apartheid icon, Machel was the first lady of Mozambique. Her husband, President Samora Machel, died in a mysterious plane crash in 1986. -- Nelson Mandela once said his wife, Graca Machel, makes him "bloom like a flower." It wasn't love at first sight: After they met, she was reluctant to marry him or discuss their relationship in public. She has something in common with the Kennedys: In Mozambique, she was nicknamed Jackie Kennedy.
