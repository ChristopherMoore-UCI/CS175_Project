Clinton placed a bet on the women's World Cup finals with her Japanese counterpart -- New York apples for Japanese pears. Her daughter Chelsea Clinton was among those who attended Sunday's game in Frankfurt, part of a delegation that included Jill Biden, wife of the U.S. vice president. Clinton alluded to the bet in a phone call she placed to the U.S. team to wish them luck ahead of the game.
