Dr. Christina Pietz, a forensic psychologist, told the judge Tuesday that Loughner is "one of the worst" mentally ill patients she's ever seen. Pietz met Loughner in March 2011 and determined he had schizophrenia and wasn't competent to participate in court proceedings. Pietz recounted Loughner's teenage years, saying that he appeared normal until age 16. Loughner also understands that he will never leave prison and has told Pietz: "I'm 23 years old, this is it. Loughner is worried that he will be harmed by other inmates, but Pietz said she thinks Loughner would be all right in the general prison population.
