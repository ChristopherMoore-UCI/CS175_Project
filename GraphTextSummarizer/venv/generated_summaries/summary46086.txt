On Pi Day, one number 'reeks of mystery' Math may be scary, but pi is not -- as evidenced by the widespread revelry on Pi Day. On Pi Day, is 'pi' under attack? The Museum of Science in Boston has educational Pi Day events, and the Seattle Children's Museum will celebrate too. Lots of restaurants and bakeries are offering Pi Day specials. David Blatner, author of the comprehensive book "The Joy of Pi," is hosting a Pi Day competition in which students from three classrooms will square off to see who can recite the most digits of pi from memory.
