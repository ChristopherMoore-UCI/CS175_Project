After his death, people poured out their heartbreak in letters and telegrams to his widow, Jacqueline Kennedy. "Letters to Jackie: Condolences from a Grieving Nation," a book by historian Ellen Fitzpatrick, highlights hundreds of the 1.5 million consolation letters sent to Jacqueline Kennedy.
