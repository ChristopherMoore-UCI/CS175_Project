Eddie Vedder's second solo album "Ukulele Songs" and a live concert film titled "Water on the Road" will both be released on May 31st, Vedder announced today. As the title suggests, "Ukulele Songs" will feature Vedder performing original songs and covers on his ukulele. The album's first single "Longing to Belong" will be available in digital stores tomorrow.
