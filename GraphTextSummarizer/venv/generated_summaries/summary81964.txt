The Employment Non-Discrimination Act is a simple bill that would outlaw workplace discrimination on the basis of sexual orientation and gender identity, protections that are extended to all Americans on the basis of race, age, religion and other categories. The fact that this law does not already exist for the LGBT community will come as a surprise to many Americans.
