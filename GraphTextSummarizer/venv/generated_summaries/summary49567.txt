Gaga said Tuesday that several shows would be postponed because of an injury she had been hiding. -- Superstar Lady Gaga will have surgery to repair a tear in her hip and has canceled the rest of her "Born This Way Ball" tour, Live Nation announced Wednesday. "I've been hiding a show injury and chronic pain for sometime now, over the past month it has worsened," she tweeted Tuesday afternoon. On Tuesday, the singer described her injury as synovitis, a severe inflammation of joints. Fans can get refunds for the canceled performances at point of purchase starting Thursday, Live Nation said.
