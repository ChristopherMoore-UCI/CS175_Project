Complete coverage on Ferguson shooting and protests The Ferguson police chief has refused to name the officer who shot Michael Brown or to reveal key details about the shooting. Citizens in Ferguson and across Missouri have protested and organized marches and vigils. Ever since an unarmed black teen was shot and killed by Ferguson police five days ago, what's unfolded has been positively un-American.
