-- A gunman shot four people in Greenville, North Carolina, before being shot by police, Mayor Allen Thomas told CNN. Even after he was wounded, the gunman refused to comply with police commands to surrender, and officers used the cover of a vehicle to approach and arrest him, police said. The man, armed with a shotgun, was walking through the Walmart parking lot when he disobeyed police orders to drop the weapon, police said. He fired the shotgun at police officers, who returned fired and shot him, police said. The incident occurred near Landmark Street and Greenville Boulevard, police said.
