Militants staged a deadly attack on Yemen's Defense Ministry on Thursday, ramming the building with an explosives-laden vehicle, followed by gunmen who battled security forces inside. Earlier, Defense Ministry officials had said 10 soldiers and nine militants were among the dead. Yemen is home to al Qaeda in the Arabian Peninsula, believed by many analysts to be the most dangerous affiliate of the terror network. Yemeni lawmaker killed, UN envoy reportedly fired upon in separate attacks The attack targeted a hospital at the Defense Ministry complex, Yemen's state-run Saba news agency reported.
