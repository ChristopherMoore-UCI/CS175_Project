Etihad Airways scooped Best First Class, Cathay Pacific took the Best Business Class prize, Thai Airways got Best Economy Class, while Best In-Flight Entertainment went to Emirates. Multiple prizes were also doled out for down-under flyer Qantas -- honored for Best Domestic Service, Best Catering and Best Lounges, with a special commendation for its safety record. As yet, there are no awards for airlines flying to Middle Earth. Air New Zealand, the carrier that has regularly pressed the diminutive denizens of Middle Earth into service in its advertising campaigns, has been named the world's best airline. AirlineRatings said Air New Zealand had been honored for "its award winning in-flight innovations, record financial performance, operational safety and motivation of its staff."
