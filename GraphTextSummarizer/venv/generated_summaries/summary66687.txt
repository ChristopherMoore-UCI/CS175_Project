The parents of Cayman Naib, 13, have been communicating through the Facebook group "Find Cayman" since a day after his disappearance, according to close friend David Binswanger. Binswanger said weather limited search efforts Wednesday, the night Cayman went missing. Newtown Police say Cayman was last seen wearing a gray down winter jacket, black ski pants and hiking boots. "We think that he got a email from school and was upset by it and left as an impulsive act," Farid Naib, Cayman's father, wrote Thursday on the group page.
