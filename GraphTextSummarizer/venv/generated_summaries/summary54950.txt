Zeta-Jones will play the lead role of Desiree Armfeldt in the production directed by Trevor Nunn, with Lansbury cast as her mother, Madame Armfeldt. The Oscar-winning star of "Chicago" will appear alongside Angela Lansbury in a revival of the Stephen Sondheim musical "A Little Night Music," according to an announcement from the show's producers. " "A Little Night Music," with music and lyrics by Sondheim and book by Hugh Wheeler, debuted on Broadway in 1973. "I'm honored that Trevor Nunn and Stephen Sondheim asked me to make my Broadway debut in this beautiful production," Zeta-Jones said in a statement.
