The gay rights movement also started in the late '60s, but it had the farthest to go. Of course, the fight for equality goes back into the 19th century, even to Founders like Abigail Adams. Capturing the highlights and essence of a decade, especially one as turbulent and historically significant as the '60s, is not easy. I was born on December 15, 1959 -- the cusp of the '60s. And if we want to have an honest discussion about where we are, and where we can and should go, we need to understand the '60s a lot better than we do.
