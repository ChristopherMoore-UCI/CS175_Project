A senior commander of Iran's Revolutionary Guard was killed in an Israeli airstrike in Syria over the weekend, Iran's semiofficial Fars news agency reported. Gen. Mohammed Ali Allahdadi was killed in the same Sunday airstrike that killed six others during a field inspection in Quneitra, a Hezbollah statement said. According to a statement from the Lebanon-based militant group, a group was doing a field inspection in Quneitra when a helicopter opened fire. CNN's Amir Daftari, Hamdi Alkhshali and Kareem Khadder contributed to this report. Jihad Mughniyeh, the son of Imad Mughniyeh, was born in 1989.
