Chelsea released their own statement on Monday, that read: "All at Chelsea Football Club are delighted that John Mikel Obi's father, Michael, has been found safe in Nigeria. Michael Obi went missing from Jos -- the main city in Plateau State in central Nigeria -- on August 12 and had not been seen or heard of since. They quoted Michael explaining his abduction: "I was taken right deep into the bush, in an isolated area in Jos (in central Nigeria). "John Obi Mikel would like to thank everyone in Nigeria, his family & friends, Chelsea FC & their fans & his agents for their total support during this terrible time. The kidnapped father of Chelsea footballer John Obi Mikel has been released alive by his captors in the Nigerian city of Kano.
