When he left office in 1825, James Monroe was deeply in debt. The Clintons wouldn't be the first presidential couple to emerge from the White House in debt. By the end of the previous year, the Clintons carried debt totaling somewhere between $2.28 million to $10.6 million. Former first lady Laura Bush wrote in her post-White House memoir that she was expected to pick up the tab for every meal she ate at the White House or the presidential Camp David retreat -- for her husband's two terms.
