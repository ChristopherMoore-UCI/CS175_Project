The worldwide panic around the Ebola virus and its mostly deadly consequences is starting to inspire tricksters, hucksters and attention hogs to test the limits of the people around them. A public bus driver in Los Angeles was placed in quarantine and his bus removed from service after a masked person yelled "Don't mess with me, I have Ebola!" Officials don't think he has Ebola, but such claims tend to ratchet up the government's response.
