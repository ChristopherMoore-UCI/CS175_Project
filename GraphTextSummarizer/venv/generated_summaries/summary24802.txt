Sand in the City, Vienna Best for: Almost any kind of beach activity you can imagine The Danube River has a number of beaches along its length, but the coolest one is located in the grounds of the Vienna Ice Skating Club: the Sand in the City beach club. For more about Barcelona and Barceloneta beach visit Barcelona Turisme. There are almost three miles of beaches here; the northern end is wild, with winding paths and sand dunes -- people come for picnics and play. There's beach volleyball and giant chess games, a water slide into the lake and a nudist area. The Sport Beach has beach volleyball, the games lit up at night with floodlights.
