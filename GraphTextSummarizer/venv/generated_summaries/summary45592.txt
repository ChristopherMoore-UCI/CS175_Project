"El Hijo del Perro Aguayo was among the most decorated AAA stars in recent memory as a three-time Mexican National Tag Team champion as well as a former Mexican National Light Heavyweight champion and Mexican National Atomicos champion," wrote Bleacher Report columnist  Mike Chiari. The world of Mexican wrestling wrestling has been deeply shaken by the death of star fighter Hijo del Perro Aguayo, who collapsed after being kicked during a bout. "There is no question that El Hijo del Perro Aguayo was among the most popular and successful luchadores in Mexico," Chiari said. The other wrestlers continued with the fight as Aguayo's limp body slid down onto the mat, according to video footage of the bout posted on social media. Aguayo's death prompted an outpouring of grief on social media from fans and members of the wrestling industry.
