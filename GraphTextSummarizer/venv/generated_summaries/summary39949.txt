The app is available on the following carriers in the following countries and will launch on more networks and in more areas soon: Dialog (Sri Lanka) Life (Ukraine) Play (Poland) StarhHub (Singapore) STC (Saudi Arabia) The app will give feature phone users access to Facebook messages, events, pictures, news feeds, profiles and more. Facebook has just announced a new mobile application designed specifically for feature phones. On the Facebook blog, the company's mobile program managerMark Heynen writes, "The Facebook for Feature Phones app works on more than 2,500 devices from Nokia, Sony Ericsson, LG and other manufacturers, and it was built in close cooperation with Snaptu." Users will be able to use the Facebook app without incurring data charges.
