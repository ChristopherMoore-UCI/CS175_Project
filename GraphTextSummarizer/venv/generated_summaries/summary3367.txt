The two cities are part of Scandinavia's Oresund region -- the transnational area made up of southern Sweden and eastern Denmark. Living green  As part of its post-industrial transformation, Malmo has become a leader in environmental design. For the latest in green design, visit the Western Harbor and Augustenborg neighborhoods. Getting around town Most of Malmo's attractions are reachable by foot, but flat terrain and an abundance of cycle paths also make the city ideal to explore by bike. The best way to explore the Oresund region is with an "Around the Sound" ticket, which gives you access to all the trains you need to travel around the area, including the ride across the Oresund Bridge.
