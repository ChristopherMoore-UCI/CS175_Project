"We have some great outfits like an Uncle Sam costume, Apollo Creed-like boxing shorts, a super hero get-up and even '70s disco duds," said Donald Mustard, creative director and co-founder of Chair Entertainment. The game focuses on arcade-style fighting and encourages players to keep on swinging for more votes. Using the same technology that allows thousands of players to connect in "Infinity Blade II's" ClashMobs multiplayer experience, "Vote!!!" keeps a running tally worldwide of who's collecting the most votes. Players are fighting for points, which, in turn, unlock votes. "There's this cool metagame where millions of people around the country will be playing the game and earning votes.
