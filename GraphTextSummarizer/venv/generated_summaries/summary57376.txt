Japan currently administers the area, but since late last year, China has mounted a concerted campaign to try to change the situation. The game was originally developed by China's military as a training and recruitment tool and featured Chinese forces taking on American soldiers. Compared to other countries, China is relatively late in terms of using games as military training and recruitment. But the game drew massive nationwide attention because of one level, which pits Chinese soldiers against a different enemy -- Japan. This level is set on an island chain disputed between China and Japan.
