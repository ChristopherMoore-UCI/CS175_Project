Initiatives like the Elders+Youngers dialogues help increase knowledge for young and older generations, giving youth the impetus to commit to sustainable development in ways our current leadership hasn't even thought of yet. This one had a small article about a video speech contest called Date With History, organized by a non-profit alliance called TckTckTck, asking for two-minute speeches answering the question "If you had two minutes to tell leaders what kind of future you want, what would you say?" I immediately started writing ideas on my hand during school. How can young people inspire action, not only hopeful words, from leaders attending the summit? What shall I tell world leaders gathering at the Rio+20 Summit? What do you think Brittany should I say to say to world leaders?
