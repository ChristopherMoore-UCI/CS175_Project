Wireless carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile and Verizon buy back old phones for store credit. Goodwill and Dell teamed up for the Reconnect program, which accepts donated computers at select Goodwill locations. Other electronic retailers such as Best Buy, Game Stop, Target and Radio Shack offer store credit for phones, tablets, computers, TVs and more. Apple has a Reuse and Recycling program that offers Apple Store credit for your old iPads, iPhones, iPods and computers. Wireless carriers will take old flip phones at no cost.
