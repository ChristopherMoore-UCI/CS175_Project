Only 2% of respondents to a global survey didn't check in with work while on vacation, according to Leslie Perlow, a Harvard Business School professor who conducted a 2009 survey of Harvard Business School executive education alumni. How do you handle work while on vacation? About 57% of Americans who get vacation time don't use all of it, most of them because of their heavy workload, according to a Harris Interactive study for JetBlue. So why is it so hard to put down work and enjoy time off? Those twin concerns are real: Workers heading out on vacation legitimately worry about the work that can pile up while they're away.
