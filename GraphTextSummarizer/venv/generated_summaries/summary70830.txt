Cleary was due at Simpson's house by 5:30 p.m. on Friday, June 6, but never arrived. According to friends and family, Cleary had expressed fear for her life to both her sister Brandy Gerard and her close friend Nicole Simpson. On the morning of June 7, police were called and Cleary was reported missing. When Cleary didn't arrive, Simpson called her family. Cleary, his mother and the children were found June 10 at a motel in Peoria, Illinois.
