The video, broadcast Monday by the Venezuelan Telesur network, is the first to show images of French journalist Romeo Langlois since he was captured last month by the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia, known as FARC. Statement: FARC to release Langlois Langlois, a war journalist with more than 10 years of experience in Colombia, was reporting alongside soldiers when the rebel group opened fire, according to the government. Over the weekend, FARC announced that it would release Langlois on Wednesday. While severely weakened in recent years, FARC continues to carry out kidnappings and attack security forces. Colombian President Juan Manuel Santos has said his government would do whatever was necessary to guarantee Langlois' release.
