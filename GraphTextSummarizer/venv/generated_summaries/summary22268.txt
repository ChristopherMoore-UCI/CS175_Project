-- He's been out of competitive action for most of 2014, but Tiger Woods will return to golf next week. Woods, who underwent back surgery to repair a pinched nerve in March, will play at his own Quicken Loans National event at the Congressional Country Club as he begins his preparations for the third major of the season -- the British Open. "After a lot of therapy I have recovered well and will be supporting my foundation next week at the Quicken Loans National," said Woods on his Facebook page. "I've just started to hit full shots but it's time to take the next step. The 38-year-old's last major victory came at the 2008 U.S. Open.
