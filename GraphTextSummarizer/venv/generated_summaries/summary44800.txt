Being born in that country means: "you don't have a wall separating the physical reality from the magical reality." -- One of the most widely read authors of recent times, Brazilian-born novelist Paulo Coelho talks exclusively to CNN about what Brazil means to him. Coelho was branded a "subversive" by Brazil's then-ruling military government and was arrested and tortured. But as a Brazilian poet said: "Don't lose your hope in difficult times: God was born in Brazil". CNN: How has Brazil changed in your lifetime?
