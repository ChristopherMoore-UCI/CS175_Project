Buddy pictures Traveling for a month in April with a portable, life-sized cutout of her father, New York-based Yang trekked across Europe and posed for pictures in front of famous landmarks, from Skogafoss Waterfall in Iceland to the Leaning Tower of Pisa. Worldwide attention When she returned to New York, Yang posted her collection of photos on her blog (see gallery above). " Travel as therapy Now back in New York, Yang is planning another European tour she hopes to take in July. "I consider myself an artist, and when I set out on this mission, I wanted to honor my father's memory and take beautiful photos to showcase the project," Yang says.
