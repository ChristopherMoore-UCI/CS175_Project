The price per kilogram of boxed tomatoes is less than the price per kilogram for loose tomatoes -- $2.20 per kg for the boxed tomatoes compared to $2.75 per kg for the loose tomatoes. The box of tomatoes is better value for money than the loose tomatoes. Mrs Jones will have extra money to use and will get a lower interest rate.
