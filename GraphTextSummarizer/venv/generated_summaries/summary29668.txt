Richard Branson: War on drugs a trillion-dollar failure Over the past three decades, and in area after area of social life, Americans have replaced simple rules that anybody can follow with complex rules that baffle large numbers of people. When we write social rules, we always need to consider: Who are we writing rules for? At a time when they need more help than ever to climb the ladder, marijuana legalization kicks them back down the ladder. But we need to recognize that modern life is becoming steadily more dangerous for people prone to make bad choices. Marijuana use damages brain development in young people.
