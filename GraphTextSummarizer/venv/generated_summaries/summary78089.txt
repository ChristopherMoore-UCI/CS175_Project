It lets you create a social network geared toward meeting new people around you, sharing photos and videos of what you're up to and supporting other users who are also living it up. Yang also said it's important for users  "to have their own space and little secrets." Texts you send to other users disappear after a few seconds and photos or videos you share can go away nearly as quickly. Sobrr lets users post content "that they might otherwise feel too embarrassed to post on other platforms," he said. Using GPS, Sobrr identifies other users close to you and shows them your posts.
