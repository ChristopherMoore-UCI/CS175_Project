Part of a collapsed ConAgra Foods plant lies atop parked cars Tuesday in Garner, North Carolina. -- Two people were found dead and a third person is still believed missing in a North Carolina food plant heavily damaged in a morning explosion, police said Tuesday night. Four burn victims -- two males and two females -- were undergoing critical care at the North Carolina Jaycees Burn Center, said Dr. Charles Cairns, director of emergency medicine for UNC Hospitals. Police said recovery workers still were trying to get the two bodies out of the plant, which makes Slim Jim food products. The Garner plant is known for producing Slim Jim beef jerky products.
