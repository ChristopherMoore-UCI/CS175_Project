U.S. officials have repeatedly called on North Korea to release Bae but to no avail. Three Americans detained in North Korea spoke out about their conditions and pleaded for U.S. help in interviews with CNN. The Swedish Embassy in Pyongyang acts as the "protecting power for issues involving U.S. citizens in North Korea," and the United States is in "regular, close coordination" with the embassy, she said. Even former basketball star Dennis Rodman, who has visited North Korea and called its ruler Kim Jong Un a "friend for life," asked Kim to "do me a solid and cut Kenneth Bae loose. North Korea claimed Bae was part of a Christian plot to overthrow the regime.
