Camel racing bears no relevance to the modernity embraced here, but this old-fashioned sport is equally as fascinating. The conversation is all about camels; the best breeds, famous old champions, training methods, bad losers and potential winners. And in addition to the honor and prestige, the camels are racing for thousands of dollars in prize money and fancy new cars for their owners. As the only Gulf national in the CNN team, I am expected to be familiar with camel racing, an ancient tradition in the region. We take our place on the floor and slowly, the world of camel racing begins to open up to us.
