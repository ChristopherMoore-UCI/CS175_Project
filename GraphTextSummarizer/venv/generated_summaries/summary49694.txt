Twenty-one-year-old Rihanna was allegedly attacked by her boyfriend, singer Chris Brown, on a Los Angeles street before the two were to perform at the Grammys on February 8. Brown was arrested on February 8 in connection with the case and and booked on suspicion of making criminal threats. Los Angeles police have launched an internal investigation to determine who leaked a picture that appears to show a bruised and battered Rihanna. TMZ said it was a photo of Rihanna.
