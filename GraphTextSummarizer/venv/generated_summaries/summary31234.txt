He knew his habits of binge drinking, smoking two packs of cigarettes a day and overindulging in food were slowly killing him. He stopped eating fast food, fried food and red meat and drinking soda. When he had lost down to 305 pounds, Colao went through a difficult breakup and eased up on his calorie counting and exercising habits. Through his journey, Colao has found support and motivation in his parents and girlfriend, Julia Gonzalez. By 2010, Colao was drinking up to 10 drinks in one night and smoking two packs of cigarettes a day.
