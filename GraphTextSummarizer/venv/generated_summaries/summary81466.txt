The Greek-owned vessel, named the Navios Apollo, was hijacked by Somali pirates in the Gulf of Aden on December 28, the European Union Naval Force Somalia reported. -- A Greek owned vessel that was hijacked months ago was released Sunday, naval officials said. The ship and all the crew members were freed Sunday and they were sailing to Oman, the naval force said. The vessel was carrying 19 crew members. There was no word of whether a ransom was paid to the pirates who hijacked the vessel.
