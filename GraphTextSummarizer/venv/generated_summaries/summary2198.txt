Austerity California is a glimpse of the future for America; the rest of the country, blinded by extravagant election promises, just doesn't yet realize it. Over the past few years, California has made deep cuts in education, social services, and even releasing some prisoners early to save money. California's economy is in better fundamental shape than many eurozone countries. In the past, when California fell on hard times, it could rely on a rising national economy to pick up the slack.
