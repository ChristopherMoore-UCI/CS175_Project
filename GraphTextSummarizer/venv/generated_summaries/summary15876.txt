White button mushrooms found in salad are also high in vitamin B complex, and the antioxidant properties of mushrooms could protect against heart disease and cancer. You might know of only a handful of mushroom types, but there are more than 150,000 species of mushrooms, Stamets says. The research on all these mushrooms is still preliminary, so there's no way to say for sure that mushrooms are going to add years to your life. Besides traveling the world to look for exotic mushrooms, he also lectures about them and runs a mushroom supplement company with his wife called Fungi Perfecti.
