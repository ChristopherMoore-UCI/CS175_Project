For those who want to help Haitian children, Whitbread said, they should donate to aid agencies that are working on reuniting children with their families. -- The images of Haitian children crying or injured or wandering the streets alone are heartbreaking. Haitian children must first be fed, sheltered, clothed and given medical attention; the next step is to register them and trace their relatives. Allowing adoptions to proceed without thorough background checks can lead to child trafficking and other crimes. Adoptions without consent are child trafficking.
