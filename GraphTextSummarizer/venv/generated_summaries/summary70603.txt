The sun is setting, night is coming on quickly, and Wasilewski is on the prowl for snakes -- and one snake in particular. A "Python Patrol" was launched in the Florida Keys, south of the Everglades, by Alison Higgins of the Nature Conservancy. -- Joe Wasilewski drives along a narrow stretch of road through Florida's Everglades. The state has a pilot program with several volunteer snake hunters such as Wasilewski. It is believed that the problem originated when reptile-breeding facilities near the Everglades were destroyed during Hurricane Andrew.
