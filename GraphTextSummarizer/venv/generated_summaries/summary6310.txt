On first glance, the new, never-to-be-actually-named iPad is exactly the same as its predecessor: Same size, same price, same colors. In a demo, Apple reps showed videos loading considerably faster on the new iPad than on the iPad 2. That's good for people who want to watch movies, look at photos and play games on the new iPad. Apple's photo-organization software really comes to life on the high-def iPad, where users will be able to sharpen their photos with new features. Apps Apple announced several new ones, and while these should work on older versions of the iPad, they're designed to take advantage of the new tablet's better screen.
