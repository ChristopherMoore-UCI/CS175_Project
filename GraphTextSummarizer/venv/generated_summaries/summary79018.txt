New Delhi rape exposes perils of being a woman in India The horrific nature of the act was one thing, but would people have reacted in the same way if this had happened in rural India? Daughters, especially in rural India, are often seen as a burden. But what about the tens of thousands of other rape cases left languishing in India's notoriously slow-moving judicial system? The definition of what constitutes rape varies from country to country so there's no internationally accepted method of comparing India's rape statistics with those of other nations.
