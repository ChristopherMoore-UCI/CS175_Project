The Brooklyn Youth Chorus sang "The Nation Anthem" and cellist Yo-Yo performed a Bach selection. -- Whether evoking loss or hope, music performed Sunday at Manhattan's ground zero served as soothing support for family members still grieving 10 years after the 9/11 attacks. Other musical performances included James Taylor's "You Can Close Your Eyes" and flutist Emi Ferguson's rendition of "Amazing Grace." Many did paper rubbings of victims' names or left flowers.
