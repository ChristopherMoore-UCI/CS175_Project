In a private transaction (not related to her company business), her family and the Safra banking family of Brazil just bought a 40% stake in the iconic GM building in New York City -- the building that houses the flagship Apple store on Fifth Avenue. She rose from the faceless assembly line of a Beijing factory to a property magnate richer than Donald Trump and Oprah. Zhang recently sat down with CNN's Pauline Chiou to talk about everything from her Beijing childhood to the volatile property market and how her 14-year old son tried to get a job at McDonald's. In China, there are a lot of problems but also there are a lot of opportunities. Her company, SOHO China, literally changed the landscape of Beijing and Shanghai over the past two decades.
