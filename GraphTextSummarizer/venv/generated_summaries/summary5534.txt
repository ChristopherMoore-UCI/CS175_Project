South Carolina lottery officials said a man showed up at their office Monday with the winning ticket to last Wednesday's $399.4 million Powerball jackpot. South Carolina doesn't divulge the identities of lottery winners to the public if they don't want it to be known. Wednesday's haul was the fourth largest lottery price in U.S. history, the biggest prize ever in South Carolina.
