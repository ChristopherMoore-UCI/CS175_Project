Like most children in Iceland, Stefansson started playing handball at school, but even then he had his own spin on the game. Stefansson is a handball hero -- and handball is the second-most popular sport in Iceland after soccer. Stefansson's international career took him to European and World championships as well as three Olympic Games. After more than 20 years at the top, Stefansson retired in 2013 and is now handing down his knowledge to the next generation of Icelandic handball hopefuls.
