Klug was given a precious second chance of life after a liver transplant and grabbed it with both hands to join Moe in Winter Olympic folklore. Earning a place in the U.S. team for a "home" Winter Games in Salt Lake City, Klug achieved the remarkable feat of winning a bronze medal in the parallel giant slalom event. Step forward Tommy Moe, whose approach may border on the reckless but who seized his moment to win the blue riband event in alpine skiing. Klug also competed on the professional skiing tour where he raced against Moe, who naturally is full of admiration for his feats.
