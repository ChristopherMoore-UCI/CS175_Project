Not small potatoes: Preserving potato varieties in Peru is vital to global food security. "If it reaches South Asia and China then millions could face a major threat to food security," says Dr Bhatti. "Agricultural biodiversity is essential," Dr Shakeel Bhatti, Secretary of the Treaty, told CNN. Different species of plants are often able to cope with widely differing environmental conditions and many obscure varieties could hide vital disease resistance. "The figures are quite disturbing," said Dr Bhatti.
