After all, she managed the response to SARS there in 2003, and MERS is a close genetic cousin. Similarities between the SARS and MERS viruses are more than genetic. Sadly, resources for confronting such outbreaks have decreased since the 2008 financial crisis, and MERS has emerged in one of the most difficult regions in the world. Both viruses spread between people through close contact, putting family caregivers and health care workers at risk for infection.
