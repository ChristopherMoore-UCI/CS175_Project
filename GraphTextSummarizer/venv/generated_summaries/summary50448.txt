-- Two British men were convicted Thursday of torturing and killing two French students in London last June, London's Metropolitan Police said. The victims were biochemistry graduate students on a three-month course at Imperial College, London, studying genetic developments. Gabriel Ferez (left) and Laurent Bonomo were graduate biochemistry students at London's Imperial College. The bodies of Laurent Bonomo and Gabriel Ferez, both 23, were found in Bonomo's burned apartment in southeast London last summer. Hand-held Sony portable games, mobile phones and credit cards were missing, police said.
