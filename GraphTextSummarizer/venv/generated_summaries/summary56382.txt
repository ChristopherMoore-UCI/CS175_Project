Julien Fountain, the former fielding coach for Pakistan's Test cricketers who also played baseball for Great Britain, is recruiting American players to a scheme he calls Switch Hit 20 -- aimed at taking the inherent aptitude and athleticism of ballplayers and training them in the nuances of Twenty20 cricket. Cricket is not a game that looks ready to take off among American sports fans -- but it could be about to provide an unexpected new career for professional athletes in the United States. "Having moved back and forth between the two sports myself, I know the potential these guys have for success in T20 cricket," says Fountain. "These guys have the potential to take the world of T20 cricket by storm," he said.
