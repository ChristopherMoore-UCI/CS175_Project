Typhoon Haiyan: Reports from the field Difficult to assess death toll But Tacloban is far from the only devastated area. But Martin Romualdez, the area's congressman, said authorities need help clearing roads where power lines, trees and whole houses are "literally strewn across the pavement of the highway."
