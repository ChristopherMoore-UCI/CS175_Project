"CATHY COME HOME" The Drama that Transformed the Welfare State Directed by Ken Loach (who later became one of Britain's most respected filmmakers), the drama "Cathy Come Home" was a poignant episode of the BBC-1 anthology series The Wednesday Play. A truly horrifying story, its impact was compounded by the fact that "Cathy Come Home" was filmed in such a realistic style that some viewers thought it was a documentary. Four years later, the newly Republican Reagan was elected governor of California, and the rest is presidential history. Popular television shows have influenced politics in the United States and around the world.
