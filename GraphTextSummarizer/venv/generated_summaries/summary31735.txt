Posts range from the personal to the global, the reality of life on the open waves to plastic pollution, the North Pacific Garbage Patch, and how people could reduce the amount of waste they generate. "I love my life, and want to show people how much fun it can be when you get out there and grab life by the horns. The courage she has found, both to take on the might of the open ocean and give up safe, conventional notions of success to pursue her dreams has made Roz a hero to many people that hope to find inspiration in her life -- including a large number of CNN.com readers who have nominated her as a "Green Inspiration". In one she kept going with life as it was. And if we all pull together, we can make a world of difference." Fundamentally, her message is that life is for living and we learn as much from failure as success if we are open-minded and positive.
