Christie drops swagger amid heat of scandal Bill Clinton Former President Bill Clinton was a veritable maestro of careful and calculated confessions during a career riddled with sexual scandals. Then there were those baffling New York political sex scandals. He recently lost a bid to become New York City comptroller. He resigned from office, but we were treated to Weinergate part deux when, during the 2013 New York City mayor's race, more explicit pictures emerged. Then, after details emerged about a plot in which campaign staffer Andrew Young falsely claimed paternity and campaign funds used to support Edwards' secret family, the candidate finally admitted in 2010 that he was indeed the baby's daddy.
