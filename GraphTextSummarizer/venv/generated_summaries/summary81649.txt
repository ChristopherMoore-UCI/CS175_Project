Exantus, 29, was quickly rushed to a hospital in neighboring Dominican Republic, where he spent over two months recovering. Dominican Republic has always relied heavily on Haitian workers for its agriculture and construction industries. However, Haitians and Dominicans have had tense relations dating back as far as the mid-19th century, when Haiti occupied Dominican Republic for more than 20 years. Haiti and Dominican Republic are united by geography, sharing the island of Hispaniola, both former sugar colonies under France and Spain respectively. Fernandez analyzes refugee and immigration patterns for the Jesuit Refugee Services in Dajabon, Dominican Republic, on the border with Haiti.
