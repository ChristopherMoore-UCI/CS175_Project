Robertson said the company hopes to eventually expand Lockitron into UK locks and beyond. "The timing was right for this sort of product because people have become so used to their smartphones and using apps, that it was a natural extension they should have an app for the key as well," Robertson says. "A lot of people see the hardware and think that's what we've done but [Lockitron] is the combination of hardware and app and software," Robertson added.
