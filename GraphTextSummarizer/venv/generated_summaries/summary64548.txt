Unexpected benefits of giving your relationship a cleanup No wonder Al and Tipper Gore are going their separate ways after 40 years; they only have about five minutes of conversation a day! A recent British study found that the longer couples are married, the less they have to say to each other over the course of an hour-long meal. Immediately after marriage, the downward trend kicks in, with the time spent talking dropping to 40 minutes per hour. Twenty years into marriage, the average couple talks for 21 minutes of the hour; 30 years in, conversation takes up 16 minutes.
