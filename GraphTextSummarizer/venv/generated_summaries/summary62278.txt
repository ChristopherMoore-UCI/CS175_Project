He was running for re-election against golden boy Pete Dawkins -- Heisman Trophy winner at West Point, Rhodes scholar, Vietnam vet, Army general, millionaire banker -- a man so handsome, he was a model in ads for men's shirts. "I can get people to criticize me for free!" Lautenberg: The last 'swamp dog' Frank was so cantankerous, so combative, that he often called his 1988 campaign manager, the great James Carville, "the second opponent." In 1988 I served as Frank's press secretary. From that day to the last time I saw him, I would always ask Frank how the old swamp dog was doing. Frank Lautenberg grew up in Paterson, New Jersey -- a tough mill town.
