Calle and Berlin formed "SEC Excursions" a travel company that provides busing, tailgate parties and hotel accommodations to college football games. "Let our customers write our business plan." Calle, a senior at the University of Georgia who is pursuing a degree in business administration, launched SEC Excursions with a mix of investors and help from the Terry College of Business Entrepreneurship Program at UGA. In addition to their course work, studying for midterms and balancing extracurricular activities, they wrote business proposals and figured out financing. DreamIt is a "business incubator" that helps entrepreneurs launch companies. The company has contracts with bus companies and various hotel chains.
