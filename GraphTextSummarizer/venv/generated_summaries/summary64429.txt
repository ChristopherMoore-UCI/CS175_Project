Mark Andrus was booked for murder, San Francisco Police Department spokeswoman Grace Gatpandan said. A 59-year-old man was arrested early Saturday in connection with the grisly discovery of body parts in a suitcase left on a San Francisco sidewalk. Gatpandan said the investigation is ongoing and encouraged people to continue to call the tip line. The dismembered body was discovered Wednesday when police where called to the South of Market district in San Francisco. Police have not disclosed what body parts were in the suitcase and trash can.
