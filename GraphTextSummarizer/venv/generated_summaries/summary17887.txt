Here is a sampling of some of her most powerful speeches: 1) Presidential Inauguration Ceremony, 1993 She became the second poet in history to speak at a presidential inauguration -- after Robert Frost, who spoke at John F. Kennedy's inaugural -- when she read her poem "On the Pulse of Morning" at the swearing-in of President Bill Clinton in 1993. The funeral of Coretta Scott King, 2008 The writer sang and spoke at the homegoing service for the widow of the Rev. Martin Luther King -- calling her "born of flesh and destined to become iron." 2) 2013 National Book Awards Angelou was selected to receive the Literarian Award for Outstanding Service to the American Literary Community. The Children's Defense Fund National Conference, 2012 Angelou shared one of her poems as the keynote speaker at the Children's Defense Fund National Conference held in Cincinnati in July 2012. -- More than anything, Maya Angelou was known for her words.
