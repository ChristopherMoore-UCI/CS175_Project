" Business beginnings Before he could start shining shoes, Mgayiya pawned his fridge to buy business equipment. Big plans Success in Cape Town didn't cool Mgayiya's ambition, and after a year he got a chance to pitch to the person in charge of all South African airports. If someone has shined your shoes in a South African airport, it's probably thanks to Lere Mgayiya. Today, Mgayiya has scaled back to the three major ones: Cape Town, Durban and Johannesburg. Failed attempts His mother's brother took away his job, but Mgayiya kept in contact with his farmer clients and soon embarked on his next project: selling the farmers' eggs to the kitchen of the South African parliament.
