If voter ID suppressed votes, we should see a drop in turnout, right? So, in terms of raw votes, turnout in 2013 increased by about 63% over turnout in 2011 in comparable elections. Democrats who oppose voter ID have consistently claimed that it suppresses votes. If voter ID was intended to suppress votes, it is failing as spectacularly as HealthCare.gov.
