BEIJING, China  -- As Wimbledon 2009 gets under way this week, sports commentators and tennis aficionados will closely watch China's tennis superstar, Zheng Jie. China's Zheng Jie during her semifinal tennis match  against Serena Williams at Wimbledon "Ana is the world number one player, I won over her and that gave me a so much confidence," she told CNN, recalling one of the biggest upsets in Wimbledon history.
