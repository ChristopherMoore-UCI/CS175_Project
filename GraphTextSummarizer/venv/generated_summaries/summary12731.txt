Jonathan Gould, an American University justice, law and society professor, said it's almost certain the White House approved Holder's recent remarks on race though Obama still had some deniability about them. "Attorney General Eric Holder is speaking for the president, but there's enough distance for Obama to say it's not the view of the president." Early in the Obama administration, Holder said that we are "a nation of cowards" when it comes to issues of race. Provocative, or not, Holder has attracted little public criticism for his language from the one person who matters most -- his boss, President Barack Obama. -- There is one thing to be said about Attorney General Eric Holder.
