Snowfall will begin in the Midwest on Friday and reach the Northeast by Saturday morning. New York City issued a hazardous travel advisory for Saturday and urged all motorists to carry contact information for a towing company in the vehicle. Snow amounts could top 10 inches in northern Pennsylvania and parts of upstate New York and New England. The National Weather Service predicts 1 to 3 inches of snow in Philadelphia, site of the annual Army-Navy football game on Saturday. Residents could see up to 8 inches of snow in Cleveland, 1 to 3 inches in Pittsburgh, 6 to 8 inches in Boston and 4 to 6 inches in New York.
