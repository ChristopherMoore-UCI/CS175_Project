Birdseed sellers now refer to wild birds as "outdoor pets," helping to condition people to think that putting out food for wild animals is an act of kindness. Conflicts between people and wild animals will continue to rise as both populations grow into one another. More bears and more denatured people living in the same habitat. Black bears are shy and docile creatures motivated by hunger and fear, but they, like deer, beavers, turkeys, waterfowl and others, were almost wiped out in the United States by the end of the 19th century. We've become forest people -- yet we spend 90% of our time indoors.
