England's Olympic Triple Jump medallist in the Triple Jump announced on Twitter that he decided not to travel to the Games because of safety concerns. While some countries like Australia have hired private security companies and will be guarded by Australian federal police, an estimated 100,000 police and security personnel will be protecting the 17 competition venues as well as other Games-related sites. The need to rest and recover after a long season has been the reason other medal favorites have given for missing the Games. As well as athletics and other Olympic events, squash, lawn bowls and rugby sevens make up the sports at the Games.
