Keywords Constitution, executive branch, legislative branch, judicial branch, amendments, Supreme Court, Bill of Rights, current events, news stories Ask students: Why do you think that the framers of the Constitution organized the U.S. system of government this way? Federal powers listed in the Constitution include the rights to collect taxes, declare war and regulate trade. Remind students that in 1787, the framers of the U.S. Constitution established a federal system of government that divided power between the national and state governments. Procedure As a class, read out loud the Preamble to the Constitution.
