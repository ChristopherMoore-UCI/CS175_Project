Malmo throws a vibrant festival, staging parades, live music, film screenings and drag shows around the city. BUFF Film Festival (March)  There are about a dozen film festivals held annually in Sweden, the birthplace of master filmmaker Ingmar Bergman. Hundreds of live acts fill the city with sounds of classical music, heavy metal and everything in between. Oktoberfest (September-October) Beer flows endlessly and the beat goes on at nightclub Slagthuset during Oktoberfest. If you aren't busy dancing the night away, you'll want to take in the film screenings, comedy shows and theater performances staged during the festival.
