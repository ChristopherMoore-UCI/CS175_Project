Coopers Beach, the main beach in the village of Southampton on the east end of Long Island, New York, took top honors this year on the 20th annual list released by Dr. Stephen P. Leatherman, director of the Laboratory for Coastal Research at Florida International University. Sarasota, Florida's, Siesta Beach took the second spot on the list, followed by Coronado Beach in San Diego, California. A sandy stretch in the tony Hamptons topped 2010's best beaches list, compiled each year by a coastal scholar known as Dr. Beach. Siesta Beach in Sarasota, Florida 3.
