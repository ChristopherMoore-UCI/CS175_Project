Myanmar is still working on a real credit system and ATM machines are a relatively new development -- if they work. The banking system we take for granted in most countries -- easy loans, financial products, interbank operations, credit -- they're all virtually non existent here. It's a small step, but for Myanmar a small step in the right direction is critical as it gets ready to be part of a global integrated financial system.
