-- Police have detained a man accused of having killed at least three kindergarten students Tuesday in east China's Zibo City, Shandong Province, the city government said Wednesday, according to the state-run Xinhua News Agency. The attack is one of at least five knife or cleaver attacks on schoolchildren in China since March. Fang Jiantang, 26, was detained hours after the attack and has told police he was responsible, said the government in a statement. Two of the teachers were in serious condition, it said. Police found the two-foot (60-centimeter) knife that Fang said he used, the statement said.
