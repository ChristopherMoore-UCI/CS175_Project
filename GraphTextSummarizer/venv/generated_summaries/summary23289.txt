Out and proud, China's gay activists are an increasingly vocal minority pushing for change that could very well reach every corner of Chinese society. Many Chinese gays and lesbians are responding to the family pressure with "cooperative marriages" -- gay men and lesbian women marrying each other out of social and economic convenience, often finding each other online. According to a 2013 survey by U.S. research group Pew, only 21% of China's population was in favor of the acceptance of homosexuality. In the last two decades, China's LGBT community has made huge gains in social acceptance.
