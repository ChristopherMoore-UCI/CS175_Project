-- A prison brawl and fire left 29 inmates dead and dozens injured in eastern Bolivia, state media reported Friday. One group attacked the other with sharp weapons and firearms, and set two liquid gas cylinders on fire, sparking an explosion. State media reported that the fire started early Friday with a confrontation between two groups of inmates. Bolivia allows children to legally live with incarcerated parents. At least 100 additional troops were dispatched to reinforce security inside the prison, according to state media.
