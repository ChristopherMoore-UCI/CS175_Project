In addition to four 23-foot-long whale sharks, Winkler gazed on a manta ray, hammerhead sharks, goliath grouper and sawfish. Scott Winkler had many scary encounters while serving in Iraq, but they were nothing compared with his recent experience at the world's largest aquarium: swimming alongside a massive whale shark. He rolled his wheelchair down a long ramp to a dock floating in the 6.3 million-gallon tank of salt water. Winkler was paralyzed five years ago during an accident while unloading ammunition in Tikrit, Iraq. Scott Winkler, 35, was paralyzed five years ago during an accident in Iraq.
