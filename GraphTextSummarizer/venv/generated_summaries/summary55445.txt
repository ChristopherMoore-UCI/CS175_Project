Keywords Veterans Day, sacrifice, freedom, war, U.S. armed forces, interview Students will interview U.S. military veterans to learn about the sacrifices that these men and women have made in the defense of our country. Power, Authority and Governance: Students will understand the historical development of structures of power, authority and governance and their evolving functions in contemporary U.S. society as well as other parts of the world. Encourage students to locate veterans by asking their friends and family members, or by contacting a local Veterans Center. Have students deliver their presentations to the class and invite local veterans to attend the event.
