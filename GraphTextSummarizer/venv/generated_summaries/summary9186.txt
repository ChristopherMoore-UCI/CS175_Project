He's happy and full of life, a character and the light of our lives. Our son Jonah Weishaar was diagnosed with Sanfilippo Syndrome Type C in May 2010 when he was 22 months old. There are four different enzymes responsible for breaking down heparan sulfate: They are designated A, B, C and D. Jonah has Sanfilippo C, which along with D is ultra-rare and occurs in one of 1.4 million births. Very few federal dollars are allocated for funding rare disease research, and most go toward the diseases with larger patient populations. Most children succumb to organ failure or upper respiratory infections.
