Awards are calculated using Cruise Critic member ratings. Disney Cruise Line dominated this year's awards, winning in 12 categories. "Cruise Critic's community of cruisers is the largest in the world, so to be named to this list is truly an honor," said Carolyn Spencer Brown, editor-in-chief of Cruise Critic. Other Disney awards include Disney Dream's wins for "best entertainment" and "best cruises to the Bahamas" and Disney Fantasy's wins for "best cabins," "best service" and "best for families."
