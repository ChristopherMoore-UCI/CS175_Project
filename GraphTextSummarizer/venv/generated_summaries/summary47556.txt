An important component of CBT is daily homework, part of which involves patients charting their daily moods, energy levels, sleep patterns, activities, and thoughts. App name: Mobile Therapy   Developed by: Dr. Margaret Morris, a clinical psychologist working for Intel   How it works: You can map your moods, energy levels, sleep patterns, activities, and food intake on a daily basis. Some therapy apps are being designed to help patients track moods or even predict psychotic breaks. (The Frisky)  -- Mobile therapy apps are on the way. App name: CBT MobilWork Developed by: Judy Callan at the University of Pittsburgh and scientists at Carnegie Mellon How it works: This app lets patients check off CBT homework as it's completed and prompts and coaches the patient on to the next step.
