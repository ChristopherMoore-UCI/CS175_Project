This obituary includes biographical information from Vogue magazine, biography.com, Current Biography, Contemporary Fashion and Dictionary of Hispanic Biography. Legendary fashion designer Oscar de la Renta, who spent half a century putting high society in haute couture, has died. Atop Admired by contemporaries, adored by consumers, de la Renta had a long, successful run. Emboldened, de la Renta switched his focus to fashion. He finessed his style, learned to tailor, and, two years later, moved to New York -- where he became a designer for Elizabeth Arden.
