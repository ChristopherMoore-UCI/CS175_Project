More than 24 militants opened fire at two buses, forcing them to stop in Bolan district of Balochistan, police official Zubair Ahmed said. Elsewhere in Balochistan province, armed militants dressed in police uniforms attacked two oil tankers Monday night. The tankers were carrying fuel for local security forces in the Mach area of Bolan district.
