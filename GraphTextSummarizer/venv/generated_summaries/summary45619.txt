The band's massive hooks have been boosted by Michael Stipe of R.E.M., who helped frontman Gary Lightbody overcome writer's block, and moments like the gospel-choir chorus of ''This Isn't Everything You Are'' are genuinely affecting. With its sweeping guitar-rock symphonies and pounding electronic beats, "Empires" could've been called "Fourteen Songs About Fourteen Different Types of Melancholy." True, they just bowed their new single, ''New York,'' on "Grey's." But it's been six years and two albums since their anthemic ''Chasing Cars'' appeared on the show and helped 2006's "Eyes Open" go platinum.
