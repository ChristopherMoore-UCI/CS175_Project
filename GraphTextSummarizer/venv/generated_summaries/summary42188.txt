His constant fear upon waking up every morning probably was: "Is this my last day on Earth?" Most American soldiers captured in the wars in Iraq and Afghanistan were executed within days or weeks of captivity. Survivors of the Hanoi Hilton, POWs during Vietnam such as Sen. John McCain and Medal of Honor winner Adm. James Stockdale, endured the rigors of long days, months and years of surviving captivity. Like all soldiers, Bergdahl made a solemn vow to defend his country and fellow soldiers -- to serve with honor. Did he indeed walk off his combat outpost in Afghanistan on June 30, 2009, as many soldiers say he did? He was not held by a nation-state recognized by the United Nations, nor did he have fellow prisoners to commiserate with or rely upon for comfort, aid and solace.
