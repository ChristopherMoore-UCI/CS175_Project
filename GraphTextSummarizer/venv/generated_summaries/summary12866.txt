The rape case that changed India Several high-profile cases have drawn attention to sexual violence against women in India. The men were accused of raping and robbing the American woman, who had sought a lift from them in their small truck, Vinod Kumar Dhawan, the state's police superintendent, told CNN. All Nepali men in their 20s, they were convicted of sexual assault and robbery, police said. Officially, they were sentenced to 20 years each for sexual assault and five years for robbery, but the sentences will run concurrently. A 17-year-old boy was also sentenced to three years in prison for murder, rape and robbery in the attack.
