However, just 15% of this year's guests are female The meeting Only once was the annual meeting held outside of Davos: In 2002, in New York, as a gesture of solidarity after the 9/11 terrorist attacks. The attendees 250 top political leaders and heads of international organizations attend the forum, this year, 40+ of them are current heads of state or government. Moreover, the annual meeting contributes CHF 45 million ($50M) to the local Davos economy and CHF 70 million ($77M) to Switzerland's economy overall. There are four World Economic Forum bureaus around the world: The headquarters in Geneva, and in New York, Beijing, and Tokyo.
