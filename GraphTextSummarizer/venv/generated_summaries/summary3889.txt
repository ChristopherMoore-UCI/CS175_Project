The Libyan government has notified the United States that an employee at the U.S. Consulate in Benghazi, Libya, was killed Tuesday, a State Department official told CNN. Earlier Tuesday, a group of militants attacked the consulate, State Department spokeswoman Victoria Nuland said. Members of a radical Islamist group had been protesting at the consulate, a freelance journalist working for CNN in Benghazi said.
