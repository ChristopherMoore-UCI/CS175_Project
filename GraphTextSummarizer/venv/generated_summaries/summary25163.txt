Yemen in midst of an upheaval Houthis follow the Zaidi sect of Islam and are considered Shiites by al Qaeda, a Sunni extremist group. One month after signing the ceasefire deal, Houthis took control of large areas of the Sunni-dominated Ibb province, following weeks of clashes with militants. The injured were taken to Al Thawra and Al Manar hospitals. A huge explosion killed at least 23 people and wounded 48 others during a Houthi gathering in the central Yemeni province of Ibb on Wednesday, Yemen's Interior Ministry said. There has been a series of recent attacks on Houthi targets across the country.
