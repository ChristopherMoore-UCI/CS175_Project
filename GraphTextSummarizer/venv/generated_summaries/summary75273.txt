"One has to be a little more worried about a return to military dictatorship," said political analyst Sarah Eltantawi. Rights groups say talk of an El-Sisi run shouldn't be cause for celebration, but reasons to worry. For now, like it or not, the military seems to be playing a central role in post-revolution Egypt where establishing democracy is still the stated goal. It was accused of beating and killing protesters who took to the streets to demand then-President Hosni Mubarak's ouster. It was El-Sisi who announced Morsy's ouster, sparking wild celebrations.
