In Group D, Belarus recorded a 2-0 win over Luxembourg thanks to goals from Sergei Kornilenko and Anton Putilo to put them one point behind the leaders France. Azerbaijan managed to pull a goal back in the final minute of the match through Murad Huseynov but Schurrle put the game beyond doubt in injury time. Hungary moved within three points of Sweden after they beat San Marino 3-0, Zoltan Liptak, Imre Szabics and Vladimir Koman finding the net. Goals from Mesut Ozil, Mario Gomez and Andre Schurrle helped Joachim Loew's side to a 3-1 triumph in Baku as they moved 11 points clear in qualifying Group A. In the same group, Haris Medunjanin and Darko Maletic were on target for Bosnia-Hercegovina as they beat Albania 2-0 to move into third, two points off Belarus and three points behind France.
