Queen Elizabeth II, William's grandmother, remarked on her trip to Canada last year that, for the royals, the country is a "home away from home. -- Britain's Prince William and his wife, Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, arrived Thursday in Canada on their first official foreign trip since their wedding in April. Prince William has said it is a "great privilege" for him and Catherine to have been invited to Canada for their first trip as a married couple. However, as many as 300 anti-monarchy protesters are expected to demonstrate Sunday at Quebec City Hall, Canadian media reports say. Prince William, who was famously mobbed by screaming girls when he visited Canada as a teenager in 1998, has been keen to return to the country for many years, his private secretary said ahead of the trip.
