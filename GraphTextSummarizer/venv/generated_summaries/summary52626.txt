How you can help a Veteran any day California-based  Operation Gratitude sponsors programs honoring veterans year-round with care packages that include handwritten letters. The group sends care packages to VA hospitals, veterans nursing homes, veterans groups and other support organizations. -- "What day is Veterans Day?" It was a simple question my colleague asked me a few weeks ago. When I was growing up, and even now, every day is Veterans Day. For him, every day was Veterans Day, because he lived his time at war in some way every day.
