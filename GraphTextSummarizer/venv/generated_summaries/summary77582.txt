-- A suicide car bomber killed at least 56 people and wounded more than 100 others Friday when his vehicle exploded in a market in Mohmand Agency in Pakistan's tribal region, officials said. In the confusion following the attack, various officials provided conflicting death tolls. Authorities also believe more victims may be trapped under the rubble of damaged buildings. Maqsood Mahed Khan, a local government official, said three children and two women were killed. But the vehicle detonated in a market near the building, said Hayat Khan, a local administration official.
