And Sunday night, she won a Grammy for the Best Engineered Album, Classical category. Brandie Lane is returning to West Point clutching the golden hardware. Until she joined the U.S. Army last summer, Lane worked as head audio engineer at Sono Luminus, a record label in Winchester, Virginia, that specializes in classical and jazz music.
