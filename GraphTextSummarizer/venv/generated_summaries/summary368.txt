Hagins envisions a future where anyone can buy smart home apps just like they buy mobile phone apps. When Apple announced its vision of hooking up smart home devices with Bluetooth and Wifi in June, it also said it would not make its own smart home products. But with all the magic of connected homes comes a great concern about potential data misuse. In the case of SmartThings, people have to specifically opt-in to share their data with outside companies. But the idea of smart homes goes beyond showing off.
