Yes, we talk about "deaths" -- heinous deaths, tragic deaths, celebrity deaths, untimely deaths. Now, at last, death in America is starting to come out of its cold, hard shell -- in unexpected ways. For something that surrounds us so completely, bookending our lives, modern Americans can be surprisingly shy about death. But when it comes to death itself, many of us would rather use the broom of wishful thinking to sweep it away, leaving death to sit in some dark corner of our minds.
