-- Her Colorado home destroyed by a natural disaster, Trudy Dawson stops to think about fire and water. Beyond reflecting on the destructive powers of water and fire, however, Dawson is unwavering on her next step. "I sit back and reflect on the difference between flood and fire," Dawson said. Comparing what she witnessed at her parents' home in New Orleans when it was flooded by Katrina and the fire that engulfed her own home, Dawson knows which is worse. Thinking back to Katrina, Dawson recalls the look in her parents' eyes when they returned to their flooded home to see their losses.
