"It is with deep sadness that we inform you that after a brief illness John Malcolm Fraser died peacefully in the early hours of this morning," according to a Fraser family statement sent to Australian media. Fraser was Australia's 22nd prime minister, from 1975-1983. Fraser will be remembered for his significant influence in changing relations of countries within the British Commonwealth, according to the National Archives of Australia. Former Australian Prime Minister Malcolm Fraser has died. The office of current Prime Minister Tony Abbott confirmed Fraser's death to CNN.
