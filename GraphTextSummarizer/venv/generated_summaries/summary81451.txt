New Jersey voters were both more Democratic and more liberal than Virginia voters. Almost one-third of New Jersey voters cited the economy as their top concern in early exit polls, with another 25 percent citing the state's high property taxes. Independent voters broke even more heavily for Christie in New Jersey than for McDonnell in Virginia. Last year, in contrast, Obama edged out GOP presidential nominee John McCain by one point among Virginia independents. Democrat Bill Owens has won a vacant U.S. House seat in upstate New York, CNN projects.
