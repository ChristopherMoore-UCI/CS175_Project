"What we do is come in after natural disasters all around the world and help people by bringing in volunteers in the recovery process," said Marc Young, the charity's operational director in Ofunato, a small port city on Japan's northeastern coast. There are few baths or working washing machines in the area, and what people really need is fresh food, he said.
