Instead of eating a sandwich or two for lunch, she has soup and tuna salad or cottage cheese. Because she lacks discipline, she buys individual cups of cottage cheese instead of a big container to prevent herself from eating twice as much as she should. A former dance teacher, Schulman never thought about what she was eating because she burned so many calories with aerobics, jazz, clogging, tap and ballet. Now she takes a Zumba class twice a week and walks several miles to and from her synagogue to attended religious services on Saturday. One year after trying to take off five pounds to fit into that size 20 dress, Schulman was wearing a size 8.
