Figures from the National Bureau of Statistics show that visitor arrivals in Seychelles are up by over 90% compared to last year. Decline in visitors As the global financial crisis of 2008 stopped many people from taking holidays, the Seychelles was hit hard. Air Seychelles recorded a loss of $12.5 million in 2010/11 and the company made massive cuts. Indeed, the World Bank says Seychelles' economy is "highly dependent" on tourism. The research group says visitors to Sri Lanka are from the "same demographic as those who travel to the Seychelles" and the substitution has had an impact.
