Opened last year in Vietnam's central coast city of Da Nang, the Rong Cao (Dragon Bridge) is an award-winning, U.S.-designed marvel of engineering that's become a favorite of residents and visitors, and an icon of the prosperous future this once sleepy town has its eye set on. As the de facto capital of Vietnam's central coast and one of the country's rising economic success stories, Da Nang is spending big on infrastructure to bring in tourism dollars.
