This edition will be the first official collection of the Witmark Demos, 47 songs that Dylan recorded between 1962 and 1964 for his first two publishers, Leeds Music and M. Witmark & Sons. The set -- which spans 1962's Bob Dylan through 1968's "John Wesley Harding" -- will come as a deluxe box and on 180-gram vinyl, and include a booklet of vintage photographs and an essay by Rolling Stone. Also on October 19th, Columbia/Legacy will also release "Bob Dylan" -- "The Original Mono Recordings," which feature Dylan's first eight LPs newly mastered from their first-generation mono mixes.
