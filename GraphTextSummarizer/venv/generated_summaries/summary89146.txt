-- Basketball commentator and former hoops star Charles Barkley was arrested Wednesday in Scottsdale, Arizona, on suspicion of drunken driving, a police spokesman said. Charles Barkley was driving an Infiniti SUV through a trendy area of Scottsdale on Wednesday, police say. "Mr. Barkley was administered the standard field sobriety tests after the odor of intoxicating liquor was detected." Barkley's sport-utility vehicle was impounded under mandatory vehicle impound laws, police said. "The officer identified the driver of the 2005 Infiniti as Charles Barkley," according to a written statement from police.
