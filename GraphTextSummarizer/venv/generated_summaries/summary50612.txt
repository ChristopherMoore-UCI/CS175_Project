The Steamboat Natchez offers live jazz on all of its Mississippi River outings, which include dinner cruises ($70 for adults), evening jazz cruises ($42 for adults), lunch cruises ($37 for adults) and two-hour daytime cruises ($26 for adults). Preservation Hall is a Crescent City institution, established in 1961 to preserve New Orleans jazz as rock and modern jazz grew in popularity.
