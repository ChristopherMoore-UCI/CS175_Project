Iron Curtain While his Hall of Fame biography states that Ramone was born Tom Erdelyi in Budapest, Hungary, he was actually born in that city as Erdelyi Tamas, on January 29, 1949, and his name was changed to Tom Erdelyi after he emigrated with his family to the United States in 1957, according to Andy Schwartz, a representative of Ramone's longtime domestic partner, Claudia Tienan. Joey Ramone, born Jeffrey Hyman, died of cancer in 2001 at age 49. Johnny Ramone, born John Cummings, died of cancer in 2004 at age 52. Ramone also stopped composing for other artists during this period, Schwartz said. The Hall of Fame credits the band with saving rock music "from one of its lowest ebbs.
