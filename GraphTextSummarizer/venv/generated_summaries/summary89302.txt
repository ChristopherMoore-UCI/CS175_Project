But it doesn't matter whether its brown water off the British coast or sharks lurking beneath sunnier climes, nothing puts Whiteley off taking her kite into the deep unknown. At just 15, Whiteley became the youngest ever female to win the British Amateur Ladies title, repeating the feat the following year at the professional championship. Two more title wins followed in consecutive years as did the prestigious Big Air/Board off crown at the 2009 Pro Kite World Tour competition. "My dad was teaching me and I was going along fine, then all of a sudden then I was out of control and went flying through the sky," Whiteley told CNN's Human to Hero series. Reaching the top That chastening experience powered Whiteley through months of training, hitting the waves at every opportunity despite the infamous British weather.
