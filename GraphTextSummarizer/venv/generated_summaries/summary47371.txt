Booher escaped on July 12 from Indiana State Prison in Michigan City, Indiana, along with Lance Battreal, a rapist, and Charles Smith, also a murderer. -- The last of three prisoners who escaped from Indiana State Prison earlier this month has been captured, authorities said Thursday. Lance Battreal, from left, Mark Booher and Charles Smith escaped from an Indiana prison July 12. The U.S. Marshals Service captured Mark Booher, a  murderer, around 11 a.m., said Shannon Robinson, supervisor for a marshals service fugitive task force. Two of the escaped inmates had worked in the tunnels as assistants to maintenance employees, said John Schrader, spokesman for the Indiana Department of Correction.
