And when Sandomir asked Adam Silver, deputy commissioner of the NBA, about the league's position on instituting jerseys with ads, Silver said: "If we add sponsor logos to jerseys, we recognize that some of our fans will think we've lost our minds. Should the NBA decide to go forward with ads on jerseys, the players will probably put up a fight until they get their desired percentage of the income from the sponsors, but that will be negotiated.
