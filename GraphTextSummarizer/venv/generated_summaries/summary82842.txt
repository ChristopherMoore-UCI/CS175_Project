Police seized cash, canaries, finches and bird cages from a home in Shelton, Connecticut. Shelton Police say they've never seen or heard of this kind of bird fighting before. Police seized approximately 150 birds and arrested 19 people in a Connecticut home Sunday in an investigation of alleged finch and canary fighting, the Shelton Police Department said. Saffron finches are canary-size birds native to South America.
