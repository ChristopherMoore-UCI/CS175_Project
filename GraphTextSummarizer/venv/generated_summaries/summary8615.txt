Emergency responders from nearby Hill Air Force Base were searching for the pilot. -- A search was under way Tuesday for the pilot of an F-16 that crashed over the Utah Test and Training Range west of Salt Lake City, Utah, the Air Force said. The F-16 was assigned to the 388th Fighter Wing at Hill Air Force Base and was on a routine training mission when it crashed, the posting said. The F-16 crashed about 10:25 p.m. Monday, according to a posting on the Air Force's Web site.
