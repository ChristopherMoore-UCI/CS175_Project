-- When he watches personal trainers work with their clients at the gym he uses, Marqus Taylor often isn't impressed. Patience Patience is the key to a good client-trainer relationship, says celebrity trainer Ashley Borden. Trainers should also find a comfortable pace for their clients, Borden says. Education Trainers should have -- and be able to show you -- an appropriate fitness certification for their area of expertise, Borden says. "From what I've seen, personal trainers tend to get in your face and make you feel less than you are," he says.
