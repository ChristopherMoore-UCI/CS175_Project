"I had grandchildren and great-grandchildren who shunned school," she says by phone from Ndalat. Now in her 90s, Priscilla Sitienei is changing that, attending elementary school -- and inspiring a generation. If confirmed by the Guinness World Records, she would be the oldest pupil in elementary school. The last such honor by the Guinness World Records went to another Kenyan, Kimani Maruge, who in 2004 was named the oldest person to begin primary school.
