Betty White fast facts The year Betty White was born, 1922, Reader's Digest was launched, as were the first public radio broadcasts in France and Britain. White has worked in show business since the 1940s, when she appeared on popular radio programs "Rin-Tin-Tin," and "The Great Gildersleeve." For White -- who saw a career resurgence in the 2000s with guest starring roles on TV and a gig hosting "SNL" following a grass-roots campaign -- staying busy is probably the best birthday present ever. In October 2013, Lifetime picked up her canceled NBC show "Betty White's Off Their Rockers."
