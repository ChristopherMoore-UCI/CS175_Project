Kadhim shares ISIS survival tale with New York Times Kadhim, 23, is the rare witness. ISIS claimed it killed 1,700 Shiite soldiers that day, making the killings the deadliest sectarian atrocity in Iraq's recent history, according to the Times. Pretending to be dead among a pile of bodies, Kadhim told the Times that he waited for hours until he was able to escape.
