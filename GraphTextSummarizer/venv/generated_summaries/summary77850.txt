After receiving a call at about 1:30 p.m., police rushed to the building, which houses several businesses. "Shortly thereafter, gunshots are shown penetrating a wall inside of the business," police said in a statement. The unidentified owner of A K Home Health Care is believed to be the gunman, police said. The victims were two women and two men, described as being in their early 40s to mid-50s, police said. The shooting occurred at the Cherokee Place Business Incubator, CNN affiliate KTVI reported.
