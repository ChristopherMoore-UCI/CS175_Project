Fox has announced that singers Jennifer Lopez and Harry Connick Jr. will join last season's judge, country star Keith Urban, at the judges table for Season 13. "American Idol" now has its panel of judges for next season. "I think this season of 'Idol' is gonna be a blast," the Fox statement quoted Urban as saying. Former judge Randy Jackson will step into the role of in-house mentor for the contestants, it was announced. "I have always been a huge fan of 'American Idol' and really enjoyed my time as a mentor on the show," Connick said in Fox's statement.
