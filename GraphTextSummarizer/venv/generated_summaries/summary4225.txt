The quartet tested positive for Furosemide, which can act as a masking agent for other banned drugs, at a meeting back in May. Cielo is reigning world champion over 50m and 100m, breaking the championship record in the shorter sprint and setting a new world mark of 46.91 seconds in the 100 in Rome two years ago. The CAS tribunal dismissed an appeal by world swimming's governing body FINA, who wanted stiffer punishments. But another swimmer on the Brazilian squad, Vinicius Waked, was banned for a year because it was his second violation for drugs offences.
