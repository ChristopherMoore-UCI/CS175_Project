Schaffelhuber  won the women's giant slalom Sunday to go on top of the downhill, super G, slalom and super combined in the women's sitting class in Sochi. Some 547 athletes from 45 countries competed for the 72 gold medals on offer -- with host nation Russia coming out on top. A spectacular closing ceremony brought an end to the 2014 Sochi Paralympic Games Sunday. International Paralympic Commitee president Philip Craven told spectators at the stadium: "The Paralympic Spirit has united and infected us all. On the day where a referendum was held in Crimea, Ukraine flagbearer, biathlete and cross-country skier Lyudmila Pavlenko entered the stadium wearing a top emblazoned with the word "Peace".
