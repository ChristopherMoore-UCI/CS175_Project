Cuba releases Alan Gross, paves way for historic easing of sanctions with Cuba More Americans still disapprove of the job Obama is doing as President. To the frustration of Obama and his aides, polls until recently showed Americans largely pessimistic about the country's financial state, despite solid job growth and a bull market on Wall Street. The approval jump follows a late-quarter jolt in Obama's governing agenda, which gained momentum after unilateral actions on immigration and relations with Cuba.
