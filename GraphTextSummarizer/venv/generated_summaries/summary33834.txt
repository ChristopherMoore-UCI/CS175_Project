In an exclusive interview with CNN on Tuesday, Yanukovich called on Tymoshenko to accept defeat and remember the pro-Western Orange Revolution that swept her to power. Prime Minister Yulia Tymoshenko has refused to concede defeat and plans to demand a recount in some districts, officials from her party, Bloc Tymoshenko, said Tuesday. But officials from Tymoshenko's party said Tuesday that she has evidence of election fraud and intends to demand a recount in some districts. "Medvedev congratulated Yanukovich on the completion of the election campaign, which was highly rated by international observers, and the success achieved at the presidential election," a Kremlin statement news statement said. "Yulia Tymoshenko is betraying the principles of her Orange Revolution" by failing to acknowledge defeat.
