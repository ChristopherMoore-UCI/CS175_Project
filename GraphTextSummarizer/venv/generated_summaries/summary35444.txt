The magic happens when you listen to Marrabenta, Mozambique's distinctive national music, an energetic mix of local dance rhythms and folk music from Portugal. One if its up-and-coming stars is a man who goes by many names: Mr Cizer Boss, Mr Guiriga, Owner of "Bazucas", "Essa dama uma goia", and "Martelo 4Pause. " Marrabenta might represent the tradition of Mozambican music, but it's also strongly influencing its future. Whatever the truth, Marrabenta has a meaning in Mozambique that goes well beyond words. That is what shows you it's Marrabenta." Marrabenta's undisputed superstar of this generation is Neyma, "the Beyonce of Mozambique".
