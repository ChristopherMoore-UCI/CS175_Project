Five deaths have been reported in Murcia, and five in Andalucia region because of flash floods caused by the rain. -- Flooding in southern Spain has left at least 10 dead, Spanish state media outlet RTVE reported Saturday. Emergency personnel on Saturday found the body of a man in a swamp in Spain's Murcia region. Two other people remained missing in Murcia, RTVE said. The girl who died was inside a vehicle that was hit by a sudden rush of water, RTVE reported.
