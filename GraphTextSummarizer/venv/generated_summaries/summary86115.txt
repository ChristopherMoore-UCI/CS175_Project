By comparison Oslo has only ever had to cease operations because of heavy snow twice since opening its doors in 1998. "We have winter six months a year, and we need to be able to handle winter operations more or less like summer operations," Nilsen says. An extra 90 people meanwhile are hired by the airport just to assist with snow clearance during winter months.
