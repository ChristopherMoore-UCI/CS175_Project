Amy Poehler, star of the NBC comedy "Parks and Recreation," and Will Arnett, best known for his work on "Arrested Development," are separating after nine years of marriage, a representative for the couple said. He also played her love interest in one episode of "Parks and Recreation." Arnett, 42, told CNN in 2010: "There's for sure a lot of joking around -- just as much as anybody in any household. " Arnett's first marriage was to actress Penelope Ann Miller. Poehler, 40, played a woman Arnett's "Arrested" character married in Las Vegas. The couple has two sons, a 3-year-old and a 2-year-old, according to the Internet Movie Database.
