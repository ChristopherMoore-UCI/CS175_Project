-- Hollywood actor Antonio Banderas is to sponsor a new Moto2 motorcycling team for the 2010 season, which will be called Jack&Jones by Antonio Banderas, the official Moto GP Web site has reported. Riders Kenny Noyes and Joan Olive will be on board Promoharris prototypes for the team's debut season. He told www.motogp.com: "I want to be involved with the team where the riders, both Joan and Kenny, have everything they need to compete at the highest level. Banderas is a well-known motorcycling enthusiast, but this is his first sport-related business venture. The team will compete in the Moto2 world championship, the series below motorcycling's elite MotoGP series, with the first race of the season in Qatar on April 11.
