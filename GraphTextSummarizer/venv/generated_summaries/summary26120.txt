The Williams sisters, Serena and Venus, are a rare example of two top singles players who have enjoyed grand slam success as a doubles pair. The defeat highlights the different skills required for success in doubles and singles. After losing the first set to their more illustrious opponents, Pospisil and Raonic squared the match by winning the last three games of the second. The top two players in the world rankings, forming a doubles pair for the first time, slumped to defeat in the first round of the Rogers Cup in Toronto. It was the first time in 34 years that the top two players in the men's world rankings have joined forces in doubles.
