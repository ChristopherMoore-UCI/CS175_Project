ISAF said coalition troops spotted insurgents placing an improvised explosive device in the Musa Qal'ah district of Helmand province and killed several with small arms fire. ISAF also said separate patrols found a number of weapons, ammunition and explosive components in caches located in eastern and southern Afghanistan. In the Tagab district of Kapisa province, ISAF said a combined patrol of coalition and Afghan forces came under fire from insurgents. -- Afghan and NATO troops killed 10 insurgents during separate sweeps in southern and eastern Afghanistan The patrol returned fire, killing and wounding what ISAF described as "numerous" insurgents.
