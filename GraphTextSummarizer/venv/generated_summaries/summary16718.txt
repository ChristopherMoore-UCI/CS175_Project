A passenger aircraft ploughed into the sea in French Polynesia on Thursday, killing at least 14 people, with another six still missing, French authorities in Papeete said. Moorea is one of around 118 islands scattered across an area the size of Europe in the South Pacific that make up France's overseas territory of French Polynesia. The bodies of all the victims found so far have been taken by French navy personnel to a mortuary. "We heard a big boom," an unidentified fisherman told French television. A Twin Otter aircraft similar to the Air Moorea plane that crashed Thursday after take-off from Moorea Island.
