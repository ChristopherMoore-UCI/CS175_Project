"There should be a key to rejuvenation in the system of scarlet jellyfish," says Kubota. It's less immortality and more regeneration, but Kubota believes these tiny marine animals could hold the secret to perpetual life. Shin Kubota is a professor at Kyoto University's Seto Marine Biological Laboratory.
