-- An uncle of President Barack Obama was arrested last week in Framingham, Massachusetts, for allegedly operating a vehicle under the influence of alcohol, police Lt. Ronald Brandolini told CNN on Monday. According to a statement from Framingham police, an officer in an unmarked police cruiser stopped Onyango Obama just after 7 p.m. after a sport utility vehicle he was driving failed to make a proper stop at an intersection. Onyango Obama was arrested on August 24 after failing a field sobriety test, Brandolini said. A spokesman at the Department of Homeland Security said U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement does not comment on specific cases. Obama was being held without bail at the request of Immigration and Customs Enforcement, it said.
