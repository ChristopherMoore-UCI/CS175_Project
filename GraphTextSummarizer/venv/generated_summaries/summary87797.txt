With such a variety of designs, fabrics and patterns on display it's clear that Kalikawe's work is her passion. Some of Kalikawe's garments even use indigenous Tanzanian fabrics like the Khanga and Kitenge. "I make dresses, I cater mostly for women," Kalikawe explains, "and I do jewelry and also sandals, which have all got an influence of African fabrics. "I choose to include other designers because there isn't particularly a space where designers can showcase their work," Kalikawe says. "From a kid, I've always known I was going to work in the industry; in design industry somehow.
