The Chinese women's gymnastics team did not use underage competitors during this year's Summer Olympic Games in Beijing, the International Gymnastics Federation said Wednesday. Widespread reports claimed that gold medal winner  He Kexin was only 14 years old. The blogger claimed to have uncovered proof that Chinese gymnast He Kexin is only 14. The Chinese women's gymnastics team won a gold medal in a team competition at the Beijing games, and five members won individual medals. Other bloggers joined the search and reported similar results.
