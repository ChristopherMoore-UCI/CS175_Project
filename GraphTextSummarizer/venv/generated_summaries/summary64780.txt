"I was always feeling something, torn between my respect for the help of Mr. Putin for my father in early years and for their work together," she says. Putin, she thinks, will be elected president again, but Russia is no longer the same. The daughter of political icon Anatoly Sobchak -- the former mayor of St. Petersburg who helped to launch Vladimir Putin's career in government back in the early 1990s -- is trying to transform herself into a political animal, a talking head, and an opponent of Putin. "They decided to change Medvedev for Putin and Putin for Medevedev and then they give out the results," Sobchak says indignantly. Putin had become prime minister after serving the maximum two consecutive terms as president.
