Die Strandloper, Off Club Mykonos Road, Langebaan; seafood braai on a West Coast beach, north of Cape Town. Where to go for braai in South Africa Many people say that the best place to braai is their own backyard. Snoek is a cheap, popular source of protein in and around Cape Town. Outside, in a covered area with live drummers and a cool misting spray, long picnic tables sit end-to-end, packed with what may be the most diverse crowd in Cape Town.
