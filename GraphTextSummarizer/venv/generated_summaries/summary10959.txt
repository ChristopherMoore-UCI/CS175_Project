Formula One 2011 lowdown Red Bull moved retained the lead in the constructors' standings, 20 points ahead of second-placed McLaren and 55 ahead of Ferrari in third. Drivers' standings after third F1 race of season in China: 1. Lewis Hamilton won his first grand prix since August 2010 in China to move into second place in the Formula One drivers' championship, but reigning champion Sebastian Vettel still holds a 21-point lead over the Briton. Hamilton, a world champion in 2008, crept above McLaren teammate Jenson Button onto 47 points, while Red Bull's Vettel has 68. Button is on 38 points in third position, one point ahead of Red Bull's Mark Webber after the Australian claimed the final spot on the podium in Shanghai ahead of the Englishman.
