I am writing this letter to all of you girls, but especially appealing to those who have had the privilege of going to school. Together, we can ensure that girls everywhere can go to school and stay in school. They die due to illness, during childbirth, due to violence, poverty, etc. -- which is probably why the world hasn't yet understood the gravity of having more than 100 million children not in primary school, of which 60 million are girls.
