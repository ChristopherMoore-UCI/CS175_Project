Mandela, a Nobel peace laureate, spent 27 years in prison for fighting against racial segregation in South Africa. -- When South Africans open their wallets, they will be greeted by images of a smiling Nelson Mandela in various denominations. "Our currency is a unique symbol of our nationhood, with many of us handling banknotes every day," said Gill Marcus, the Reserve Bank governor . Big Five --- lion, buffalo, elephant, rhino and leopard -- refer to the most celebrated animals in African game reserves. This week, the nation launched banknotes featuring a picture of the former president and anti-apartheid icon on the front.
