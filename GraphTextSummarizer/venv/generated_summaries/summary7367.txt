Atlanta police have issued an arrest warrant for rapper Gucci Mane who allegedly struck a man on the head with a champagne bottle at a downtown Atlanta nightclub. The man told police he tried to begin a conversation with the rapper at the Harlem Nights Ultra Lounge club when he was "suddenly struck." I wanted to get a picture with Gucci Mane," the man told CNN affiliate WSB-TV. The 33-year-old rapper, whose real name is Radric Davis, is now wanted for aggravated assault. "I was speaking to the security guard, and Gucci Mane hit me in the head with a bottle."
