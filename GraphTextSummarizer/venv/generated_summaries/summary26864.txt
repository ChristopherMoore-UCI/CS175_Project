The future of parking The next step in this emerging trend may be in-car services that allow drivers to find and pay for parking spaces. But not everyone is thrilled with the emergence of these apps -- most notably, cities that make money from parking meters and, yes, parking fines. Supporters say the apps let users share information about parking spaces, not the spaces themselves. Miami Beach partnered with ParkMobile and ParkMe in May to launch apps that help drivers find and pay for parking spots.
