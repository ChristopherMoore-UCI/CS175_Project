Carroll was born in New York in 1949 and spent his childhood living on the city's Lower East Side, attending Catholic schools, said Rosemary Carroll, whom he met in 1973. Poet, punk rocker and author Jim Carroll performs at a 2002 benefit in New York. Carroll died Friday at his home in Manhattan after suffering a heart attack, said his former wife, Rosemary Carroll. The fan Web site Catholicboy.com sums up Carroll's approach to music by quoting him as saying, "There ain't much time left, you're born out of this insane abyss and you're going to fall back into it, so while you're alive you might as well show your bare ass." -- Poet and punk rocker Jim Carroll has died at age 60.
