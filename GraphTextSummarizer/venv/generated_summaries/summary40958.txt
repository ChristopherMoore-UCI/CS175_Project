Chemicals are used in hundreds of useful products, and most of the thousands of chemicals we use everyday are safe. But while scientists have warned us about chemicals such as chlorinated Tris since the 1970s, they continue to be used in products all around us, including furniture, cribs, mattresses and high-chair cushions. It is time to come together to finally fix this law and protect our families from toxic chemicals once and for all. It would ensure that chemicals are tested and that those deemed dangerous are taken off the market. Now, let's be clear: The Safe Chemicals Act is not an attack on chemicals.
