In June, Douglas kicked off an animated conversation about the cause of oral cancer when he told The Guardian that he got throat cancer after engaging in oral sex. Michael Douglas has overcome throat cancer, he says Douglas is not the first celebrity to misidentify the kind of cancer they have. An oral cancer diagnosis is particularly serious; only half of the people diagnosed with oral cancer are still alive after five years, according to the CDC. "Throat" cancer and tongue cancer are both colloquial terms that fall under the oral cancer umbrella.
