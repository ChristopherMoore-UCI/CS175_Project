There is perhaps no area of the law where we have failed to educate our citizenry more than sexual harassment. This is because the public misunderstands the high burden on a plaintiff to prove sexual harassment. Federal and state law, and in the Yahoo case, the California Fair Employment and Housing Act prohibits discrimination and harassment against an employee based on sex (among other things).
