-- English Premier League side Aston Villa have confirmed that Martin O'Neill has resigned as manager of the club. "Kevin MacDonald, reserve team manager, has assumed the role of caretaker manager and will prepare the team for the opening Barclays Premier League game of the season against West Ham on Saturday at Villa Park." A statement on the club's official website read: "Aston Villa can confirm that Martin O'Neill has resigned as manager of the football club with immediate effect. Before Villa, O'Neill had successful spells with English clubs Wycombe Wanderers and Leicester City, as well as Scottish giants Celtic. Paul Faulkner, chief executive of Aston Villa, added: "The club would like to thank Martin for the great work he has done at Aston Villa over the past four years.
