Under the plans, funding sourced from a tax on government-owned slot machines at race tracks will be redirected. "Relying on handouts is not the way forward and it is all about trying to harness all the assets you have in racing and not sit back on a single funding mechanism," says Nigel Roddis, development director of Racing for Change, an organization looking to boost race track attendances and revenues.
