Dinamo Kiev captain Oleh Husyev was left unconscious after colliding with Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk goalkeeper Denis Boyko in the first half of the Ukrainian Premier League match between the two teams. After Husyev hit the ground, Dnipro's Jaba Kankava was quickly by his side.
