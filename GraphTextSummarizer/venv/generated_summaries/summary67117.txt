This is the second accident in a week involving a celebrity on Malibu's Pacific Coast Highway Last week, reality TV star Bruce Jenner was involved in a multicar accident that left a woman dead. He was not cited and there was no evidence of alcohol or illegal substances,  said Lt. Anthony Rotella  of the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department. Actor Gary Busey hit a pedestrian while he was backing out of a parking lot in Malibu, authorities said. Busey, 70, was leaving a shopping center Friday afternoon when he allegedly hit the unidentified woman. Busey has appeared in movies such as "The Buddy Holly Story" and "Lethal Weapon."
