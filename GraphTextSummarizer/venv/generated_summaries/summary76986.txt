It doesn't really matter that many of Shinya's tricks probably wouldn't be possible without long sleeves and quick hands -- at one point he takes too long to pull a piece of cheese out of his sleeve. Apple CEO Steve Jobs deemed the iPad "a truly magical and revolutionary product" when he unveiled the tablet-style computer in January. Another highpoint of Shinya's act is when he pretends to pour a glass of milk onto his iPad. Sure David Copperfield can make the Statue of Liberty vanish in front of a live audience, but what can he do with an iPad? He redeems himself twice over when he makes a live dove appear in his hand moments after showing a video of it on the iPad screen.
