The Dallas Cowboys have decided to part ways with wide receiver Terrell Owens, according to published reports. According to published reports, Owens believed Romo and Witten held private meetings and created plays without including him. The Cowboys released Owens late Wednesday, according to published reports. Published reports also said Owens had issues with Cowboys offensive coordinator Jason Garrett's play-calling. Owens refuted reports that it was a suicide attempt and claimed a combination of painkillers and supplements made him groggy.
