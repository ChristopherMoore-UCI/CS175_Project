The NBC sitcom "30 Rock" lost some flight attendant fans after actor Alec Baldwin was kicked off an American Airlines flight when he refused to turn off his phone. "30 Rock," meanwhile, continues to air on some American Airline flights in accordance with its standard programming procedures, airline spokesman Tim Smith said. The actor mocked American Airlines over the weekend during a cameo appearance on "Saturday Night Live," issuing an apology to himself while dressed as airline "Capt. "We fully support the flight attendants and the way they handled the situation," spokesman Jeff Pharr said. Baldwin was booted off the flight because he refused to turn off his cell phone after the plane's doors were closed for departure, the airline said.
