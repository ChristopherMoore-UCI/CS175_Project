His goal, he said, is to tell the stories of people rendered powerless by wars, provide objective images and shed light on human rights issues. Fabio Bucciarelli is an Italian photographer whose work focuses on conflict and war. South Sudan split from Sudan in 2011 as part of a peace deal that ended decades of war in Africa's largest nation. Instead of jubilant faces of hope, he saw defeated faces haunted by a return of ghosts of past wars. He found citizens worn out by decades of war -- but looking forward to an independent, peaceful future.
