Advanced breast cancer is different than early stage breast cancer because the treatment path is much more winding. While early stage breast cancer survival rates remain high -- with many women and men going on to live long, healthy lives -- there is no cure for advanced breast cancer. Advanced breast cancer -- Stage III and IV (metastatic) -- is a chronic disease for which there will be no remission or survival. In her response to living with advanced breast cancer, her most striking quality was her extraordinary strength. Advanced breast cancer was a very different experience.
