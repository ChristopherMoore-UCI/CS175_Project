African policy makers need to seriously invest in creating more business schools and put the right infrastructures into place to save the private sector industry of Africa and youth of Africa. But whilst a technology revolution is taking place in Africa, with so many of these young men and women building and creating apps, they still face huge challenges in understanding how to start businesses around their innovations. But now the challenge for technology entrepreneurs is not merely to innovate and create apps, but to turn them into lucrative business ideas. The reality check has not yet taken place in the minds of Africa's innovators and lots of time is being wasted on unrealistic desires. Young entrepreneurs in Africa have a mosaic of demands that need urgent attention.
