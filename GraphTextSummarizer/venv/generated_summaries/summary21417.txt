New York received $93,213,963 for flooding from Hurricane Irene that pummeled the East Coast last August and for damages from Tropical Storm Lee in Schoharie, Tioga, Broome, Greene, and Orange counties. Alabama was given $55,566,078 for losses from last year's severe storms, tornadoes, straight-line winds and flooding in Tuscaloosa, Marion, Jefferson and DeKalb counties and in Birmingham. Vermont secured $21,660,211 for the destruction caused by Tropical Storm Irene in Washington and Windsor counties. Missouri received $53,985,768 for recovery from last spring's tornado, severe storms and flooding in Jasper County and in Joplin. New Jersey garnered $15,598,506 to help Passaic County recover from the damage of Hurricane Irene.
