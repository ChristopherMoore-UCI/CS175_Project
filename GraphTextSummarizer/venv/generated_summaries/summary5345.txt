b) Argentina vs. Nigeria Sitting at the top of the group, Argentina is guaranteed a place in the round of 16, while Nigeria will be battling to hang onto second place. If they lose, Ghana (or, less likely, Portugal) could sneak into second place in Group G. If Iran can beat Bosnia by more than one goal, and Nigeria loses to Argentina, Iran goes through to the knockout stage as the second-ranking team in Group F. The Middle Easterners have a good record against Bosnia. If they pull off a draw, they get a point and hang onto second place. -- They're hardly a global soccer powerhouse, but Iran's World Cup run has President Hassan Rouhani tweeting pictures of himself watching a game in a T-shirt.
