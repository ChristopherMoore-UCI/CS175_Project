Health officials in Odisha state have launched an investigation after reports that bicycle pumps were used on 56 women undergoing sterilization surgery at a government-run community health center in Banarpal, Angul district, Friday. A health official who did not wish to be named told CNN that the use of bicycle pumps in sterilization surgery was widespread.
