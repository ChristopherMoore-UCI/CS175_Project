Now, the music world is mourning the death of Porcaro, who died after a battle with Lou Gehrig's disease, also known as amyotrophic lateral sclerosis or ALS. "Our brother Mike passed away peacefully in his sleep at 12:04 AM last night at home surrounded by his family," Porcaro's brother Steve, the band's keyboardist, posted on Facebook on Sunday. Toto vocalist and guitarist Steve Lukather also called Porcaro a brother. For over two decades, bassist Mike Porcaro was a rock star with the band Toto, playing venues around the world.
