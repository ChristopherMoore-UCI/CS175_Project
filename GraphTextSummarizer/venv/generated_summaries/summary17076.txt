The British DJ turned festival impresario is the man behind the Bestival music festival and despite the challenges of putting on the annual event, his love of festivals remains undiminished. A desire to get back to the "boutique" feel of its earlier days lead to the birth of Bestival's little sister, Camp Bestival, in 2008.
