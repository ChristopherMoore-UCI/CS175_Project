Maggie Berry, founder of recruitment and career development agency Women in Technology, says employers are crying out for more women in their technology teams. Berry said firms could help by reducing the long list of job requirements in advertisements that could be putting women off applying. Get out there Technology recruiters would love to take on more women, but can't do so if they don't see your CV, says Berry. One comment from Berry's survey may ring true for many women with children: "The male bosses had no sympathy or understanding towards working mothers."
