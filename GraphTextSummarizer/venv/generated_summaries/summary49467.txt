South Sudan, which became independent in 2011, is facing many challenges. A strong Arms Trade Treaty will help restrict the flow of weapons and bullets to conflict-riddled countries like South Sudan. Weapons have been flooding into nations like South Sudan for decades. The government of South Sudan has been conducting campaigns to disarm civilians, but this will not be effective if weapons are still easily accessible. -- During a recent trip to South Sudan, I was reminded of my childhood and the challenges I have had to overcome.
