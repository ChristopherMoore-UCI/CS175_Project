Force me to walk -- with tubes in their noses, barf buckets resting on tricycle handlebars, beads of courage clinking from their IV poles. Finding inner strength at a time when being strong seems like such an impossibility is what can pull you through cancer. The kids at Seattle Children's Hospital got me out of bed on days that I wouldn't want to. My proudest moment was during a scary clinic visit when I toyed with going into septic shock. A parent's reality, holding onto a tiny thread of hope
