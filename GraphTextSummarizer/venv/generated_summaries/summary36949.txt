...delivers unique spectacle, breathtaking sights, narrative excitement and an overarching anti-imperialist, back-to-nature theme that will play very well around the world..." Mike Goodridge, Screen International: ...once again takes cinema to a new level of remarkable spectacle ... But mostly, it's a place of wonder full of exotically freakish animal composites -- iridescent lizard birds, hammer-headed rhinos -- and sentient vegetation. At one stage the deranged general leading the attack, with echoes of George Bush, declares: "Our survival relies on pre-emptive action. At times it verges on the tacky, like a futuristic air freshener advertisement with the color contrast turned up to the max. We will fight terror with terror."
