As more states legalized casinos, the Nevada casino industry -- and especially the Las Vegas industry -- responded by moving up-market. These were the best casino jobs in the country, which explains why total national casino wages lag 7% behind the 2007 peak even as industry revenues have nearly caught up to the 2007 level.
