Tel Aviv mayor Ron Huldai, who has been in office since 1998, was criticized by some for what they saw as a heavy-handed police response to the protests. Amit Adler, a 40-year-old writer taking part in recent protests, said: "Now we are politically matured and we understand in order to change the cost of housing and living, you must change the system. He added: "We are in the middle of creating three projects for affordable housing in the city of Tel Aviv. "Before people didn't find it attractive to live in Tel Aviv, now everybody wants to live here so the price of real estate goes up."
