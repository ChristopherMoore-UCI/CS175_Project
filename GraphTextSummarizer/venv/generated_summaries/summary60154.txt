"What we are doing is looking at digital practice across a range of art forms," said curator Conrad Bodman. "Lines are blurred between art forms." Interactivity is another key theme, said Bodman. Digital artists come from all kinds of backgrounds, according to Bodman, and this is one of the show's key themes. Collaborator Suzuki added that the concept was from a "vision of the future of music and music distribution," adding that it was a response to illegal downloading and the energy that artists spend on live shows. They are artists, architects, designers, musicians and game developers and their pioneering work includes interactive sculptures, wearable technology, immersive experiences and works built via crowd-sourcing.
