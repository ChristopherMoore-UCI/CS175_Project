Toys previously inducted into the Hall of Fame include Monopoly (1998), roller Skates (1999), jack-in-the-box (2005) and last year's inductees: Star Wars action figures and dominoes. Rubber duck and chess squeaked and check mated past 10 other finalists, including Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Pac-Man and My Little Pony to win this year's honor. -- Rubber ducky, you ARE the one -- the chosen one, that is, for this year's induction into the National Toy Hall of Fame. The National Toy Hall of Fame is in Rochester, New York. Inductees are selected on the basis of several factors including icon-status, longevity and innovation.
