Based in Atlanta, the nonprofit has collected 100 tons of used soap from hotels in the United States and reprocessed it for shipment to impoverished nations. CNN: What did it mean to you to personally deliver your group's first box of soap to orphans last summer? I want to be a soap depot where people who are under distress who need soap ... whenever there is a crisis globally, they can come to us and we can give them the soap. They are already on the ground doing incredible work and know where the needs are for soap within the communities they serve. I don't want to ever see a child without soap.
