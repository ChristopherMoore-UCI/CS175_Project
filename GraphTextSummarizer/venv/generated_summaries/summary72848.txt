Zip on over to Andora Restaurant for fresh New American cuisine, or try the gourmet Sewickley Cafe for great food that won't blow your budget. Pittsburgh is home to vibrant neighborhoods filled with distinctive shops and restaurants. Semi Suite Boutique sells unique kitchenware, or try Hot Haute Hot for eclectic house furnishings. Closed mills and clear skies have yielded beautiful neighborhoods that feature great shopping and even better dining. Dotted along Butler Street are independent galleries, swanky boutiques and locally owned cafes serving gourmet food (you won't find a Starbucks here).
