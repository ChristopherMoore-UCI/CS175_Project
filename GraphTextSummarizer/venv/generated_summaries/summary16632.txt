Facebook game developer Zynga has proved once again that it knows exactly what it needs to do to keep millions of Facebook users happy and occupied. In less than a month, its latest game "CityVille" has become the most popular application on Facebook, surpassing Zynga's previous hit "FarmVille" in all areas. According to AppData, "CityVille" now has 16.8 million daily active users, compared to "FarmVille's" 16.4 million.
