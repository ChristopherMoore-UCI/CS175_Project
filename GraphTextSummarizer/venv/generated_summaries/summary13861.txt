And, like so many others, I can now look back and realize that something was a little creepy about the vibe around the Second Mile program and coach Sandusky. Sports media are just too close to the games financially and socially to fully examine the depths of sports culture. Though most sports journalists will never admit this, the main goal in sports reporting is to celebrate and illuminate the games.
