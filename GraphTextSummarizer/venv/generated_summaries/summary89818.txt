" Jim's feats have not gone unnoticed back in South Africa, and press interest in him has been extensive. Tragedy took Jim from his home in Mthatha near the east coast of South Africa to the Western Cape as a teenager, but it would prove to be the place his sporting dream crystallized. It would be great for me to see a lot of black South Africans in the sport." " It is no surprise that despite the treacherous conditions in his first World Tour race, Jim's dedication to the cause was absolute. But not even the extreme weather conditions that blighted the Milan-San Remo cycle race could tarnish the pride the 22-year-old felt at becoming a pioneer for his sport in South Africa.
