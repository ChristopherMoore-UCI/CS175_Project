Bergdahl back at work Bergdahl spent his first day of regular duty working at the headquarters of U.S. Army North in Texas on Monday, the Army said. The Army has no definitive finding that Bergdahl deserted because that would require knowing his intent -- something officials couldn't learn without talking to the soldier, a U.S. military official told CNN. Army general to start investigating how, why Bergdahl left base CNN's Martin Savidge and Steve Almasy contributed to this report. Like many soldiers at a new assignment, he spent much of his day getting paperwork straightened out, Army spokesman Don Manuszewski said. The Army has said he has yet to be interviewed by the officer investigating his case, and he returned to regular duty at Fort Sam Houston in San Antonio, Texas, on Monday following weeks of counseling and medical care.
