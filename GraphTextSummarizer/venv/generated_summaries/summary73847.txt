Last February, Burger King introduced a line of mini-burgers called "BK Burger Shots." Despite a Twilight-themed media blitz in 2009, BK Burger Shots have disappeared from the Burger King menu. "We really felt that this was an opportune time to introduce our Mini Blizzard to all of our loyal customers, and of course people can come in more often and enjoy their favorite Blizzard flavor," Peters says. We also felt there was an opportunity there with a smaller size Blizzard -- which is our signature product -- to perhaps bundle it with a combo meal or a food meal, as well."
