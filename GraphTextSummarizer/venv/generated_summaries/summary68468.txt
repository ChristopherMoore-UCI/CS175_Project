"Wageuzi is Swahili, it means 'transformers,'" explains creator Andrew Kaggia, 25. "When people they watch the film, they're able to relate the characters to the actual politicians," adds Kaggia, who first released Wageuzi in December 2011 (Kenya's elections were initially scheduled to take place in 2012).
