Help Exchange, Worldwide A website that lists worldwide hosts who are looking for an extra pair of hands, whether it be in their farm or garden, working in schools or on religious projects, building houses or community centers, or simply helping a family take the kids to school. Placements are at schools or universities, but many volunteers help with community projects in additional to their teaching. Open only to U.S. residents, but similar projects are available for other nationals with VSO. Participate in a variety of projects, from healthcare and the environment to business development. The chance to give something back, an opportunity to share your skills and knowledge, to meet other travelers or simply to meet the locals...
