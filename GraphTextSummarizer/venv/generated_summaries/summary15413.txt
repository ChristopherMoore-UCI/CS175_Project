World champion Valentino Rossi pipped Fiat Yamaha team-mate Jorge Lorenzo on the finish line in a thrilling finale to the Catalunya MotoGP. Daniel Pedrosa, Colin Edwards, Randy De Puniet, Mika Kallio and Nicky Hayden completed the top 10 on Sunday. Rossi shows his delight after clinching a thrilling victory over Jorge Lorenzo in Barcelona on Sunday. "It was a great battle, the two Yamahas were flying on this track," Rossi told BBC Sport. Casey Stoner finished third, meaning that he, Rossi and Lorenzo are now all level at the top of the championship standings on 106 points.
