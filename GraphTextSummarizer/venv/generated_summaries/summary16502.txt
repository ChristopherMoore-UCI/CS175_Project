Action actor Joey Ansah says the days of martial arts beefcake with no acting skills are over. Do you prefer muscle-bound tough guys like Arnie or modern action heroes like Matt Damon? The real difference between martial arts or real combat and screen fighting is that real fights are shorter. In action you have to be able to deliver good technique and give good reactions, because reactions make your opponents' actions look more powerful.
