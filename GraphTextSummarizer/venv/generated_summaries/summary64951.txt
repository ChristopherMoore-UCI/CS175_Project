Regional tensions Tensions over the killings of Israeli teens and Palestinian boys marked the run-up to the conflict  in Gaza between Israel and Hamas this summer, which took dozens of Israeli lives and more than 2,100 Palestinian lives. A  Palestinian man rammed into a cyclist and four Israeli border police while he was driving in Jerusalem on Friday, authorities and first responders said. Authorities are treating the incident as a terror attack, according to police spokesman Micky Rosenfeld. Israeli police identified the attacker as a Palestinian man in his early 20s from East Jerusalem. "Hamas movement blesses this heroic act and considers it a natural response to the Occupations crimes, Sami Abu Zuhri, a Hamas spokesman, posted on Facebook.
