Indeed, there is no way to achieve any scaled and durable reform without stepping into the arena of government, policy, politics, and elections. This is why the organization I run, Citizen University, is working with partners around the country to teach people about the skills and systems of power. So seeing protesters from Seattle to New York engage in civil disobedience on Black Friday was heartening. The members of the millennial generation who are driving so many of the protests today are idealistic and networked -- but also exceedingly cynical about traditional politics and government. But what the grassroots organizers in Ferguson teach us is that there is no avoiding politics.
