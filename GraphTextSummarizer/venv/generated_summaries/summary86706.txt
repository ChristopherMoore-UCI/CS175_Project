Lonely Planet editors made the final selections after gathering nominations from their authors, contributors and larger Lonely Planet community. Winter wolf tracking, Yellowstone National Park, Wyoming Sure it's cold during the winter at Yellowstone National Park, and some of the roads are closed. Climbing El Capitan, Yosemite National Park, California El Capitan at Yosemite National Park is known as the rock where big wall rock climbing began, and the views from the top are spectacular. The natural springs of Crystal River National Wildlife Refuge are warm enough for manatees during the Florida winter season. Snowshoeing in the White Mountains, New Hampshire The White Mountains of New Hampshire are popular for winter outdoor sports enthusiasts because they keep their snow longer than other regions of the United States.
