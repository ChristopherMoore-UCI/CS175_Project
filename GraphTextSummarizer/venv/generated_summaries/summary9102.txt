Water washed into thousands of homes and 14 people died. For many years, residents were obliged to adhere to strict water restrictions during the long wait for rain. A massive dam with a capacity of more than one million megaliters -- about the size of two Sydney Harbours -- was built at Wivenhoe, west of the city, to provide flood protection system. High water closes roads in Ipswich, Queensland -- Brisbane is more accustomed to drought than flood.
