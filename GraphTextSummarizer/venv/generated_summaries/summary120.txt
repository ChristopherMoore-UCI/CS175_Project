Watch parents scramble for safe milk » Two brothers who sold fresh milk used to produce contaminated baby milk powder were arrested by Chinese investigators Monday. Twelve of those arrested are involved in the collection of milk and the production of milk products, authorities said. It is not the first time Sanlu has been connected to a scandal involving tainted milk powder, according to China Daily. In 2004, at least 13 infants in the eastern Anhui province died of malnutrition after drinking milk powder that had little to no nutrition.
