Pools of blood on the floor show how their young victims, many of them sons and daughters of army personnel from around Peshawar, were sprayed with bullets as they sat at each machine. They killed little children. Shrapnel from the ball bearings packed inside the vest, to cause maximum harm when detonated, pockmarks the wall to one side. More blood has pooled under red fabric-covered seats set in rows. Soldiers with guns still stand guard as the media are allowed in to see the culmination of a bloody siege that lasted several hours.
