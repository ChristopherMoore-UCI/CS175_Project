Mrwebi says boxing has also given her purpose whilst growing up in Hillbrow, a notoriously dangerous inner city neighborhood of Johannesburg. It's here, inside this small, dark gym at Hillbrow Boxing Club in Johannesburg, where Mrwebi, the South African female welterweight champion, comes to train twice a day. "Hillbrow is not a good place but this gym has produced a lot of boxers, it has produced a lot of champions. -- Her gloves held high, Rita Mrwebi dances in the middle of a blue boxing ring. Built in what used to be a gas station, the gym has been a haven for many youngsters in this densely populated part of Johannesburg, known for its high poverty and crime rates.
