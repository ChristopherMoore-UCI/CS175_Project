Stephen Strasburg made his last appearance of the 2012 season Friday, team spokesman John Dever confirmed to CNN. Manager Davey Johnson made good on the promise after Strasburg's rough outing Friday night against the Miami Marlins. Debate has raged in baseball over the rare move, particularly because the National League-East leading Nationals appear headed to a division title and a post-season berth. -- The Washington Nationals -- the upstarts battling for a playoff spot -- ended the season of its star pitcher to keep him healthy for next year.
