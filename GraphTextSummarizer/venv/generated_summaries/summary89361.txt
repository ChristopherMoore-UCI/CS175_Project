Malisha Spencer, manager at Indy All Night, which offers food delivery in Indianapolis nearly 24 hours a day, says business nearly doubles when a heavy cold front comes through. Another type of business that sees its phones ring off the hook when the temperature drops is those specializing in deliveries. During the snowstorms last year, Minnesota-based Cargill Inc. told The Wall Street Journal that the demand for deicing salt was "simply unprecedented in recent memory. "They're preparing and planning ahead, which is really good so they don't have to drive out in the streets," Mark Hayman, owner of Hayman's West Side Ace Hardware, told CNN affiliate KWQC. Salt for highway deicing makes up 41% of total salt sales in the U.S.
