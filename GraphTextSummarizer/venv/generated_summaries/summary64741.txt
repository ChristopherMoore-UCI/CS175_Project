He'll spend the last two months of his operational service attached to Australian Defence Force units in Darwin, Perth and Sydney. "We have prepared a challenging program that will see Captain Wales deploy on urban and field training exercises, domestic deployments, as well as participate in Indigenous engagement activities," said Air Chief Marshal Mark Binski of the Australian Defence Force. Both groups assist wounded or sick soldiers either return to duty or transition to civilian life. Prince Harry does have a few more responsibilities before he returns to civilian life. He helped spearhead and continues to champion the Invictus Games, a competition for former military personnel who have been wounded in the line of duty.
