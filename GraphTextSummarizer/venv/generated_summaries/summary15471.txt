Computer experts will continue to try to shut down the Web addresses that let Conficker's author communicate with infected machines, he said. Many authors of previous computer viruses have come from Eastern Europe outside the jurisdiction of the European Union, experts said. However, that doesn't mean the much-talked-about worm, called Conficker.c, is a joke, computer security experts told CNN on Wednesday. The worm's author could steal financial information, shut down Web pages, track keystrokes or send spam from infected computers. but so far has not caused problems for the millions of computers that are believed to be infected.
