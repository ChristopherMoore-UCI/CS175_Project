"People who remember it (the club) still speak of it with a dreamy look in their eyes," says editor in chief David DiBenedetto. "You're living and sleeping on top of a wild coral reef," says DiBenedetto. "It's like paddling back in time, and the river is slow moving, so it's an easy paddle," DiBenedetto says. "You can enjoy all the benefits of being a farmer without having to do the backbreaking labor," says DiBenedetto. "You're at a first-rate resort with first-rate amenities, and the architecture of the cottages is beautiful," says DiBenedetto.
