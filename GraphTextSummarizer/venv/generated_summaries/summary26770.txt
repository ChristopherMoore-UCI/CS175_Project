He is set to return in a new NBC show next year, but it's unlikely van den Akker will be called upon to make more sweaters. Fans began writing in, asking where Cosby got the sweater, van den Akker recalled. Freelance illustrator Kelly Tucker started a Tumblr in 2011 dedicated to Cosby's iconic sweaters. Each post summarizes episodes, showing off Cosby's best sweaters, with close-ups of each pattern. Van den Akker delivered a sweater to the studio where Cosby was working on his NBC sitcom, "The Cosby Show," early on in its eight-season run.
