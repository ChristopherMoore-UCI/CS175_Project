Wages remain flat, and will likely stay flat until unemployment contracts more, but Americans can begin to see better times ahead for the first time in a long time. President Obama will begin to claim more credit. For five years, U.S. politics have been shaped by economic hardship. By 2014, again assuming that Congress does not leap off the fiscal cliff, it will likely look as if Obama is working. President Obama responded by attacking Romney as out of touch, assuming (probably correctly) that he could not win by running on his record.
