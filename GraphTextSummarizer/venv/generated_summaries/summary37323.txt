The initial version of the doll was an art project of sorts, with Lamm creating a model of the average woman and doing a side-by-side comparison with Barbie. Here's what you need to know about Lamm and the doll: Germ of an idea It began in 2013, when Lamm began playing with the idea of what Barbie would look like as an average woman. The response to the project was overwhelming, with even actress and singer Demi Lovato taking to Twitter to call Lamm's doll "awesome." His answer came in the form of the Lammily doll: a shorter, broader, brunette version of the idealistic blond Mattel doll. Down the road, Lamm said he plans to release a boy doll.
