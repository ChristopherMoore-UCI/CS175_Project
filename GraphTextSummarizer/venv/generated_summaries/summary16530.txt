" Saudi woman files suit over right to drive Domestic violence is a problem in Saudi Arabia but no reliable figures exist on how many people get abused each year. -- Human rights campaigners have welcomed a landmark decision in Saudi Arabia designed to combat domestic violence. Until last week, no written penal code existed on domestic violence and physical abuse. According to researchers there have been very few instances in the past where rapes and physical violence cases went to court as women do not dare to come forward. For the first time in Saudi Arabia's history the kingdom's cabinet has approved a ban on domestic violence and other forms of abuse both at home and in the workplace.
