Lord Byron popularizes the Vinegar and Water Diet, which entails drinking water mixed with apple cider vinegar. A Florida doctor creates the Cookie Diet, a plan where you eat cookies made with a blend of amino acids. 1992: Dr. Robert C. Atkins publishes "Dr. Atkins' New Diet Revolution," a high-protein, low-carb plan. The aerobics craze steps into high gear when Jane Fonda launches her first exercise video, "Workout: Starring Jane Fonda." The Zone Diet, which calls for a specific ratio of carbs, fat, and protein at each meal, begins to attract celeb fans.
