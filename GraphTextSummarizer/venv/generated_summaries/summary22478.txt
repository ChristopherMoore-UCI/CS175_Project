Phone-controlled cars Samsung smartphones will soon be able to connect with Toyota's In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI) system using a new Car Mode app. The app will let drivers add voice control, multimedia capabilities and internet services to their Toyota motor. All that and it's running on the latest version of Google's Android operating system called Ice Cream Sandwich. A host of intelligent features are promised, including a fingerprint scanner and compatibility with health devices such as blood pressure monitors. The phone itself is no slouch, with a 4.3-inch Super AMOLED display running on a 1.5GHz Snapdragon processor.
