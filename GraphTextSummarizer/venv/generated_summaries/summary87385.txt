Hamas agrees to stop firing rockets at Israeli border communities and Israel will allow limited trade into and out of Gaza. An Israeli court convicts Hamas leader Sheikh Ahmed Yassin of ordering Hamas members to kidnap and kill two Israeli soldiers. Hamas is an Islamic fundamentalist organization whose military wing has admitted responsibility for terrorist attacks against Israeli civilians and soldiers. March 22, 2004 - Hamas leader Yassin is killed by Israeli air strikes. Hamas supporters wave flags and shout slogans in Gaza on December 14 to mark the group's 21st anniversary.
