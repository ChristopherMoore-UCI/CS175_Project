"Queens Park Rangers Football Club has today parted company with manager Neil Warnock -- Neil Warnock became the second English Premier League manager to be sacked this season when Queens Park Rangers announced his departure Sunday. " Warnock, who led QPR back to the top-flight last season, left proud of his record. Only a last-minute goal prevented them from crashing out of the English FA Cup Saturday at third-flight MK Dons, Warnock's final match in charge. " Former Manchester City manager Mark Hughes is the early favorite to succeed the 63-year-old Warnock at Loftus Road.
