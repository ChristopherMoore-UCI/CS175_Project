Parkinson's didn't stop his space walk Maura started to notice my good days and bad days, and I would see her instinctively change our plans and schedule. To thwart the development I was able to stay active, something I had always been; however, I started to notice small changes, and the inability on some days to complete the simple tasks I had always done. He came over and helped me in silence, the way Maura or the girls would. Maura, my wife, didn't blink an eye.
