-- A year ago, Gisella Asante and her daughter Jan Asante found themselves standing in tears in the middle of broken glass, scattered clothes hangers, bits of wedding dresses and burnt pieces of fabric. In just a few hours of riot madness, Gisella and Jan Asante lost 20 years' worth of work. Jan talks to the customers and prepares the designs, while Gisella is the one cutting the fabric and sewing. Then suddenly, she turned to Jan and said: "You have more strength than I thought you have. "Having the customers, the work, it kept us going, we were on autopilot," Jan said.
