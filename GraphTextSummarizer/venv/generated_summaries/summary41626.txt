Somchai Piakhan. -- At least 11 construction workers were killed Tuesday when a big cement beam fell on them in a Bangkok suburb, at the site where they were working, Thai police said. More than a dozen injured people have been transported to nearby hospitals, and at least one worker is still trapped inside the rubble, police said. Bangpli is southeast of Bangkok, in Samut Prakarn province. CNN's Kocha Olarn reported from Bangkok, and Jethro Mullen wrote from Hong Kong.
