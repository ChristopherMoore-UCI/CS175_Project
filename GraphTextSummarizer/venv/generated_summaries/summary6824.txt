Pro players get paid for their sacrifices regarding extra games, but college players don't. And since the bulk of the TV and advertising revenue would flow to playoff games, what would happen financially to the bulk of the bowls without playoff games? The site of current bowl games, you say, or maybe NFL stadiums, neutral sites or various places on the dark side of the moon? Just one extra game means a slew of more practices, film sessions and team meetings for those student-athletes. In their minds, a playoff system gives them the chance to add millions to their millions.
