Valued at $118.9 billion, Apple increased its value by 21% year-on-year, while Google's brand value of $107.43 billion jumped 15% compared to last year. Facebook has grown again last year, with the number of monthly active users at 1.32 billion, up 14% year-over-year. "Apple and Google's meteoric rise to more than $100 billion is truly a testament to the power of brand building," according to Jez Frampton, Interbrand's CEO. But Facebook was the ranking's top riser, gaining 86% in brand value and jumping 33 spots to number 29. It's $2.91 billion revenues in second quarter smashed expectations, growing 61% compared to last year.
