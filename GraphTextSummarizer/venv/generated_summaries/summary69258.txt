At a buffet, or a restaurant with enormous portions The problem: Buffets offer limitless amounts of food, and much of it has tons of calories. Don't rely on your willpower to get you through these tough times, advises James Hill, executive director of the Anschutz Health and Wellness Center at the University of Colorado. Restaurants with big portions of delicious foods make it hard to push the plate away.
