Polio cases suspected in Syria Photos provided to CNN by Syria Relief show a young girl with painted nails lying in a hospital bed with head wounds. Volunteer doctor David Nott, a British surgeon who's worked in several Syrian hospitals with the charity Syria Relief, says snipers are playing a "targeting game," and heavily pregnant women are on the hit list. The image would be chilling enough without knowing the child was still in its mother's womb when it became the target of snipers hiding in the shadows in northern Syria.
