The Taliban has threatened to kill Bergdahl if foreign troops continue targeting civilians in the name of search operations in Ghazni and Paktika province, Taliban commander Mulvi Sangeen said by telephone Friday after being contacted by CNN at an undisclosed location. They were made available to CNN by U.S. Forces Afghanistan. He was captured June 30 from Paktika province in southeastern Afghanistan. Last week, the U.S. military distributed pamphlets in eastern Afghanistan in an effort to locate him. The U.S. military said it believed Bergdahl may have been moved to various locations.
