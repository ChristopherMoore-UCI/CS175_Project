Raitt said she chose to recast "Right Down the Line" as "a kind of tougher reggae song." As Raitt's albums typically do, "Slipstream" contains mostly cover songs. Raitt's website lists more than 100 nonprofit partners she encourages fans to learn more about, many having to do with music education.
