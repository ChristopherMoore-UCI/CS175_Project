"I started my photography here in Makoko," Afose told CNN when we meet at the plush surrounds of the 2014 Lagos Photo Festival. Yet despite the international recognition, its the daily hustle and bustle of Makoko that continues to inspire Afose. Afose was rapidly on the spot when a fire that tore through part of Makoko in 2013. It is likely the Makoko fire wouldn't have garnered as much attention in the national and international press without Afose's timely and grabbing images. For photographer Sulayman Afose, however, the muddy waters of Makoko have always been home.
