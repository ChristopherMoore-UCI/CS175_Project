Legoland Malaysia is the sixth theme park for the brand, joining properties in Denmark, England, Germany and two in the United States. Apart from water slides and pools, one of the coolest attractions of the Legoland Malaysia Water Park is Build-A-Raft River, which lets kids put together their own raft made of soft Lego bricks and float through the park via a slow-moving current.
