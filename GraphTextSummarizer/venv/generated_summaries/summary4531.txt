-- Mahela Jayawardene has told CNN that cricket has helped the World Cup-chasing Sri Lanka team recover from their darkest hour. Jayawardene was one of six Sri Lanka players to be injured when masked gunman fired on the team bus in Lahore. But Jayawardene says Sri Lanka will be focused firmly on cricket on Saturday. Jayawardene says India and Sri Lanka were destined to meet each other. Though the events of 2009 will never be forgotten, the 33-year-old says the team have moved on as they eye their second World Cup triumph.
