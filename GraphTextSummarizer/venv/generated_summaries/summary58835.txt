Time and again I have heard politicians, journalists and business people tell me that it isn't so bad here, that Denver is poised to lead the nation in an economic recovery. Beginning last year more customers started coming in to sell Decker their guitars in hopes of making ends meet. Hari Dallakoti says business at his UPS store in Denver, Colorado, is down 25 percent. He agrees that business is better in Denver than in other parts of the country and is confident he will weather the economic storm, but it may be a while before things get better for him. "The recession affected us a little last year but overall -- knock on wood -- it hasn't really affected me too much," says owner Gregory Decker, who thinks the recession may actually be helping his business.
