Pilgrims were returning from a Hindu ritual at a temple when a vehicle overturned, prompting the stampede, said Jai Kumar, home secretary of Kerala state. Video footage showed authorities loading up bodies in large pickup trucks. -- A stampede near a religious temple killed 100 people in southern India, authorities said. Stampedes at events in India have occurred several times because large crowds gather at narrow, congested areas. The vehicle struck many of the people in a narrow stretch through a forest, the news agency Press Trust of India reported.
