U.S. President Barack Obama ordered the deployment of 30,000 extra U.S. forces to Afghanistan this year, increasing the total American troop commitment to almost 100,000. Last year, 516 coalition troops were killed in Afghanistan. -- A roadside blast killed a NATO service member Wednesday in northern Afghanistan The force comprises troops from dozens of countries. This year is already the deadliest for coalition and U.S. forces since the battle against the Taliban started some nine years ago.
