Southwest Flight 4013 from Chicago's Midway Airport was scheduled to land at Branson airport on Sunday evening, but it instead showed up at Taney County airport, said Brad Hawkins, a spokesman for the airline. Hawkins declined to say whether the flight had flown on to Branson airport from Taney County airport. The Southwest Airlines website said the flight had arrived at Gate 3 of Branson airport. -- A Southwest Airlines plane carrying more than 100 passengers landed at the wrong Missouri airport Sunday The two airports are about seven miles apart and serve the same area of southwest Missouri.
