Joe Jackson, father of Michael Jackson, attends the BET Awards on Sunday in Los Angeles, California. Among the award winners, Atlanta, Georgia-based singer Keri Hilson, who was nominated in four categories, received best new artist and Beyonce won best female artist. "I wish the world had recognized him when he were living," Joseph Jackson told CNN's Don Lemon outside the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles, California. He was "the greatest star in the world," Joseph Jackson, father of the late entertainer, said on Sunday night. In addition to Foxx, presenter Jeremy Piven mixed praise for Jackson with comic relief.
