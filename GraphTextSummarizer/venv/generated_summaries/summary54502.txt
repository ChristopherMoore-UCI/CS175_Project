iReport: Share your photos, video, stories with CNN More than 30 homes were destroyed in a mudslide Wednesday in metropolitan Rio, Agencia Brasil said. A record 11.3 inches (287 millimeters) of rain fell in Rio within 24 hours Tuesday, Mayor Eduardo Paes said, according to the news service. At least 161 people have been injured, the Rio de Janeiro state government said Thursday on its Web site. Brazil's minister of cities, Marcio Fortes, said that housing and sanitation problems are not new for Rio. Thousands of people have been left homeless, said the government-run Agencia Brasil news service.
