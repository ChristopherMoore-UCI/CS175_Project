San Juan Island is between the U.S. mainland and Vancouver Island, British Columbia. -- Richard Bach, author of the 1970s classic "Jonathan Livingston Seagull," was seriously injured in a small plane crash on San Juan Island, Washington, a dispatcher said Saturday. Best known for "Jonathan Livingston Seagull," Bach wrote several other books, including "Illusions: The Adventures of a Reluctant Messiah" and "A Gift of Wings. Bach, who lives on nearby Orcas Island, flies frequently, the dispatcher said. The author was flown to Harborview Medical Center in Seattle, where he was listed in serious condition.
