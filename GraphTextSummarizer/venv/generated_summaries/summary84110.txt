Like many people with dementia, they simply see a doorway and want to walk through it. Crippled by the prospect of putting their own parents into extended care facilities, two Dutch nurses decided to conduct an experiment: build a village for people with dementia and introduce a new level of humanity to the last few years of their lives. When you consider the developed world, the number of people living with dementia is expected to double by 2030 and triple by 2050. As things stand now, people with dementia are largely ignored. An entire village dedicated to caring for people with dementia.
