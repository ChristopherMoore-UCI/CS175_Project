Gila Wilderness, New Mexico Mesas, deep canyons, high mountains, sheer cliffs, woodlands and grasslands stretch across various areas of New Mexico's 558,000-acre Gila Wilderness. Boundary Waters Canoe Wilderness, Minnesota Sharing its northern border with Canada's Quetico Provincial Park, Minnesota's Boundary Waters Canoe Wilderness is comprised of more than 812,000 acres of preserved land, with nearly 1,200 lakes, ranging in size from 10 to 10,000 acres, and hundreds of miles of streams. Hiking, camping, backpacking and fishing are allowed in designated wilderness areas, as long as visitors follow a few rules designed to preserve the environment.
