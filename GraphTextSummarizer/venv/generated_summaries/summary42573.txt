But it's important to remember that despite the uncertainties, decision makers have to make decisions, and being under-prepared for a storm that turns out to be worse than the forecast can lead to serious loss of life and property. Good weather forecasts and well-informed, proactive emergency preparation and management save lives and property. The right way to communicate uncertain information, really, is through probabilities.
