They "tend to be the best places to photograph," says Gary Arndt, a blogger and photographer who sold his house in 2007 and has been roaming the globe ever since. This week, Arndt will surpass mile 11,000 of this summer's North American odyssey. "You want to try to take your photos within say, two hours of sunrise or sunset," Arndt said. Arndt has picked more than 20 of the most photogenic spots he's visited to highlight on Pinterest. Visually rich and undeniably gorgeous, these American destinations are ripe for photographers of all skill levels.
