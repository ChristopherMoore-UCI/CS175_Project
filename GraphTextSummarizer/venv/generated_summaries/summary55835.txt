When the original iPhone launched, it didn't have apps, it had web apps -- free programs that bring the best of the web to your phone. But things are changing, and phones and tablets due out in the second half of 2013 will pack a number of features designed to give them enough power to become your home entertainment hub. Ford SYNC AppLink allows drivers to control smartphone apps from the driver's seat, using voice control. Jump forward five years and Mozilla, the organization behind the Firefox browser is trying to bring back free web based apps with a mobile operating system completely built on HTML 5. Streaming music in cars: Ford Spotify AppLink Ford has teamed up with Spotify to bring the music-streaming service to its Ford Sync AppLink cars in Europe, starting with the EcoSport later in the year.
