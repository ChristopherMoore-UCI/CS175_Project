Financial site MoneyRates.com released a survey Thursday in which 25% respondents called cable companies the worst for customer service. In terms of services, this could be a win for Time Warner Cable customers. So Time Warner Cable subscribers would presumably have new options on that front as well. As noted above, there are activists dedicated to fighting big media mergers on the argument that relying on a small handful of giant companies kills competition and drives up prices in any market. (Time Warner Cable was spun off from Time Warner in 2009 and no longer has any connection to the owner of CNN, HBO and Warner Bros.)
