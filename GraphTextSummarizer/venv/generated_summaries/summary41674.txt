Ending attacks on students, teachers and schools requires action at both the national and international levels. But in war zones around the world, from Afghanistan to Yemen, students, teachers and schools are regularly coming under attack. Armed groups target schools and teachers as symbols of the state. The gruesome gunning-down of 15-year-old Malala Yousufzai spotlighted the fate of children in Pakistan, one of the world's most dangerous places to go to school. Let us work to ensure that it does not take more Malalas for the world to react and give attacks on schools the attention it deserves.
