Facebook has given CNN access to demographic data that tells us how many people have mentioned a topic along with age, gender and location information. -- People talk about all kinds of things on social media: Their day-to-day lives, articles generating a lot of buzz and the things they ate for breakfast. You might see that more women are talking about a particular topic than men, or that younger age groups are mentioning one news event versus another. For example, we recently learned that many of the Facebook users talking about Iran were young people, especially internationally.
