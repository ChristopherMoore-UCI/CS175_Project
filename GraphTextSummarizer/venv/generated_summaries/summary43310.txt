From pre-teens to men nearing 50, bodybuilders sweat it out at one of the most popular training spots, Akhada, which is a Hindi word that means wrestling arena. "There are few modern gyms in the village, but most men prefer the traditional style of working out," Tanwar says. The musclemen of Asola-Fatehpur Beri are capitalizing on their brawn by working as bouncers in New Delhi's clubs and bars. More than 300 musclemen work as bouncers in New Delhi's clubs and bars now. For generations, men have held two-hour workouts every morning and evening in this countryside community on the southern rim of Delhi.
