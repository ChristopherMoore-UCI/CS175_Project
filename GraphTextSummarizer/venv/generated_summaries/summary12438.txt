-- One person was killed and four police officers were wounded Saturday when police in Brazil traded fire with gunmen who fled into a luxury hotel and took people hostage, police said. Police identified the deceased as 41-year-old Adriana Duarte de Oliveira dos Santos, who was wanted in connection with drug dealing. An unknown number of gunmen fled into the hotel, where they took people hostage. The incident occurred around the Intercontinental Hotel in Rio de Janeiro, where gunmen kept 35 people hostage. Ten people have been arrested, and the hostages were freed, police said.
