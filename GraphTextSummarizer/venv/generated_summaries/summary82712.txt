Beloved teacher slain by 'nice kid' As news of the shooting was reported on Twitter, students and former students tweeted about Mr. Landsberry, who taught eighth-grade math. Landsberry, who joined the Marines after he graduated from high school in Reno in 1986, had an associate's degree in law enforcement and a bachelor's degree in education, according to a statement from the Guard. A Facebook memorial page posted in Landsberry's honor had more than 16,100 likes on Tuesday afternoon. In addition to being a math teacher he also coached basketball, cross country, track  and volleyball, as well as girls soccer at Sparks High School, the Guard said.
