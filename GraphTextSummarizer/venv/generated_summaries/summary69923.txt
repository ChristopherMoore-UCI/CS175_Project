Richard Lewis holds the Guinness Book of World Records' title of "Most Avid Golfer" after breaking the mark of most rounds played in a year when he finished his 587th on December 19. -- An American amateur golfer is poised to extend an incredible world record on December 26 as he aims to complete his 600th round of 2010. After his effort on December 26, Lewis will continue to play until the end of this year, with his final total likely to stand at 611. Lewis told the PGA Tour website that he has missed only 19 days this year -- all due to bad weather. Four Seasons club manager Rob Cowan has hailed the veteran golfer's efforts.
