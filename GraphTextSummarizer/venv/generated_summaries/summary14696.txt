TOKYO, Japan -- Three-time grand prix winner Norifumi Abe of Japan has been killed in a street crash when his 500cc scooter collided with a truck making an illegal U-turn, police have revealed on Monday. In 1994, while racing in his home championship, Abe had a chance to race at the 1994 Japanese Grand Prix as a 'wild card'. Abe won two grand prix in Japan and another in Brazil during the 1990s. His impressed Kenny Roberts' Yamaha team sufficiently to be offered two more rides in the 500cc championship that year.
