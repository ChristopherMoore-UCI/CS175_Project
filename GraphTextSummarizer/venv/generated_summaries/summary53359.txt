-- Fans of the character Easy Rawlins don't want to hear it, but author Walter Mosley says he has officially moved on. Author Walter Mosley found fame with his Easy Rawlins mysteries and has debuted a new series. The book features an ex-boxer named Leonid Trotter McGill, the latest colorfully named Mosley character. Everybody reads crime fiction and they read it to find out about different worlds. Three of his previous books involved ex-convict Socrates Fortlow, and another trio concerned bookseller Paris Minton.
