FDA: Problems found at another facility of pharmacy's sister company "Let me be clear, Mr. Cadden," Stearns said. Barry Cadden, owner and director of the New England Compounding Center, was questioned by Rep. Cliff Stearns, R-Florida, chairman of the House Energy and Commerce Committee's Subcommittee on Oversight and Investigations. Stearns: "Will you invoke your Fifth Amendment rights in response to all questions today?" Cadden: "Yes." Stearns then excused Cadden and advised him the subcommittee may recall him. Meanwhile, federal authorities have launched a criminal investigation into the practices at the Framingham-based company, Massachusetts Gov. Deval Patrick has said. Cadden was subpoenaed to appear before the body, his spokesman said.
