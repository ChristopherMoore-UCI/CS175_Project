The idea for sonic fabric was actually born long ago; as a child, Santoro raced sailboats using strands of cassette tape as 'tell-tales' to indicate the direction of the wind. The spools of cassette tape used in the construction of sonic fabric maintain their magnetism, and can produce sound if amplified by the head of a tape player. In the future, Santoro hopes to collaborate with artists and performers to see sonic fabric to further explore the intersection between fashion and music. " CNN caught up with Santoro for a Q&A.
