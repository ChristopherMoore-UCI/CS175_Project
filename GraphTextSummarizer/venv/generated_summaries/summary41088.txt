Internet businesses make it easier for free web series like "Annoying Orange" to make an actual profit. Launched earlier this year by animator Daneboe of GagFilms, "Annoying Orange" has amassed a hefty 137 million views. "Annoying Orange" holds several top 20 spots on YouTube, including among the most-subscribed channels of all time. What does the unlikely success of "Annoying Orange," say about the web, and entertainment in general? It may be pennies on each visit, but it becomes serious dough when you're talking millions of visits.
