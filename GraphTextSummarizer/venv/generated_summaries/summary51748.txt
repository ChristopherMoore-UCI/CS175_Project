(EW.com)  -- "Sure, NBC can launch a reality show," competitors pointed out last week after "The Voice" debuted huge, "but then what happens? That's up 10 percent from last week's premiere, which broke NBC records. The big question now is whether "The Voice" will continue its winning streak when the coaches ditch the swivel chairs and start the "battle rounds" next week.
