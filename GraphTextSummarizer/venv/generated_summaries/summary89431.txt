Mega churches are extra-large churches that can accommodate upwards of 15,000 people and are common among members of the evangelical Christian faith. -- Mega churches across the United States are becoming increasingly popular which is not only bringing thousands of worshippers together, but also billions of dollars in profit. From self-help books to CDs and DVDs, mega churches are becoming big money makers for the pastors and ministries they are a part of. The Lakewood Church which Osteen is in charge of has a yearly budget of more than $80 million, but church officials deny that it's about money. Mega church worshippers tend to not only be younger, but also more diverse.
