Delwar Hossain, the owner and managing director of Tazreen Fashions, and his wife, Mahmuda Akther, the chairwoman, surrendered Sunday and were ordered to jail by Metropolitan Magistrate Mohammad Tajul Islam. In December, a Bangladeshi criminal investigation found 13 people guilty of gross negligence of safety measures. -- The owner of a Bangladeshi apparel factory where 112 people died in a fire in November 2012 has been arrested and sent to jail. Ready-made garments make up 80% of Bangladesh's $24 billion in annual exports. There were arrest warrants issued for six people, including Hossain and Akther.
