Tough and tenacious on the rugby field, Van der Westhuizen is having to apply those skills in his fight to stay alive and help find a cure for motor neurone disease. Francois Pienaar, who captained South Africa to success in 1995, described Van der Westhuizen as "a phenomenal athlete." Van der Westhuizen was diagnosed with motor neurone disease, also known as ALS, in 2011. It is a huge step for Van der Westhuizen given it's not common practice in his native South Africa.
