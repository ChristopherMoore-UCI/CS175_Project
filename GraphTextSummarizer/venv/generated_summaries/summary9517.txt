The margin for maneuver in a sovereign state in Europe is extremely limited by law as much as by economic circumstances. This rise of right-wing populism affecting mostly the young, which one witnesses from Hungary to France, is of course the direct consequence of the economic, social -- if not ethical -- crisis that besets Europe once again. What would a Hollande victory mean for France and for Europe? As for Europe, Hollande in power in France would merely be an accelerating factor in the slow evolution of the EU away from a strict austerity policy, which the Germans themselves have started to question. His lack of charisma has not been a handicap, so great was the rejection of incumbent President Nicolas Sarkozy.
