Mark Potok of the Southern Poverty Law Center said that increasingly virulent hate speech emerging from anti-immigrant groups has a role in recent attacks on immigrants. Julio Serrano, 39, remains in critical condition after he was attacked Saturday, police said. "This is yet another example of America's anti-immigrant propaganda to demonize immigrants," Potok said. Peekskill Police Chief Eugene Tumolo said it is possible that Serrano was beaten "because of the fact he is a Latino male, and a lot of these people are immigrants.
