Seeing Pistorius at the Olympic and Paralympic Games was amazing and did something very special to raise awareness of athletes with physical differences. But, whatever happened, there is a lesson: Too often in life, we put people on pedestals. We want to be reminded that ordinary people are capable of extraordinary things. The reality is that, despite their seemingly superhuman performances, they are just people with their own sets of challenges. The first time I saw Oscar Pistorius run, I was captivated.
