Chris Wright was playing professional basketball in Turkey last year, working toward achieving his lifelong dream of playing in the NBA, when something strange happened at the end of practice. The Mavericks did not re-sign Wright when the 10-day contract expired. With the stroke of a pen, Wright became the first person with MS to play in the NBA. Asked what he would tell other patients with MS, Wright didn't hesitate: "Don't be afraid to step out and do what you want to do," he said.
