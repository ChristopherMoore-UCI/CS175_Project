Don't... automatically dismiss the food I might be a bit biased here because I find traditional Irish fare incredibly satisfying: hearty shepherd's pie, fat pink Irish salmon, mussels, chips and potato boxty -- what's not to like? Do... try Irish classics with a twist For souped-up takes on homestyle Irish classics like fish pie and bangers & mash, try The Farm or The Winding Stair. While you might get some sessions in touristy Temple Bar or at The Duke, you'll have better luck finding spontaneous traditional Irish music out on the West Coast or in the countryside.
