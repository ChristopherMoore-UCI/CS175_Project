In the case of Gaza, a seven year air and sea blockage imposed by Israel since Hamas came to power has isolated its economy. But the tunnels have also been used by Hamas to bring in weapons and launch attacks on Israel. As Gaza's official economy has contracted, the shadow economy -- also known as the tunnel economy -- blossomed. Underground tunnels were built by Gazans to circumvent the blockade allowing goods to be illegally smuggled from Egypt. JD: Grinding poverty and the desire by Hamas to strike at Israel.
