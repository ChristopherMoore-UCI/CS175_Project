Amin Maalouf Meet Amin Maalouf, a renowned Lebanese-born French author whose romantic tales of Arab mythology have enlightened international readers -- both in the east and west -- for decades. Today, the Caracalla Theater has produced internationally-acclaimed performances in major cities and venues around the world, from the U.S. to China. Take a tour of this vibrant neighborhood with filmmaker and intellectual Nigol Bezijian. -- This month, Inside the Middle East explores the struggle to preserve traditional Arab culture in a rapidly changing world. Born in Syria, raised in Lebanon, and educated in the United States, Bezijian has first-hand experience of this cultural melting pot.
