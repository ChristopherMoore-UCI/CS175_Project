"God said, 'I need somebody strong enough to clear trees and heave bales, yet gentle enough to yean lambs and wean pigs and tend to pink combed pullets; who will stop his mower for an hour to splint the broken leg of a meadowlark.'" The thought of it, too, being taken from him made Mr. Harvey lie awake at night and pray. He was Mr. Harvey, and for a very long time, he was as big a name as there was in the world of radio. The voice was that of Paul Harvey, taken directly from a recording of a speech he made in 1978 to the Future Farmers of America.
