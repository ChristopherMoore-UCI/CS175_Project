A small, private ceremony was held at the actor's home in the islands in the West Indies on Saturday, according to publicists Rogers and Cowan. -- Actor Bruce Willis married model-actress Emma Heming over the weekend in the Turks and Caicos Islands, the actor's publicists announced. Their mother and Willis' first wife, actress Demi Moore and her husband, actor Ashton Kutcher, also attended the ceremony. It is the second marriage for actor Bruce Willis, 54, and the first for model-actress Emma Heming, 30. At the wedding were Willis' daughters, Rumer, 20; Scout, 17; and Tallulah Belle, 14.
