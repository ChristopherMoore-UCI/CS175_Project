After a few days of lounging at Basunti, it's easy to forget you're in vibrant India. The Basunti retreat in Himachal Pradesh, north India, is set in a wildlife reserve in the Kangra Valley and offers immersion into one of the most spiritual regions of India. Though yoga retreats are on offer year-round, one of the best times to go to Basunti is October, when Bridget Woods Kramer is visiting. Kramer says the need to stand tall and strong in yoga translates into everyday life: "It's a reminder to stand tall in the face of fear. Yoga retreats are held throughout the year, hosted by teachers from around the world who specialize in a variety of styles.
