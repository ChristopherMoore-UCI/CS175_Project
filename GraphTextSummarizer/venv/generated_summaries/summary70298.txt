'Heroic' mission rescues desperate Yazidis from ISIS Their plight, like so many, began more than a week ago when they fled into the surrounding mountains when ISIS fighters stormed the town of Sinjar. When ISIS advanced on Sinjar, an ancestral home for many Yazidis, the Hamid family was forced to flee. The family didn't make it far in their car before they ran into ISIS fighters, who were shooting at fleeing Yazidis on a bridge. U.S. President Barack Obama ordered targeted airstrikes last week, partly to protect the Yazidis and others fleeing ISIS. ISIS fighters have vowed to kill the Yazidis, calling them "devil worshipers.
