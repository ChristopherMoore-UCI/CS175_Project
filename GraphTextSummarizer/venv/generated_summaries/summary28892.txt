Moran approached an officer who was handling a traffic stop in the church's parking lot on Wednesday morning, police said. Jose Moran was arrested early Wednesday morning after interfering with the duties of a public servant in the parking lot of a Webster, Texas, building that is being remodeled for the Iglesias Profetica Peniel Church, Webster police said in a written statement. Moran identified himself as the church's pastor and began yelling at the officer, police said. The officer told Moran to leave several times, but Moran did not, police said. But Moran pushed the officer and ran into the church building, police said.
