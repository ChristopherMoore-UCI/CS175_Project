Serie A kicks off new season Two matches were played on the opening day of Serie A in Italy on Saturday. Bayern took the lead in the 10th minute courtesy of Robert Lewandowski -- scoring his first goal for his new club since joining from Borussia Dortmund in the summer -- before Schalke picked up their first point of the new season with a 62nd minute equalizer from Benedikt Howedes. Atletico win in Spain In La Liga, reigning champions Atletico Madrid picked up their first win of the new season beating new boys Eibar 2-1. Jose Mourinho's side continued their impressive early season form with a 6-3 win against Everton in the English Premier League on Saturday. Despite fielding new signing Angel Di Maria for the first time, United were unable to break down the defenses of newly promoted Burnley in a game that ended goalless.
