"If you look at Vietnam or Cambodia or Myanmar, they don't suffer from the up-ended triangle that we all suffer from in the West of too many old people and too few young people," said Napoleon Biggs, a Hong Kong-based digital media specialist. He said groups like Rocket Internet from Germany were very good at identifying "clones," or emerging market copies of internet ideas that have originated elsewhere. One recurring feature of emerging market startups is that they are often aimed at solving specific problems in a country. Venture capitalists and startup funds are now circling South East Asia looking for ideas to invest in.
