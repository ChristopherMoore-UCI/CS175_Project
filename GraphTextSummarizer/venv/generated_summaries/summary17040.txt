Now a senior fellow on health care for the nonpartisan watchdog group Center for Media and Democracy, Potter writes a blog on health care reform. Wendell Potter once was a vice president in the public relations department for insurance giant Cigna. In his testimony and during an interview with CNN, Potter described how underwriters at his former company would drive small businesses with expensive insurance claims to dump their Cigna policies. At the event, Potter took pictures of doctors offering free health care to the uninsured.
