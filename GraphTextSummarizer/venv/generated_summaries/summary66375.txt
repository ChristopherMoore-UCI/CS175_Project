His family receives a letter from Publishers Clearing House, addressed to "Mr. S. Sh*tgart," and believes it has just won $10 million -- though, to be sure the judges approve, he and his parents fill out everything and sign up for several magazines. The Gary Shteyngart who can find absurdist comedy in overseas revolutions, economic collapse and small electric appliances? Still, "Little Failure" is an attempt to revisit his life -- that of the Russian Jewish immigrant who made his way in the United States -- and get at the root of his woe. His parents, stifled in many ways, bickered. -- They called him "Scary Gary." Gary Shteyngart had a bong named Big Blue.
