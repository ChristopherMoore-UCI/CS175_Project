The operator had moved from the cab of his crane to its very tip to escape flames and smoke from a building fire that broke out below him Tuesday, and bystanders captured the rescue in a series of images posted to social media. David Agnew told CNN affiliate CTV of the crane operator. The Tuesday rescue was captured by television cameras and bystanders and posted to social media. The helicopter crew arrived to find smoke and flames everywhere and were concerned about the crane collapsing, Agnew told the network. The crane operator suffered only minor injuries, according to CTV.
