And finally -- to those of you, like me, who are parents of young boys -- teach him to seek out "the other": Other clubs and groups, other sources of information, other places to see, other kinds of people, other cultural values. There are some men who are natural monsters, but most monsters are made. Work up to atrocity step by step. Work up to altruism and moral courage step by step. The far more frightening possibility we must face is that such evil is not diabolically inhuman or beyond understanding.
