Another valuable initiative is the Rental Assistance Demonstration Program, which enables private developers to work with public housing authorities to preserve affordable housing. So while RAD offers promise to select cities such as Baltimore, San Francisco and Houston, hundreds more housing authorities from Los Angeles to Little Rock, Arkansas, to Lackawanna, New York, are waiting for Congress to lift the cap so that desperately needed private capital can flow to aging public housing. In cities and towns across America, there's rising demand for affordable housing while the stock of low-cost homes evaporates. Affordable housing is key to sustaining successful communities. On May 1, the mayor is set to introduce his plan for how to address the accelerating demand for affordable housing.
