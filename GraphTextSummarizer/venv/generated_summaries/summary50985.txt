Families should be able to turn on the tap and have safe drinking water for their children, vacationers expect healthy rivers for fishing and swimming, and businesses need a steady supply of clean water to make products. These bodies of water may seem small, but they form part of the drinking water supplies for more than one third of Americans. Poll after poll shows that a strong majority of Americans value clean drinking water. -- Clean water is vital to every single American. I write now to add my voice to the chorus demanding clean water.
