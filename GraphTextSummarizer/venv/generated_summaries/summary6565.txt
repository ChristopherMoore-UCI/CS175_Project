According to Zamora and other dissidents, Soto was detained by police on Thursday and was beaten in public view in a park when he tried to resist arrest. The state-run, Spanish-language website www.cubadebate.cu repeated those claims in an article posted on Monday night titled "A press release from the Cuban government: Cuba denies the campaign about the death of Juan Wilfredo Soto Garcia." " Juan Wilfredo Soto died in a hospital on Sunday in the central city of Santa Clara. Fellow dissidents accused police of beating him and causing his death. " Soto was not well-known outside of Santa Clara, but according to Zamora, he was active within the dissident community there.
