"We take these matters seriously which is why we have initiated the recall and have cooperated with the government," Klein said in an e-mail to CNN. The products were sold in Arizona and New Mexico, said Mark Klein, spokesman for Cargill Inc., which owns Beef Packers, Inc. Cargill spokesman Klein said the company is cooperating with the government. Beef Packers Inc., based in Fresno, California, recalled 22,723 pounds of ground beef products produced on September 23, the U.S. Department of Agriculture's Food Safety and Inspection Service said in a statement. The agency's statement did not identify brand names.
