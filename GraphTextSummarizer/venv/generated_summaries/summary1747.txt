Watch out, McConnell: Cruz's 2015 plans If Mitch McConnell, the presumed new Senate majority leader, is ever trying to find Sen. Ted Cruz of Texas, he might just want to turn and look over his shoulder. Jonathan Martin of The New York Times chatted with Cruz on election night and came away with the distinct impression Cruz will be tracking every McConnell move. " Next year, Martin points out, McConnell might be wishing Cruz finds himself in Iowa more often than in Washington. There is one more round in the 2014 election, and Republicans are heavily favored: the Louisiana runoff for the U.S. Senate between Democratic Sen. Mary Landrieu and GOP Rep. Bill Cassidy. Now, as McConnell prepares to rise to majority leader, the 2016 lineup suggests he might want to savor every minute.
