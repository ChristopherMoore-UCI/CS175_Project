"Bugaboo's Picture Perfect," aka Swagger, an English sheepdog, is named Reserve Best in Show, a runner-up award reintroduced for the 2013 show after not having been awarded since 1925. The dogs compete for Best in Breed, then move up to Best in Group, then compete for the grand prize of Best in Show. The biggest dog to win Best in Show is Seward's Blackbeard, a 155-pound Newfoundland. The smallest dog to win Best in Show is Great Elms Prince Charming II, a 4.5-pound Pomeranian. 2013 - "GCH Banana Joe V Tani Kazari," aka "Banana Joe" or Joey, becomes the first affenpinscher to win Best in Show.
