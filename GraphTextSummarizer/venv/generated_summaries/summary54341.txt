" In recent years, states across the country have struggled to maintain a supply of lethal injection drugs as manufacturers either stopped producing the drugs or barred their use in executions. The European manufacturers of pentobarbital, an anesthetic, explicitly banned U.S. prisons from using its drug in executions. If scheduled executions this Wednesday and the next go forward, the state will have completely exhausted its supply of pentobarbital. The state leads the nation in the numbers of executions. Other states have sought out substitutes or gone with one drug instead of the traditional three-drug cocktail.
