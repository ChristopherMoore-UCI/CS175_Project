Powerball jackpot winners in New Jersey claim prize Bethea said the ticket was sold Wednesday afternoon at a Murphy gas station in that 5.8-square-mile town of 17,800. South Carolina lottery rules lets winners remain anonymous if they want. Bethea told reporters outside the station Thursday that she encourages the winner or winners to sign the back of the ticket, put it in a secure location and seek counsel from an accountant, lawyer or financial planner. No one has yet come forward to claim the prize, said the director of the South Carolina Lottery, Paula Harper Bethea, on Thursday morning. The winner or winners must claim the prize within 180 days, Bethea said.
