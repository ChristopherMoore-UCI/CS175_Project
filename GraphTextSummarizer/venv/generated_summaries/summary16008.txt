Doctors at Atlanta's Northside Hospital delivered the boys Thursday morning, then transferred them to Children's Healthcare of Atlanta at Egleston, parents Michael and Robin Hamby wrote on their Facebook page, where they posted frequent updates on their boys. Said Robin, "God gave us extra time with them that some parents don't get to have, because a lot of babies are stillborn that are conjoined twins, and we had almost two days." Robin called the boys "beautiful" and had said late Thursday they were even offering glimpses of their different personalities. Eli and Asa Hamby shared a torso, heart, arms and legs.
