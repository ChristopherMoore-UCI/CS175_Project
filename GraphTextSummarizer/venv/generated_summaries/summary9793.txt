The knowledge that best-selling artists rank highly on video sites may be of little utility to aspiring YouTube stars -- except to note that dancing videos and "lip dubs" have proved popular. The cute factor is perhaps the most prevalent trend among popular YouTube videos. --  YouTube this week announced the biggest viral video sensations of 2009, with Scottish singer Susan Boyle topping the list. Had YouTube not created a second list to highlight top music videos on the site, these would have dominated the rankings for 2009. Value in views Topping the YouTube charts brings significant value -- Boyle's clip had more than 120 million views on the site.
