The incident came as Sarkozy visited the town of Brax in southwest France. The suspect, who is 32 and works at a local music school, was taken into custody and questioned by police who were already present for security reasons, a police spokesman said. The president was due to attend a meeting of local mayors during his visit to the Lot-et-Garonne region, according to local media reports.
