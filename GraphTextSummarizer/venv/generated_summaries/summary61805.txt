In Focus -- Tunisia tourism Tunisia in the process of defining its post-revolution road map. Showtimes Marketplace Middle East airs weekly at the following times (all GMT):   Fridays: 0845, 1645,  Saturdays: 0615,  Sundays: 0515, 1745,  Mondays: 0045 Part of the plan aims to instigate domestic growth by repositioning the North African country in the global market. MME sits down with Mehdi Houas, Tunisia's Minister of Culture and Tourism, to discuss his country's plans to reposition itself as a regional hub for business. Unlike its neighbors to the East and West however, Tunisia does not sit upon vast oil and gas reserves to aid in the post-revolutionary process.
