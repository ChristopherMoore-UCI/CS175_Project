Vin Diesel has been paying tribute to his friend Paul Walker as fans eagerly await the release of "Furious 7." " Diesel has been honoring Walker a lot lately and recently had some special words at an advance screening of "Furious 7." Diesel told "Today's" Natalie Morales that he and girlfriend Paloma Jimenez named their baby girl Pauline. The film was finished using previous footage and stand-ins including Walker's two brothers. The producers have said they don't plan on killing Walker's character off in the new movie.
