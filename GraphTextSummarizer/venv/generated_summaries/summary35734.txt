A tiny step forward for spinal cord injuries Clark attributes his progress to intense physical therapy and clinical studies, as well as to the stem cell therapy. " Clark is seeking more cutting-edge SCI studies and continues to spread the word as a national ambassador for the Christopher and Dana Reeve Foundation. So why couldn't I push for more?" Clark completes five hours of physical therapy a week. From stem cell surgery in Beijing to different clinical trials, Clark was fiercely determined to move again. Clark's recovery is documented in his book "Walking Papers."
