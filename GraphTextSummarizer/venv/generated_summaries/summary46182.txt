Microsoft received much Twitter flak after a small PR account for its Xbox system encouraged followers to remember recently deceased musician Amy Winehouse by purchasing her last album on the company's entertainment marketplace, Zune. Apple posted an image of Winehouse on the front of the iTunes store with the caption "remembering Amy Winehouse." Sales of "Back to Black" increased 37 times immediately after Winehouse's death. "Apologies to everyone if our earlier Amy Winehouse 'download' tweet seemed purely commercially motivated," it said. Our thoughts are with Amy's family and friends at this very sad time.
