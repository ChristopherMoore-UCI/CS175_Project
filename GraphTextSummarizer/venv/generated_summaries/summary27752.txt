-- The son of Green Bay Packers offensive coordinator Joe Philbin died from "fresh water drowning" and there was no evidence of foul play, Oshkosh, Wisconsin, police said Wednesday. The body of Michael Philbin, 21, was pulled from a river Monday, a day after he went missing while in Oshkosh to visit friends, police said. Michael Philbin was the second-eldest of six children of Joe Philbin and his wife, Diane, according to the Packers' website. Philbin thanked the agencies that assisted in the search and expressed gratitude for "the prayers and the support of our family, friends and the Packers family. The body was found Monday in the Fox River after Oshkosh police put out an alert asking citizens for help locate Philbin, who lived about 20 miles away in Ripon.
