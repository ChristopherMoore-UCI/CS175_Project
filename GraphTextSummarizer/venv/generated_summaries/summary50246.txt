To make the good news even better, tax filing has gotten rather simple for most people, with various software and service providers offering to do the dreaded paperwork for free. Service providers kindly offer to help out the masses of befuddled Americans. The once dreaded Tax Day has become a happy spending spree for most Americans. In brief, the U.S. income tax system is increasingly a wage tax, with limited taxes on capital (what the rich have) and limited deductions for most of us. Just as lottery winners notoriously go on impulsive spending sprees, the "found money" of tax returns can finance many nice purchases.
