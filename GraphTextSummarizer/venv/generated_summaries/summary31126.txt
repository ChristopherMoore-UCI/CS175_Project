While writing her "Lost And Abandoned Children" column for New Vision, one of Uganda's leading newspapers, Kalibala came into contact with several forgotten children, some of whom had been born into extreme poverty or suffered from abuse. Through the success of her features, Kalibala was able to rapidly increase awareness of the ongoing plight faced by many local children. A mother to many Today Kalibala has become known as a mother to many of these motherless children -- and in turn she considers them her own. In 2010, Ugandan journalist Gladys Kalibala embarked on a mission to bring attention to her country's lost and abandoned children. Nonetheless, Kalibala was determined to make a name for herself and enrolled herself in a course in Uganda.
