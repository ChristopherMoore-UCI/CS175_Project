The game's creators are partnering with Zynga, maker of such popular social games as "Farmville," "Cityville" and "Words With Friends," to produce Zynga Slingo. -- "Slingo," a leader in online games for 17 years, is making its way to Facebook with a redesigned version of the popular bingo/slot-machine game. "Our partnership with Zynga will bring the 'Slingo' brand to a whole new audience in a fresh, new way," said Rich Roberts, CEO of Slingo. " 'Slingo' is one of the earliest and most cherished games of the Internet," said Rich Sawel, product manager for Zynga Slingo. Zynga launched 12 new games in 2011 -- four titles on Web-based platforms and eight others on mobile platforms.
