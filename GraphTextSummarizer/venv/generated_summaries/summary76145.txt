In 1960, an unprecedented number of African nations won independence from their colonial masters, bringing to an end decades of rule by European powers. From those who lived through the handover of power, to young Africans who have only known life in a free state, click on the flags to see what some of them had to say. Reporting by Stephanie Busari, Tim Lister, Kiesha Porter, Mark Tutton and Grace Wong As part of CNN's special coverage of this landmark occasion, we asked Africans from these countries to share their thoughts and opinions about their homelands. Tell us your story and share your photos and videos on CNN iReport.
