Coach Paul Tolbert says many members of Liberia's amputee football team had lost the will to live. The fighting brought rape and mutilation, children were abducted and forced to become fighters and untold numbers had to flee their homes. Since 2003, peace has returned and while efforts are underway to rebuild the country the emotional and physical scars of war remain. They're also looking for a sense of belonging and brotherhood among those who know what it's like to live with the horrors of war and to struggle just to survive. Take the example of the guy who won Most Valuable Player in the recent African Cup of Nations.
