Viral star Sophia Grace Brownlee came to fame in 2011 after she and her cousin Rosie McClelland covered Nicki Minaj's "Super Bass" in an online video. The 11-year-old looks like she took some cues from her idol Minaj (to whom Ellen famously introduced Sophia Grace and Rosie during their first appearance on her talk show) on the attitude one needs in order to carry off being a raptress. Now, Sophia Grace has released a video for her single "Best Friends." There's plenty of preteen shopping, makeup and modeling sessions as Sophia Grace jams with her posse of "best friends. The only question we have is, where is Rosie?
