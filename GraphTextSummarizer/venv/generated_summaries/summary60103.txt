Paul Giamatti in talks to play Rhino in 'Spider-Man' sequel It's unclear exactly how big a role Cooper will play in the sequel. He'll play Norman Osborn, the father of Peter's best friend Harry who is destined to become Spider-verse uber-baddy the Green Goblin.
