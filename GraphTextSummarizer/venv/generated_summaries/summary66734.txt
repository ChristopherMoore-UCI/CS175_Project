Senior airline executives have also voiced encouragement for an easing of ownership restrictions. Steve Ridgway, CEO of Virgin Atlantic Airways expresses his frustration that airlines are the only companies that cannot be owned by foreign buyers in the U.S. On Tuesday, United States surprised EU negotiators by proposing to drop investment restrictions for airlines for more than 60 countries. Such a move could be crucial for airlines that have already invested hundreds of millions of dollars in preparation for reduced restrictions.
