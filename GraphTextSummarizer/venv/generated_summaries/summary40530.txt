-- Italian drug lord Roberto Pannunzi, one of Europe's most wanted traffickers, was captured in a shopping center north of Bogota, Colombia police said Saturday. He was known as the "Pablo Escobar of Italy," Colombian defense ministry said in a twitter post. He allegedly headed a criminal organization in southern Italy. The Colombian police and Drug Enforcement Agency collaborated to capture him. Pannunzi first presented a fake Venezuelan ID and identified himself as Silvano Martino.
