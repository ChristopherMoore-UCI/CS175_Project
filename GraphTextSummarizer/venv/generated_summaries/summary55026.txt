Crude oil prices have jumped, triggering higher prices at the pump, said publisher Trilby Lundberg. So if crude oil prices stay approximately where they are, we will see a few more cents at the pump -- maybe 5 or 6 cents (per gallon) in the coming days. -- After plummeting for three months, gas prices are working their way back up, according to a survey published Sunday. The Lundberg Survey tabulates prices at thousands of gas stations nationwide. The highest average found in the latest survey was in New York's Long Island, at $3.75 per gallon.
