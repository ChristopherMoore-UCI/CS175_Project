According to an official communique published in state media, Construction Minister Fidel Figueroa de la Paz was removed "for mistakes made in his job." Ramiro Valdes, a historic leader of the Revolution and vice president of the Council of Ministers, was removed from his post as communications minister. -- Cuba announced a Cabinet reshuffle Friday, sacking the construction minister for undisclosed "errors" and removing the communications minister in order to give him broader oversight over various ministries. General Medardo Diaz Toledo will be the new communications minister and Rene Mesa Villafana will assume the head of the Construction Ministry. President Raul Castro has replaced a number of ministers since officially being elected in 2008.
