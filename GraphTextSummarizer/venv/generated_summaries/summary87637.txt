Another 40 to 50 people were injured when one train traveling from Cairo to Asyut crashed into a train en route from Giza to Fayyoum, Deputy to the Health Ministry Mohammed Sarhan told state-run Nile News TV. The collision occurred in Kafr Girza, a village in Al-Ayyat district of October 6th province, Nile News TV reported. People were still trapped beneath the wreckage late Saturday, reported Nile News TV.
