During that research, I found in the lives of other people with Asperger's threads of similarity that led to the diagnosis. ... The one thing people seem to know about Asperger's, if they know anything at all, is the geek factor. It is true that we can be very literal, so we often miss the humor in everyday banter, but we can and do enjoy even subtle humor. I searched for years for answers and found none, until an assignment at work required me to research autism. A misconception is that Aspies do not have a sense of humor.
