Global economic recovery feeds growing inequality Opinion: Will global elite listen to Pope of poor? The same should be said for Davos and the issue of inequality. In its latest Global Risks report WEF says inequality is now the greatest threat to global stability. It's simple economics: Global stability equals global growth equals profits. There is a reason why Pope Francis sent his Cardinal to Davos, and who gave us the quote of the day when he said we must ensure "humanity is served by wealth and not ruled by it."
