Besse Cooper gained the distinction of being oldest living human from Guinness World Records in January 2011. When asked for her secret to a long life, Cooper told the Guinness website earlier this year: "I mind my own business. " Besse Cooper was born in Sullivan County, Tennessee, on August 26, 1896.
