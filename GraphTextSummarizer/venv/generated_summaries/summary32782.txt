Miami Dolphins defensive end Randy Starks was arrested early Sunday, according to police. Officers said they saw the Freightliner truck in bumper-to-bumper traffic on South Beach's Ocean Drive with some 13 people inside, including a woman sitting on the lap of Starks, who was driving. It was not clear from the police report whether the Freightliner -- normally part of a tractor-trailer -- was connected to a trailer. Starks, 25, faces a charge of aggravated battery, according to the report.
