The transgender life: What to know, say and understand Still, activists agree there's a long way to go before transgender people stop facing discrimination or worse. Transgender people have long been misunderstood and persecuted -- as recently as 2012, the American Psychiatric Association classified them as having a mental "disorder." A recent report by the Human Rights Campaign found that transgender people in the United States are more likely to face discrimination from employers and the effects of unemployment and poverty.
