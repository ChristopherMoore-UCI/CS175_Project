He said he found humanitarian photo projects to be the most fulfilling, and he became especially intrigued by landfills even though they are "scary, dirty and kind of grotesque. Timothy Bouldry is a photographer based in New Hampshire. For the past seven years, Bouldry has traveled the world photographing landfills. He helps the people living there grow gardens fertilized with compost he makes with organic waste from local smoothie shops.
