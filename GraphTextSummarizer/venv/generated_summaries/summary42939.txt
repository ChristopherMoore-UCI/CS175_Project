A new president changes the tone Even if the numbers may be shifting in his favor, Mexico's new president hasn't been talking much about violence. It's unclear whether Mexico has turned a corner, Wilson said, but the fact that cities like Ciudad Juarez and Tijuana have seen violence drop gives some hope for the future.
