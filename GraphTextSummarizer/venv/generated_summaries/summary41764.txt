Bergen -- who was 84, according to her profile on IMDb.com -- died peacefully at her home in Southbury, Connecticut, on Saturday morning, publicist Judy Katz said in a statement. Bergen was nominated in 2008 for outstanding guest actress in "Desperate Housewives," playing the mother of Felicity Huffman's character, Lynette. She was nominated three more times, including for roles in two miniseries: "Winds of War" in 1983 and "War and Remembrance" in 1988-1989, both on ABC. She had notable roles in the films "Cape Fear" in 1962 and "The Caretakers" the following year. She was surrounded by her family, longtime manager and friends.
