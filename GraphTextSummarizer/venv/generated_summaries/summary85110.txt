Wimmer said his restaurant, which he has owned for 30 years, may be more resilient than lakefront businesses because he relies more on Wisconsin Dells locals. Business owners around Lake Delton say they hope to bounce back sooner, perhaps by next summer, but this summer may be a wash. Like many business owners around Lake Delton, Zowin is seeing canceled reservations from disenchanted visitors who will be spending their summers elsewhere. Lake Delton Village and Wisconsin Dells, the self-professed "waterpark capital of the world," are home to about 5,000 residents. Business owners say, some skeptically, that there are efforts afoot to refill the lake by mid-July.
