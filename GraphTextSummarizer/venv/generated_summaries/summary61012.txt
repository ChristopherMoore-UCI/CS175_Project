iReport: Share your photos, video, stories with CNN More than 30 homes were destroyed in a mudslide Wednesday in metropolitan Rio, Agencia Brasil said. A record 11.3 inches (287 millimeters) of rain fell in Rio within 24 hours Tuesday, Mayor Eduardo Paes said, according to the news service. Thousands of people have been left homeless, said the government-run Agencia Brasil news service. CNN affiliate TV Record showed firefighters, military personnel and other rescuers using heavy machinery to dig for buried residents. At least 161 people have been injured, the Rio de Janeiro state government said Thursday on its Web site.
