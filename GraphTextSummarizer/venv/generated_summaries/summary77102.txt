Fire officials initially said the three suffered serious injuries. The 1966 Piper PA-28-180 aircraft landed about 3:20 p.m near the East 233rd Street exit on the Major Deegan Expressway, authorities said. The three were were taken to St. Barnabas Hospital, fire department spokesman Frank Dwyer said.
