Pop singer Ricky Martin declared publicly this week what he avoided discussing for years: He is gay. His song "Livin' La Vida Loca" rose to the top of the music charts in 1999 and propelled Martin to stardom. "I am proud to say that I am a fortunate homosexual man," Martin wrote on his official Web site. "From the moment I wrote the first phrase I was sure the book was the tool that was going to help me free myself from things I was carrying within me for a long time," he wrote.
