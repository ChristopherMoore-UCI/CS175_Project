Kuhn, 46, says he, too, started out as an intern and it always struck him as "odd" how interns have a combination of anonymity and access. -- Interns -- between office duties, they fetch your coffee, pick up your dry cleaning, maybe even cover for you when you play hooky from the office. There has been a handful of lawsuits from interns against some big targets, including movie studios and media companies, over unpaid wages and overtime. Almost everyone trying to climb the corporate ladder has to start out at the bottom; some huge success stories started out as interns, including people like Oprah Winfrey, Steve Jobs and Sean Combs. A growing number of cities and states are passing laws to protect unpaid interns against sexual harassment and discrimination.
