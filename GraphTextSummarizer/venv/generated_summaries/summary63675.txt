Decision day for states on health insurance marketplaces States can decide to set up an exchange at any time, but the clock is ticking. While it varies from state to state, primary care physicians see on average a 73% pay bump according to the Kaiser Family Foundation. This is supposed to help you understand your benefits better and make health care spending more transparent. Some 50 million Americans still lack health insurance. Another advantage to the exchange may be a lower cost for health insurance, according to Jay Angoff.
