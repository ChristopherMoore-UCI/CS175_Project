Rosie Perez wasn't raised by her parents Her parents, Lydia Perez and Ismael Serrano, were both married to others when they met. Perez was mentally, physically and sexually abused for much of her childhood and diagnosed with PTSD Perez, along with other children at the group home where she was raised, were often subjected to the nuns' cruelty. Her father had a panic attack in the movie theater when he saw her nude scene in "Do the Right Thing" When Perez's first movie came out, her father invited everyone from Aguadilla, Puerto Rico -- including the family pastor -- to watch. Perez was discovered by "Soul Train" and Spike Lee while dancing in clubs The nun said something to her that became a turning point in Perez's life.
