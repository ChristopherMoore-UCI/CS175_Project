Robert Schwentke, the German director best known for Flightplan with Jodie Foster and the successful action-comedy "Red," has been selected to direct "Insurgent," the sequel to Summit's upcoming franchise-starter, "Divergent." Shailene Woodley stars as multi-talented Tris Prior in the adaptation of Veronica Roth's best-selling novel, which tells the story of a dystopian future where society is divided into five factions.
