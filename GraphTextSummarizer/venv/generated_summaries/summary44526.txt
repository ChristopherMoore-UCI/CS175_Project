Fortunately the options are greater than ever before, as exercise programs for cancer patients have become increasingly popular nationwide. Regular exercise can help fight the weight gain that often comes with cancer treatment, including breast cancer. "The risk of inactivity for cancer patients is so great that it's best to just get started with something," says Schmitz. In the past, doctors often told cancer patients to take it easy during treatment. Prostate cancer, cancers of the stomach and gut, and head and neck cancers can all lead to a significant decrease in muscle mass, as well as a loss of taste and an inability to process certain foods.
