The former Liverpool star struck with three minutes left of added time to deal a hammer blow to Iraq's title defense. Kewell, who now plays for Galatasaray in Turkey, headed a cross from Matt McKay past Iraqi goalkeeper Mohammed Kassid to settle a tense encounter. The Socceroos will now take on Uzbekistan for a place in Saturday's final in Qatar.
