The Taliban's promise of Islamic justice would be welcomed in farm fields on the outskirts of Islamabad. In farmlands just 15 miles (24 kilometers) from the center of Islamabad, Mohammed Daoud and his 15-year-old son Faisal eke out a living by cutting grass for their four water buffalo.
