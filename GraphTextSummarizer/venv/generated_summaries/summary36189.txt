The 42-year-old had a long and distinguished playing career, making 535 appearances in the English Premier League for clubs including Leeds United, Everton, Newcastle United and Bolton. On Tuesday the Premier League announced there would be a minute's applause before every weekend match in Speed's honor. Speed's death provoked shock and disbelief within the world of soccer after his body was found at his home on Sunday morning.
