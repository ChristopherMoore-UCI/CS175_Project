The goal of achieving "ippon" -- the fight-ending maximum score a judoka receives when a move that flips or throws an opponent onto their back is executed -- was a source of particular pleasure for the young Riner "Before judo I did many other sports," he explains. For giant judo champion Teddy Riner, however, the motivational music of choice is something altogether more sensitive. In judo, more so than other sports, these qualities are especially valuable. "The moments before the fight, I put my headphones on to enter into the zone," Riner told CNN's Human to Hero series.
