Some people are referring to it as a "lake burst," said Fawad Hussain, a U.N. relief coordinator for Sindh. "The monsoon rains may be over, but the floods are not," said Andro Shilakadze of UNICEF. Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon announced a few days ago that the United Nations is seeking another $1.594 billion for flood victims, bringing the total to more than $2 billion. The United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization has been trucking in animal feed and seeds to plant anew. Every day, the United Nations is seeing 20,000 to 30,000 more people who have just lost their homes to rising waters.
