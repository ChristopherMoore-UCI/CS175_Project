Negotiation is not about always meeting in the middle If negotiations always met in the middle, that would just encourage everyone to take extreme initial positions. Chicken is for people who value getting a marginal advantage in some deals, at the cost of losing other deals entirely. Parties in negotiation tend to say, "They are unreasonable," "They are negotiating in bad faith," or "They are crazy!" -- The problem with seeing negotiation as a game of chicken is that sometimes you get what you want, but most of the time everyone just gets plucked.
