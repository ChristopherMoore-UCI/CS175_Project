Contains: A theme park, shopping arcades and amusement arcades. -- It's a good summer for piers. Los Muertos Pier (Puerto Vallarta, Mexico) This 328-meter-long pier was designed by architect José de Jesús Torres Vega and opened in 2013 as part of a scheme to regenerate Puerto Vallarta. Scheveningen Pier (Scheveningen, Holland) Scheveningen pier stands out because of its unusual construction, which comprises four island-type sections and upper and lower areas. If you can't get to these icons of jetty-hood though, there are plenty of other spectacular piers to travel for.
