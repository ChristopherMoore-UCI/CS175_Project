Lightning struck Sunday afternoon at a Petroleos de Venezuela refinery in the northern city of Puerto La Cruz, causing an explosion and subsequent fire, company Vice President Asdrubal Chavez said. The state oil company describes the Puerto La Cruz refinery as one of its "most important centers of crude oil processing." In a Twitter post, Venezuelan President Nicolas Maduro said he was in close contact with teams on the ground. Officials said lightning struck a pond at the refinery around 3:15 p.m. Sunday. Authorities are evacuating residents who live near the refinery, Anzoategui state Gov. Aristobulo Isturiz said.
