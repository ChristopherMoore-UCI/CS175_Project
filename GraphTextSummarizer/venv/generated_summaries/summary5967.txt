Minnesota: Land of the Vikings (You got that right) The people of Minnesota are hardy folks, tempered by years of long brutal winters. Track winter weather From the Rockies to the heartland, ferocious winds are ripping across the Plains, bringing freezing temperatures as far south as Oklahoma and the Texas Panhandle. Even though we're still a couple of weeks from Black Friday, there seems to be a preseason special on snow and cold. "This is the first snow I've seen this season, and it's a major snowstorm, rather than the usual light dusting you'd expect to start off the winter with," said Katie Robinson of St. Paul. The upper Midwest and Great Lakes are buried in snow, shivering under the icy blanket.
