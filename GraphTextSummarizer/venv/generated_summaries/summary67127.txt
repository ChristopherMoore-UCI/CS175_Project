To make loading photos onto the iPad easier, I bought the $29 iPad Camera Connection Kit to transfer photos and videos from an SD card and my iPhone. The Web Albums app for iPhone and iPad lets me upload batches of photos to Facebook at the same time and create and easily manage my Facebook photo albums. There's no official Facebook iPad app (yet), and the iPhone app only lets you upload one photo at a time. My iPad has become my mobile command center to entertain and inform myself and to share news and photos of my son with friends and family.
