Weather is dangerous, and when it comes to photographing it, Jones says it's important to be cognizant of general weather safety. Lightning photos tend to be a common subject in weather photographs, and Miller says at times they start to look similar. Miller says to avoid adding filters when capturing weather photographs. Here is Jones' rule of thumb for editing weather photographs: "Only do in post-production what you could have done in a darkroom," he said. If you want to capture weather photographs, Miller suggests monitoring the National Weather Service and Storm Prediction Center.
