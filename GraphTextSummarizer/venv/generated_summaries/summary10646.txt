Chile will continue to provide economic leadership in the hemisphere, but in addressing this current tragedy, can the nation provide social leadership as well? But now the country's greatest challenge will probably not be economic recovery; rather, it will be social recovery. Chilean leaders have a mixed record in rebuilding after severe quakes; effected areas rarely recovered well. -- Images of destroyed homes, people sleeping in the streets and broken freeways reveal the recent tragedy of Chile. These are "callampas," or impoverished wards of major cities and small towns that have been bypassed by progress.
