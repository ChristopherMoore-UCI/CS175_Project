Accidents and natural disasters Back then, postcards weren't just for stirring envy among friends and relations. World Post Day celebrates the anniversary of the 1874 creation of the Universal Postal Union, an international organization linking mail services around the world. Postcards appeared about the same time, with picture postcards following soon after.
