Students and educators view education as a public good available to all and will continue mobilizing to restore funding for public education. Rising student fees have placed barriers between thousands of eligible students and their dreams of higher education. Like many other low-income and working students, community college was her entry into higher education. Younger generations in the U.S. have consistently achieved a higher level of education than the generation that came before. Will they receive support or will education become a luxury available to fewer and fewer people?
