A recent survey of elementary school teachers found that two-thirds of teachers reported spending money out of their pockets to help feed hungry students. In the seven years that Gahn has been principal at Whitney, students have doubled their proficiency scores on standardized reading tests. Gahn said she believes her students deserve rigorous classroom instruction and the efforts are showing results. Hundreds of students are sent home each Friday with food supplies to keep their families fed through the weekend. Hernandez is at the school most mornings, helping to fill food baskets, assisting kids who need clothes or helping out any way she can.
