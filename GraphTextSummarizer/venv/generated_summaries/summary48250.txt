Mistake: Lost patients     • Cause: Patients with dementia are sometimes prone to wandering. Consequences: Patients can die from infections spread by hospital workers. Mistake: Treating the wrong patient      • Cause: Hospital staff fails to verify a patient's identity. Prevention: Doctors listen to other doctors, so on your way to the hospital call your physician and ask them to call the emergency room. Mistake: Fake doctors     • Cause: Con artists pretend to be doctors.
