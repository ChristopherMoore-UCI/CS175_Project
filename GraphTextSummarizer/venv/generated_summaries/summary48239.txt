Trust will not be restored by clerics investigating and judging clerics. This would add more personnel for critical church functions in locations without sufficient priests. Some will call for improved administration, finding a way to draw new Catholics into the church where it is in decline, and increasing engagement with other faith traditions. Indeed, the very name Francis honors St. Francis of Assisi, a person of humility and deep caring. The church has conservative positions on human sexuality, bioethics, etc., but liberal positions on issues such as economic regulation, the death penalty and immigration.
