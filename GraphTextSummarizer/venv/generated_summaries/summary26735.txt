In stark comparison to pop music, contemporary jazz seems too circuitous for most listeners to enjoy casually. Jazz music teaches you about who you are by exploring the humanity of others. The way jazz musicians walk, talk and greet each other, and the way they play is very distinct. The challenge for the contemporary jazz musician, as I see it, is making this subtle and complex art palatable to the greater public. Some of the greatest musical minds of all-time were jazz artists.
