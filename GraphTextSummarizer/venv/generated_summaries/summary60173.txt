Everyone's favorite male models made a surprise appearance at Tuesday's Valentino show at Paris Fashion Week. Ben Stiller and Owen Wilson reprised their roles as the vacuous models from the popular 2001 film "Zoolander" at Tuesday's women's couture show. There was no mistaking Stiller for anyone other than the fierce Zoolander as he sashayed down the runway in a Valentino trench, casting Blue Steel in every direction. The duo is gearing up for "Zoolander 2," which is slated for release in February 2016.
