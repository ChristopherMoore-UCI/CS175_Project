Stanford's alternative spring break has students visiting social enterprises ranging from education to criminal rehabilitation. Social entrepreneurship has surged on American campuses in the past few years, according to Melanie Edwards, who lectures on the subject at Stanford University. Sophomore Nishant Jacob said he chose Stanford because of its "Social E" opportunities.
