Teen featured in viral video dies "In many ways, Olivia has lived a shortened, but full life," her mother wrote in the letter to CNN. Olivia gained fame in the last weeks of her 16-year-long life when a Katy Perry song she recorded in a Toronto studio in September became a viral hit online. Her version of Perry's hit "Roar," which she recorded in September after learning there were no more treatments available, drew the attention of Perry after it was published on YouTube in October.
