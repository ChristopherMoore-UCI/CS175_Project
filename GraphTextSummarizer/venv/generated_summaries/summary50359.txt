Shannon Johnson, 34, was arrested January 11 on one count of child abuse by knowingly and recklessly causing a death, said Jennifer Finch, community relations director for the Weld County District Attorney's Office. The infant, not named in the affidavit, was 13 months old, according to CNN affiliate KUSA. According to the Weld County Sheriff's Office affidavit, the child died on September 20, 2010. The autopsy listed anoxic brain injury, cardiac arrest and drowning as the cause of death. "The Defendant admitted that she was on Facebook playing a game called Cafe World, checking on friends' status and sharing videos, in the living room, while [the infant] was in the bathtub," according to the affidavit.
