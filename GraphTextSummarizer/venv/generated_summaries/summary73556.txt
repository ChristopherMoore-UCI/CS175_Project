"We still have about 500 homes -- we're calling them threatened -- and we suggest people to stay out of them for now," Lynne Tomalchoff, a Cal Fire spokeswoman, told CNN affiliate KCRA. "We're encouraging those folks to leave their homes," Suzi Brady with Cal Fire told affiliate KDRV. The King Fire is roaring through the El Dorado National Forest, about 60 miles east of Sacramento Some 8,600 acres have burned and the flames are only 5% contained, according Cal Fire. Mandatory and voluntary evacuations are in place across large swaths of El Dorado County. Some 1,500 homes are under evacuation orders in Siskiyou County.
