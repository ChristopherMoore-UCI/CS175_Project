Believe me, most endurance runners would love to be mentioned in the same sentence as any of Kenya's elite athletes. It's a pittance in comparison to the professional athletes who play sports such as football and basketball, but in Kenya, elite runners are celebrities who often live like royalty. Take a look at just about any major endurance race of the past 30 years, everything from the Boston Marathon to the World Cross Country Championships to the Summer Olympics, and you'll see runners from Kenya, men and women, standing on the winner's podium.
