Socialist Michelle Bachelet, who was Chile's president from 2006 to 2010, will face off against Evelyn Matthei of the conservative Independent Democratic Union party. Chile's constitution requires a candidate to garner more than 50% of votes to win the presidential election. Matthei had garnered 25.1% of votes.
