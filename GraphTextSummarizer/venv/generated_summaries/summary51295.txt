Students also receive comprehensive support from a social worker to address issues, learn life skills, finish high school and apply to college or trade school. This year, Green said, all 12 seniors in the program graduated high school and are headed to college. According to the New York State Education Department, only about 42% of students in the Hunts Point area graduate high school. Alumni continue to receive support through college; a number of them are hired as program assistants. The experience had such a profound impact, Green was inspired to use the same concept to help other young people in need.
