In 2001, Zanardi was leading in a Champ car race at Lausitzring in Germany, with 13 laps to go. Four years later, he won a World Touring Car Championship race, using his hip to work a modified brake pedal attached to a prosthetic leg, an accelerating device mounted under the steering wheel, and a clutch mechanism worked by hand.
