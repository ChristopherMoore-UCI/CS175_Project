The dark history of Bunce Island Former Chinese laborers are still trying to gain compensation and an official apology from Mitsubishi for their enslavement at sites across the empire during World War II. Before and during World War II, Hashima, like many industrial sites in Japan, was a location for forced labor. Hashima is one of several industrial sites awaiting inclusion on UNESCO's list of World Heritage Sites. For almost 100 years, Hashima was a mining facility run by corporate giant Mitsubishi.
