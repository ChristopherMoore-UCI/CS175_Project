"Our main challenge is our governing bodies in Barbados take sport too lightly, it's one of the reasons it hasn't reached other Caribbean countries. Road Tennis is a fast and furious scaled-down version of the sport which can be played almost anywhere, and has attracted the interest of stars such as Andy Murray and Venus Williams. There is prize money at stake for the Caribbean island's 150 active players, but Road Tennis remains semi-professional. According to the official Professional Road Tennis website, the sport was first played in the 1930s in the parish of St Michael.
