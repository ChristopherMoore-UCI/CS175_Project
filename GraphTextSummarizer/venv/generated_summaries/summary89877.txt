Mourinho has said he wants to join Madrid after leading Inter to a treble of Champions League, Italian league and cup last season. -- Real Madrid have fired Manuel Pellegrini as manager, paving the way for Jose Mourinho to take over. The Spanish club's president Florentino Perez said Mourinho has agreed personal terms and will be appointed once he has negotiated an to end his contract with Italian champions Inter Milan. "We're very happy that Jose Mourinho will be the man to take over the reins of Real Madrid for the coming years - he's won everything possible. "It's not been an easy decision to reach but we're 100 percent behind the need for a change," Perez told a press conference in Madrid.
