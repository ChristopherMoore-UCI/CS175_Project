In Africa, we worship at the altar of innovation Innovation is the fountain from which most African entrepreneurs drink -- their raison d'etre. Solve a real problem, damn it Interestingly, Africa's key drivers in terms of innovation came from very simple products that wanted to solve real problems. At this stage we might have to rename Africa "the place where good companies die of too much innovation."
