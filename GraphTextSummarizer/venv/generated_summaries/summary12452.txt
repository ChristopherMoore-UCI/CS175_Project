The Metzgers' story is playing out all across America as businesses and communities feel the ripple effect of the recession "When jobs get lost, demand falls for a whole bunch of businesses. Job losses suffered by Linda and Chris Metzger have had a ripple effect on other businesses in their community In early March 2008, 31-year-old Linda Metzger went to the New York office where she had worked as an assistant vice president in the marketing department at Lehman Brothers for about a year. Metzger says that would be her last day at Lehman Brothers. Vacations were put on hold; furniture purchases had to wait; fine meals out were now home-cooked meals.
