After this weekend's Abu Dhabi race, teams will stay at the Yas Marina circuit to take part in the annual young driver test event -- where a Prost will take to an F1 track for the first time since the 1993 Australian Grand Prix. Alain Prost has revealed that he hopes to resurrect the French Grand Prix, a race he won six times during his illustrious Formula One career. We have been everywhere in the world for the benefit of Formula One but at the end of the day, the culture, the tradition of Formula One and motor racing is really in Europe." Meanwhile, reports have suggested F1 teams face increased entry fees next season as the sport looks to increase revenue. Prost has special attachment to the track in Le Castellet, where he took some of his first steps on the road to F1.
