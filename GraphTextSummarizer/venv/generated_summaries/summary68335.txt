Boulder, Colorado (University of Colorado) In a town that always pops up on "healthiest cities in America" lists, bike trails are abundant and outdoor options are endless. Burlington, Vermont (University of Vermont) College towns don't get more scenic than Burlington, where a 7.5-mile bike path on old railroad beds provides wind-riffled views of Lake Champlain. During the off-season, browse for art at Red Window, owned by Elizabeth Edwards, wife of former senator John Edwards. There are notable distinctions between college towns and other American cities.
