"TSA was notified of a passenger allegedly smoking onboard American Airlines Flight 117 from John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) to Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)," the Transportation Security Administration said in a statement. It was diverted to Albuquerque on Wednesday afternoon. The FBI took the male passenger into custody, said Daniel Jiron, a spokesman for the Albuquerque International Airport. The flight from New York was on its way to Los Angeles. The plane later continued onto Los Angeles without the detained passenger  late Wednesday.
