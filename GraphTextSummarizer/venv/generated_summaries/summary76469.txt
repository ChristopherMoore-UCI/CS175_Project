Lee hopes that Vessyl will be used in conjunction with existing health technologies that record personal details such as heart rate and body mass index to optimize consumption choices. Rather than a desperate government intervention, this is the concept of the Vessyl 'smart cup' -- a groundbreaking addition to the field of consumer health technologies, which has launched for pre-orders. The data has to be clear and actionable." Certainly, one need not be a technology guru to make use of the Vessyl, the ease of which owes much to the collaboration with award-winning designer Yves Behar, creator of the Jawbone. The sleek 13-ounce design is fitted with a sensor that analyzes the molecular content of drinks in real time, and then displays selected nutritional information such as sugar or alcohol content on the side of the cup. Moments later, sugar and calorie content flash up.
