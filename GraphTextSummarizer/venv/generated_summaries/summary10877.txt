Kyra Sedgwick took home her first Emmy for best actress in a drama for her work on "The Closer," and Archie Panjabi from "The Good Wife" won for best supporting actress in a drama. Aaron Paul of "Breaking Bad" scored his first win for outstanding supporting actor in a drama, and Jim Parsons from "The Big Bang Theory" beat out such veterans Alec Baldwin and Tony Shalhoub for best actor in a comedy. -- First-time winners and new shows took the lion's share of the hardware Sunday night at the 62nd Primetime Emmy Awards. "The Daily Show" won for outstanding variety, music, or comedy series. Falco, who had previously won for "The Sopranos" in the drama category, took home the award for best actress in a comedy for her role on "Nurse Jackie."
