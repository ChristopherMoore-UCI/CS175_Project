"Following Friday morning's incident club president Augusto Cesar Lendoiro and coach Miguel Angel Lotina and his staff have decided not to use Aouate and Munua for the Villarreal game," the club said on its Web site. -- Deportivo Coruna goalkeepers Dudu Aouate and Gustavo Munua have been left out of the squad for the match at Villarreal after being involved in a fight during a training session on Friday. Aouate, 30, was replaced by Munua, 29, as the club's number one goalkeeper after Deportivo's 2-1 league defeat at home against Osasuna lat month. He had been first choice since arriving from Racing in 2006. Aouate needed six stitches in his face after being punched by Munua.
