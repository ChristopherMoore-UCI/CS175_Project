A long journey Lois Pope was visiting the Vietnam War memorial when she was first struck by the need for a special place to honor disabled veterans. The American Veterans Disabled for Life Memorial, located just blocks from the U.S. Capitol, was dedicated Sunday in a ceremony that focused on life after war and the many sacrifices disabled veterans still make. The memorial was designed as a space for disabled veterans and those they love to find quiet reflection.
