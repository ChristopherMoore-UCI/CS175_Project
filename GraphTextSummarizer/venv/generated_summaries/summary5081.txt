For the vast majority of Japanese voters, support for the LDP stemmed from the prospect of economic revival, and they were prepared to swallow parts of Abe's security agenda if that was part of the package. Abe and the LDP returned to power in December 2012 determined to reassert Japan's role in a rapidly changing East Asia. Yet opinion polls show the LDP increasing its share of seats in the parliament, despite dropping approval ratings for the Cabinet and Abe. -- Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe wants a mandate. Unfortunately for Abe, the economy has slowed in recent months with Japan now technically in recession after two consecutive quarters of negative growth.
