In 1998 he made it into the ranks of London's Royal Ballet, advancing to principal guest artist in 2003, to become the Royal Ballet's first black principal. He went on to dance as principal with the English National Ballet from 1991 to 1992, the National Ballet of Cuba from 1992 to 1994, and was a principal with Houston Ballet between 1993 and 1998.
