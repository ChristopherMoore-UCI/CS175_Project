Fans air concerns about new PlayStation, Xbox The new Xbox One used-games policy only affects disc-based games. Reacting to "feedback from the Xbox community," Microsoft is appearing to reverse course and change two key components to policies for its new Xbox One video game console. As a result of feedback from the Xbox community, we have changed certain policies for Xbox One reflected in this blog. Consumers have been reacting with anger over the policies, but the tipping point may have been when Jimmy Fallon, host of NBC's "Late Night," pointed out that only the PlayStation 4 could freely play used games, which created more confusion. The company has been taking a public berating since it announced restrictions to used games and their requirement for an Internet connection.
