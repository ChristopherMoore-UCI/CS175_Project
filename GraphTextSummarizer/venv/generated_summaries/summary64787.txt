5 things we've learned about cruising Crime on cruise ships, though infrequent, presents special problems, Rockefeller said. Only a tiny portion of alleged crimes on cruise ships is ever publicly disclosed, according to a report by the Senate Commerce Committee. Rockefeller has introduced legislation that would require industry to disclose all crime alleged on cruise ships, and beef up video surveillance of public areas. But cruise ships can also lay claim: Whatever happens on cruise ships, stays on cruise ships. Sen. Jay Rockefeller, a West Virginia Democrat, disclosed the statistics on Wednesday in advance of a hearing on safety and security on cruise ships.
