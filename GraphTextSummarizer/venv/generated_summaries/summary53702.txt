And 20 states -- California among them -- grant "philosophical exemptions for those who object to immunizations because of personal, moral or other beliefs," NCSL says. In California, it's relatively easy for parents to obtain exemptions based on their personal beliefs. According to the San Jose Mercury News, 87% of kindergartners at the Berkeley Rose School, for example, had exemptions from vaccination. The result: It's easy for misinformed parents to skip vaccinations in California.
