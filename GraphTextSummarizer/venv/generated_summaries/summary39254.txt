Maya Angelou remembered by those she inspired Legendary author Maya Angelou dies at age 86 -- Poetry, performance and prayer celebrated the voice of literary giant Maya Angelou at a memorial service held Saturday at Wake Forest University in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. Angelou taught American studies for years at Wake Forest. The memorial service also featured singer Lee Ann Womack performing "I Hope You Dance," considered Angelou's favorite song. Angelou, 86, died at her Winston-Salem home on May 28.
