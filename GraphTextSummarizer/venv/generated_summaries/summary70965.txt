Despite the dwindling odds, King is convinced Dr. Kasem and his staff say they will go on performing medical miracles. Despite the chilling images sent in by journalists and opposition activists, King says Syrians like Dr. Kasem believe the world has turned away. "Since late April, I have been photographing and filming inside the besieged city of Al Qusayr," King says. Smugglers risk their lives to bring in medical supplies and surgical equipment, King says. Dr. Kasem is aware that every patient could be his last.
