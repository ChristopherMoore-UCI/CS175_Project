Progress was made when U.S. President Barack Obama visited New Delhi last month but it will take years for nuclear energy to power India in a significant way. Aware of international pressure to reduce its greenhouse gases, India is trying to ramp up alternative energy options. Compared with the three gigawatts India produced last year, it seems ambitious. Coal is a polluting fossil fuel, which currently powers more than 60% of India. So why is India so hungry for coal?
