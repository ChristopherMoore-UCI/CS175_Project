If parents are not going to assume responsibility for their children's online access on their own -- and it seems like the parents in this case are not -- then I would support legislation that places legal responsibility on parents, making them liable for what the children do with the online access parents provide. In the wake of this suicide, Judd has implored parents to take more responsibility for their children's online behavior.
