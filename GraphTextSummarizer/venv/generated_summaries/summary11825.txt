London cocaine, Prague meth The study reveals apparent preferences for certain drugs in certain regions. Lab tests on sewage water to detect chemicals excreted after drug use turned up high levels of cannabis, cocaine, ecstasy, meth and other amphetamines. The laboratory analysis of wastewater has the potential to get more accurate results more quickly and detect new drugs spreading into a local market. Drug users also sometimes don't know what they are really taking or whether other drugs are mixed in. Serious problems The study may seem like a review of dereliction hot spots, but its aim is serious: to help detect dangerous addictions and spikes in the use of certain illicit drugs.
