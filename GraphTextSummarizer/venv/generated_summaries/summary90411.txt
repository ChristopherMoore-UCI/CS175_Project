South Beach is the classic Miami sunning spot, but if you want to avoid the crowds, Sunny Isles Beach, at the northern end of Miami Beach, is a better bet. Breaking point From early March to mid April Miami is plagued by a voracious swarm of college kids on their spring break. A city for all seasons Because Miami is a beach destination, it has definite holiday seasons. A little further afield, Key Biscayne, five miles from the mainland, has some great beaches, as does Fort Lauderdale, some 30 miles north of Miami. Miami Beach during Spring Break may not be to everyone's taste.
