For a host of reasons, many women grow up not truly appreciating what we can do and what we can be, says Nelson, whose book is broken down into 20 codes or keys that can help women "unlock" their true potential. "What women have to do is not work for less money," she said, according to CNN Money. Code No. 1, she says, is for women to know their own value, and we're talking value beyond what they earn or believe they should earn. If women want to eliminate pay disparities in Hollywood, they need to demand more money. That is certainly easier said than done for most women in America, who can't afford to turn their backs on their jobs when they are helping to support families or doing it all on their own.
