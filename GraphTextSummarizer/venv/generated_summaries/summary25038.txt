The shipyard in the Polish port city employed around 20,000 workers building container ships and vessels for the navies of many East European communist countries. Sunreef Yachts is a French company that has been building its high-end catamarans in Gdansk since 2002. During the 1970s and '80s it was said that what happened in Gdansk moved the world.
