The alchemy skill tree boosts gathering and blending abilities as well as increasing the effects of potions and oils. It is rated M for Mature due to blood and gore, intense violence, nudity, strong language, strong sexual content, and use of drugs. Leveling up through earned experience allows players to augment certain abilities through skill trees. Preparations for combat, which are almost always necessary, will require multiple button pushes to cycle through numerous menus for potions, oils and the like. All different kinds of herbs, trinkets and supplies are lying around, waiting for Geralt to pick them up and convert them into potions, armor or oils.
