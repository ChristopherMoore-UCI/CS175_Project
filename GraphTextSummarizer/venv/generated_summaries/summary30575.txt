Hosted by CNN anchor Kristie Lu Stout in Hong Kong and produced by Ravi Hiranand, News Stream is a daily hard-news show that uses technology and social media to give viewers a deeper understanding of the day's top stories. The Best News Program title went to CNN flagship program News Stream. Lu Stout has also led coverage of major breaking news stories for CNN, most recently covering the 'Umbrella Movement' pro-democracy protests from the heart of Hong Kong. In the gripping documentary, CNN followed the story of three girls sold into sex slavery as 'virgins' by their own mothers.
