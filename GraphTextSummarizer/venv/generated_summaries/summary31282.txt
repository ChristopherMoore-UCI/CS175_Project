Cars connect with smartphones Driving and smartphones may not mix now, but car companies are still looking into ways to safely combine the two. Cars are new territory for the smartphone operating-system battle, with Apple and Google each angling for prime placement inside of popular car brands. Fully self-driving cars are still a ways off, but early features that will let vehicles communicate with other cars, gather data about your driving patterns and take over in stop-and-go traffic are beginning to appear. Some tech that started with game systems, such as motion-controlled interfaces, will branch out into other devices.
