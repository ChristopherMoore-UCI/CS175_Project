How you can help Kamlari girls and girls like Purnima in Nepal: Give You can help girls like Purnima go to school and chase their dreams by donating to Room to Read's Girls Education program. Thousands of girls in Nepal and other countries have been helped through school with books, school fees and mentorship. According to Room to Read, about 76% of its graduates go on to some kind of university, college or vocational training after secondary school. More than 7 million children have been given access to education through the nonprofit's network of libraries and schools. In Nepal, government schools start charging tuition in the sixth grade.
