Changing landscape Once a dusty regional backwater, Pemba town is already changing as a result of the country's enviable natural resources. The protected Bay of Pemba in northern Mozambique may look like a sea of tranquility, but this area is surging with change. When locals in Pemba saw other Mozambicans working with Subtech, attitudes changed. In Pemba, the time to make money is now. "The Mozambicans have employed a very very high profile department of environmental affairs and they're really looking into the background of the oil and gas as to what effect it's going to have on the population," explains Bevis.
