While admitting he has created something of a brand of himself, Lang Lang is happy that he gains more plaudits from his contemporaries than negative reviews, and that he's able to help inspire others to take up music. -- A Grammy-nominated classical pianist who wears personalized trainers, Lang Lang is one-of-a-kind in the world of classical music. An estimated 40 million children in China are learning the piano because of the "Lang Lang effect." At the age of 9, Lang Lang gained a place at the Central Music Conservatory, moving with his father to Beijing, where he faced some of the toughest moments in his young life. Being so far from his mother, he found little support at the time from his father, with whom Lang Lang admits to having a difficult relationship as a child.
