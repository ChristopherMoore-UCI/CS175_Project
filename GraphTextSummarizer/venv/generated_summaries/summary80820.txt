Infographic: A short history of hotel room perks "There were five-figure rooms in the '90s, but there were fewer," acknowledges Nikhil Bhalla, vice president of equity research in lodging at FBR Capital Markets. "We call them our e-wow suites -- short for 'extreme wow,'" he says, referring to suites at the W Hotels. These days, luxury hotel are clamoring to turn over space (and a lot of it) for these ultra-luxury abodes. According to Paul James, the global brand leader for Starwood's luxury brands, of the 40 or so new properties the hotel group has in the pipeline, about half will be outfitted with premium rooms.
