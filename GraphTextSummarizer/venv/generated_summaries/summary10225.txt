Mourners from Najafabad, Montazeri's hometown, passed out dates, an Iranian tradition at funerals. Around 300 to 400 people gathered at Tehran's Mother's Square and lit candles on Sunday night in memory of Montazeri, several sources told CNN. His death is "a terrible blow for the (reformist) green movement and everybody who has been active these past few months," said Mehrdad Khonsari, a senior research consultant at the Centre for Arab and Iranian Studies in London.
