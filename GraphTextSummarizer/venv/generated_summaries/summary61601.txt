The smallest of AT&T's new data plans gives mobile users 200 MB of data per month for $15. The "DataPro" plan gives people 2 gigabytes of data -- about 10 times more -- for $25 per month. Mobile access to data also entails considerable overhead, and consumers pay for that, too.
