Among Mac enthusiasts, a popular form of artwork is the conversion of an old Mac case into a shell for a fish tank. As storage innovations cropped up, users sought to purchase additional floppy drives and, later, units with hard drives. Over the years, he has amassed several old Macintosh units. In fact, he likes to do much of his art on Mac cases themselves. Luckily, he still has a late-1980s picture of actor Kurt Russell with his boxy Mac desktop.
