They insist that only armed resistance can force Britain's withdrawal from Northern Ireland and lead to a united Ireland. Yet, peace in Northern Ireland is still described by many as fragile. They continue to target Northern Irish police stations, with weaponry of growing sophistication. Thousands of additional police officers are being brought in from England, Wales and the Republic of Ireland to assist. They weren't even born at the time of the 1994 Provisional IRA cease-fire, and some of the older members are senior ex-IRA members who bring much needed political acumen, as well as expertise in bomb-making, recruitment, training and targeting.
