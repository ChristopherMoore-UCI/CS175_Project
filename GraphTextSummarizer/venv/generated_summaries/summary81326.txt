The majority of pickle factories in America ferment their pickles in outdoor vats without lids (leaving them subject to insects and bird droppings). People have been eating pickles ever since the Mesopotamians started making them way back in 2400 B.C.E. Here are some even more important things you should know about them. Cleopatra claimed pickles made her beautiful. The Department of Agriculture estimates that the average American eats 8.5 lbs of pickles a year. If it weren't for pickles, Christopher Columbus might never have "discovered" America.
