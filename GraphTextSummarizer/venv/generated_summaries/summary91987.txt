Msindisi Fengu and Yandisa Monakali shared the top honor of CNN MultiChoice African Journalist of the Year for their series "School hostels of shame," which appeared in South African newspaper Daily Dispatch. " Nico Meyer, CEO of MultiChoice Africa, said journalists played a vital role in the development of African media. The awards were established in 1995 to encourage, promote and recognize excellence in African journalism. An investigation into appalling conditions in school hostels has won two South African journalists the top prize at the CNN MultiChoice African Journalist Awards. " The award carried a substantial cash prize, plus a visit to CNN Center in Atlanta to attend the three-week CNN Journalism Fellowship.
