Indifferent South Dubbed the "Defector Girl Boxer" in South Korea, Choi is a rare success story among thousands who escape poverty or persecution in the North to be confronted by the reality of a highly competitive and often indifferent South. She trained in North Korea until she defected with her family to South Korea in 2004.
