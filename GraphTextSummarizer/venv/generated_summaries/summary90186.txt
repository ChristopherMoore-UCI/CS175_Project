This story echoes what so many have suffered through during 16 years of eastern Congo's war, the deadliest since World War II, nearly 70 years ago, with about 6.9 million deaths, based on a New York Times estimate in 2010. But calls for international justice have intensified inside Congo and beyond, and accused war criminals are beginning to face sanctions.
