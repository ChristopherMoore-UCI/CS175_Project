Ferrari have admitted their approach to the 2011 Formula One season has been too conservative, after the Italian team failed to register a podium finish in the first three races of the year. Fernando Alonso and Felipe Massa, are fifth and sixth respectively in the drivers' standings, with the former trailing current leader and reigning world champion Sebastian Vettel by 42 points. "Now we are about to tackle three races in four weeks: it will be important to show that we are back on form and to make it to the podium, something that has escaped us in all of the first three races of the season. "Our approach was not aggressive enough at the design stage," the 45-year-old told Ferrari's official web site.
