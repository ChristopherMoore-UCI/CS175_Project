The Eastern Conference All-Star team had a South Beach feel to it, with Miami Heat players LeBron James and Dwyane Wade on the starting squad. Chris Paul and Blake Griffin of the L.A. Clippers and Kobe Bryant and Andrew Bynum of the L.A. Lakers made the Western Conference starting team. The teams will play in the 2012 NBA All-Star Game on February 26 at the Amway Center in Orlando. The NBA will announce reserve team members on February 9. -- Basketball fans love L.A., if this year's NBA All-Star ballot results are any indication.
