We searched for a name for twenty years for Mandy disease. Surgically implanted tubes in her ears, infection, tubes out, surgery, tubes in. But in the meantime, Mandy is beginning a career in motivational speaking. By day five, after six trips to doctors and emergency rooms, when her eye was swollen beyond seeing and looked like a piece of raw meat, we were finally able to beg her doctor for more aggressive testing. Mandy was a healthy, nine pound, green-eyed beauty -- until the day she was diagnosed with Spinal Meningitis.
