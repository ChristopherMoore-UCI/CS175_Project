Schuller, also the founder of Crystal Cathedral megachurch, had been diagnosed with esophageal cancer in August 2013, a release from "Hour of Power" said. Bobby Schuller Jr. left "Hour of Power" and the pulpit at Crystal Cathedral after a short time. In 1970, Schuller began broadcasting "Hour of Power," believed to be one of the first, if not the very first, Sunday service to be shown regularly on television. His confident, breezy version of Christianity -- too breezy, by some estimations -- drew hordes of seekers and lapsed Christians who were put off by the hellfire fulminations of many post-War American preachers. He was a great Dad and a great man of God," said Schuller's daughter-in-law, Donna Schuller, in a Twitter message.
