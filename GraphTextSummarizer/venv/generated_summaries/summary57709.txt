Mobile analysis firm Metrico performed a comprehensive study on several AT&T and Verizon handsets, including the iPhone on both networks. Some of their findings: -- The Verizon iPhone performed "below average" in data download speeds when compared to other Verizon phones including the HTC Incredible. Most peculiar to Wired.com is that the Verizon iPhone is "below average" in download speeds compared to other Verizon handsets. For downloading data, the Verizon iPhone is slower than the AT&T iPhone, and it's also slower than most other Verizon smartphones, according to a study published Monday. Independent tests by multiple publications, including Wired.com, have found that AT&T's network is faster, while Verizon's is more reliable with phone calls.
