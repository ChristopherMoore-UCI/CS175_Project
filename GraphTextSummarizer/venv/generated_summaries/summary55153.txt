Looting also occurred in pockets of Enfield, next to Tottenham, in north London. In Lewisham in south London, a car was set afire. There was evidence of vandalism in central London, with visible damage to a number of shops. In the Hackney area of east London, gangs attacked a police car and shops. "The scenes of violence and destruction over the weekend are utterly appalling," said London Mayor Boris Johnson, in a statement.
