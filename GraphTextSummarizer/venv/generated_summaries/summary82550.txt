-- Ferrari's Fernando Alonso has told CNN Sport's Don Riddell that Michael Schumacher is still the fastest driver on the Formula One grid. Speaking ahead of the opening grand prix of the season in Melbourne, Australia, Alonso -- himself a double world champion -- believes that seven-time world champion Schumacher remains a cut above his rivals. "If Formula One was 24 cars exactly the same, then Michael would be the driver I would fear most," revealed Alonso. Despite this, Alonso confirmed that coming so close to his third world title last year, left him sad and frustrated.
