The avalanches occurred at 11:30 a.m. (5:30 a.m. ET) and about noon Sunday in the Diemtigen Valley, 34 miles (55 km) south of Bern, said Thomas Jauch, a spokesman for police in Bern County. , Bern police said. In the first incident an avalanche buried one person who was with a party of eight cross-country skiers, Jauch said. As they were searching for the missing skier, a second avalanche buried 10 to 12 of them, Jauch said. "It was not a high-risk day; not a high-risk situation," Jauch said.
