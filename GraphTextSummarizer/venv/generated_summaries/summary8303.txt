Mohammed Jamjoom said: "We watched at least six boys play and enjoy themselves while aid was being distributed." Syrians would go to any lengths to reach Lebanon, Jamjoom said. Despite the miserable conditions, for many children in refugee camps, the snow was a novelty. Lebanon has absorbed the highest number of Syria's refugees. The snowman photo above was captured by CNN cameraman Christian Streib as he toured the U.N.-run Arsal transit camp in Lebanon's Bekaa Valley, now home to 40,000 refugees.
