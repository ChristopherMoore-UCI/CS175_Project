The origin of Pi Day Pi Day started 27 years ago at San Francisco's Exploratorium. A few more tidbits about pi and Pi Day: About pi Pi is the ratio of the circumference of a circle to its diameter. In Princeton, New Jersey, where Einstein lived for more than two decades, signs of the scientist permeate the Pi Day festivities, from birthday parties at the Historical Society of Princeton to an Einstein lookalike contest. Pi is essential in architecture and construction and was used frequently by early astronomers. Celebrations This year, the Exploratorium is hosting a day-long celebration at its facility on Pier 15, including a Pi Procession, servings of pie and a pizza pie dough-tossing event.
