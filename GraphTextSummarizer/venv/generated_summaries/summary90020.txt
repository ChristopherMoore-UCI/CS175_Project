The four main intersecting streets: Via Montenapoleone, Via Alessandro Manzoni, Corso Venezia, Via della Spiga are noisy and busy. Smaller players: Via Sant'Andrea, Via Santo Spirito These are the top internal alleys, away from the buzz of Via Monte Napoleone. "Here you can breathe the essence of Milan's fashion universe: creative, vibrant, dynamic," says personal shopper Annamaria Lamanna, who takes foreign clients on tours. Now he has a store on one of the most enviable retail streets in the world, at the southern end of Via Montenapoleone. Via Montenapoleone
