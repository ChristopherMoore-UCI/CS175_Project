In Group C, Sporting Lisbon came away from Lille with an impressive 2-1 win courtesy of first half goals from Simon Vukcevic and Helder Postiga. Dutch side AZ Alkmaar lead Group E after their opening 2-1 home victory over Sheriff Tiraspol, while Dynamo Kiev and BATE Borisov of Belarus shared four goals in the other match in Ukraine. The other group match saw Spanish side Getafe recover from going a goal down to defeat Odense 2-1. The other match saw Levski Sofia twice come from behind to defeat Belgian side Ghent 3-2. Brazilian striker Vagner Love scored twice for CSKA Moscow in their 3-0 Group F success at Swiss side Lausanne.
