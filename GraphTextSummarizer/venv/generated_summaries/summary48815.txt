Addie made the mailing lists of thousands of strangers around the world after her family asked the public to give her a lifetime of Christmas cards after a gloomy prognosis: A mysterious condition will probably cut her young life short, doctors have told the family. They've created the Facebook page for "Little Addie" to update followers with pictures of Addie opening letters and celebrating Christmas. Addie is appreciative of the kind words that have been flooding in, and she had a message for KSL's camera: "Merry Christmas. "She likes to laugh and have a good time and hear stories, but lately she has had a lot of days she will just cry all day long," Tami Fausett, Addie's mother, told CNN affiliate KSL. This year, the Fausetts were told this would probably be Addie's last Christmas.
