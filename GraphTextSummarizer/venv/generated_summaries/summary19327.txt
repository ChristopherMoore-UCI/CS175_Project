The 90-degree temperatures will last another day across New Mexico and western Texas, with daytime highs plummeting to the 50s on Thursday. "It's incredibly frustrating," Trela Phelps, manger of City Flora, told affiliate KDVR. "Crazy temperature contrast," said meteorologist Kathy Sabine at CNN affiliate KUSA.
