"Football helmets and football pads are good for football; combat helmets and combat pads are good for combat. Army officials recently met with counterparts from the National Football League and NASCAR to study ways to design helmets that provide better protection from blunt force contact. While Kevlar may be the answer to protecting arteries and groins, Kevlar helmets may soon be history. Currently, Kevlar helmets come with 3/4-inch foam pads inside. Right now about half of all soldiers wear large helmets.
