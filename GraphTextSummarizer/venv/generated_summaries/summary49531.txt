-- A command post set up to investigate the disappearance of missing Missouri girl Lisa Irwin has been shut down, Kansas City police said Wednesday. Police told CNN affiliate KCTV that investigators also have to focus their attention on child abuse and neglect cases that have come in since Lisa's disappearance. Dozens of investigators, including police and FBI personnel, have conducted numerous searches for the missing girl -- but have come up empty.
