Mars One, a Dutch nonprofit organization, aims to establish the first human settlement on Mars in the coming decades. By sending humans there, we can find answers to Mars' past and future, and ultimately, maybe find answers to the future of Earth. Much like the astronauts on the International Space Station learn to live and work in space, the Mars Society's MDRS outpost teaches us how to live, work and solve problems on Mars.
