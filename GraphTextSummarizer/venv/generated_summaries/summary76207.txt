A relapse of his condition in July last year saw Vilanova resign as Barcelona coach. -- Former Barcelona coach Tito Vilanova, who has been battling cancer, has died at the age of 45. "The club wishes to express its most heartfelt sympathy to his family, who are being joined in these moments of mourning by FC Barcelona members and fans all around the world, as well as the rest of the footballing and sporting world." May he rest in peace." Vilanova, who had first been diagnosed with a cancerous tumor of the throat in November 2011, stepped down as Barcelona coach in July 2013. Vilanova's Barcelona team, which included star players such as Lionel Messi and Andres Iniesta, started the 2012/2013 campaign brilliantly.
