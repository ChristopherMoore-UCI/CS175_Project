Organized attempts are made from time to time by white supremacist groups to use white power music to attract young people, but none of these attempts has truly been successful. There are other small subgenres of hate music; even a few white power hip hop artists, though most white supremacists dislike hip hop. White power music is often cited as a recruiting tool for white supremacists. Onstage, people are playing deafening music, shouting more than singing, with lyrics urging white people "to stand up and fight. Others purchase or download white power music from the Internet.
