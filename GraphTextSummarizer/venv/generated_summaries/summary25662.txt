The dynamic journey features sustainable transport projects such as the Hong Kong Police's fleet of electric scooters, clean fuel taxis, electric cars and solar ferries. One company is pioneering a solution to the high carbon emissions: carbon capture. Nitrogen fertilizers are responsible for over a third of the sector's emissions, and plants are notoriously bad at absorbing nitrogen. CCS technology captures carbon dioxide produced during the coal combustion process and aims to store it underground indefinitely. They taste the delights of the Intercontinental cuisine - it's 'carbon neutral menu' and watch as the waste oil is collected to be converted into bio-fuel at a local power plant.
