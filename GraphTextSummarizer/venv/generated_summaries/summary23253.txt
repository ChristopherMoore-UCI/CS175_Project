A patent application filed by Apple reveals an interesting possible development of iPad's Smart Cover, which may get a display of its own down the road. The secondary display, as described in Apple's patent application, could simply display notifications related to what's happening on the iPad (the name of the movie currently playing, for example). It could also be a full-fledged companion to the main screen, sporting a virtual keyboard or another set of application icons.
