In Philadelphia alone, electric provider PECO said early Friday that 319,000 homes and businesses were without power. By Friday evening, more than 270,000 customers were still with power in the Mid-Atlantic states. In the Baltimore area, about 19,000 homes and businesses remained without power early Friday, utility Baltimore Gas and Electric Co. said on its website. "Harder to push, not as easy actually to plow," said Boston snowplow driver Nick Sfravara. Utility companies scrambled Friday to restore power to the Northeast as hundreds of thousands shivered.
