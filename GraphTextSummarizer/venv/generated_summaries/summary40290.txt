"To be at London is very important for people with disabilities in Rwanda because we have to be there to present our country to the people with disabilities and those without disabilities," he said. "About eight years ago I was training regular volleyball and by coincidence I saw sitting volleyball and it got me, it hit me in my heart. and I wasn't comfortable with it, but after a year I saw it was a good game for people with disabilities," said 25-year-old university student Emile Vuningoma, who plays as an attacker in the team, as he took a break from training in the dark hall. We are lucky because sports is one of the only ways to integrate people with disabilities (into society). We are making sure we tell people disability is nothing.
