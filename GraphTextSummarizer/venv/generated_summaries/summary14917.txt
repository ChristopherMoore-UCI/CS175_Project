Consumers are looking for innovative products and despite difficult economic times across the world, Adidas is delivering, Hainer said. Adidas is now working with top designers, such as Stella McCartney, to create a distinctive genre of sportswear and lifestyle fashion products. But while ceo Herbert Hainer is happy with the diversity the collaborations provide, its main priority will always be sportswear, he told CNN. CNN's Eoghan Macguire contributed to this report This means its commitment to Europe will grow despite the continent's vulnerable economic conditions, Hainer said.
