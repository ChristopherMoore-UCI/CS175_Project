The story of the Beatles has taken on the power of myth. Though the songs came in handy for their own group, even McCartney and John Lennon were under no illusions that the Beatles would last. "It's a story that's lost all its excitement, because it's been trodden down through too many bad tellings through the years," says Lewisohn, the Beatles historian and author of such detailed works as "The Beatles Recording Sessions" and "The Beatles Chronicle."
