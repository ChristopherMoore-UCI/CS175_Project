Fire rips through clothing factory near Dhaka Thousands of people from dozens of clothing factories in Ashulia took to the streets Monday to protest the way in which their colleagues died. Wal-Mart Stores Inc. said Monday that the Tazreen Fashion factory in Ashulia, near the Bangladeshi capital, Dhaka, was no longer authorized to produce merchandise for its stores. A period of national mourning was also held Tuesday for those killed at the factory and for the victims from a recent overpass collapse in southeastern Bangladesh.
