"I did photograph a lot of people on the Tube, hundreds and hundreds. " East London blog Spitalfields Life first championed the then little-known Mazzer's work in 2013, and was bombarded with responses. -- For four decades, London's underground transport network has been Bob Mazzer's photographic playground. Spitalfields Life Books has now released the images in hardback, titled Bob Mazzer Underground, with a debut exhibition held in Shoreditch's Howard Griffin Gallery. Mazzer, 65, was born in East London to a Jewish couple.
