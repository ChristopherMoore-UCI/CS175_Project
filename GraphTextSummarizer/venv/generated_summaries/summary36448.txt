Fashion: Backstage Pass with Alina Cho debuts on Sept. 15 at 2:30pm E.T. on CNN US. "My goal is to not alienate Anna Wintour -- and also bring in viewers in Peoria," says Alina Cho, host of CNN's Fashion: Backstage Pass (airing Sept. 15 at 2:30 p.m. E.T.), on which she has interviewed Marc Jacobs, Donatella Versace and more. Alina Cho is a national correspondent for CNN and host of Fashion: Backstage Pass. Cho: In order to tell great stories, you need great characters. And there's no shortage of characters in fashion!
