Syria: Fast Facts In June, the White House said Syrian President Bashar al-Assad's forces had crossed a "red line" by using chemical weapons, including the nerve agent sarin gas, against rebel forces. Meanwhile, United Nations inspectors in the Netherlands are preparing to travel to Syria to investigate three reports of chemical weapons use, a spokesman said Thursday. Syria's government, meanwhile, has claimed rebel fighters have used chemical weapons as well. Amid the fighting, there have been numerous allegations that chemical weapons have been used.
