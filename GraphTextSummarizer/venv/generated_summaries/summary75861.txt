We spent some time testing and touching the new iPad Mini and iPad Air after Tuesday's press conference. A number of Apple products already have the Retina Display: the iPhone 4S and up, the fourth generation iPod Touch, the 10-inch iPad and some MacBook Pros. iPad Mini with Retina Display The iPad Mini got its first upgrade since it was first launched one year ago.
