Former U.S. soldier Steven Green faces life in prison after being convicted of murdering an Iraqi family. -- An Iraqi official condemned Friday the decision by a U.S. jury not to sentence a U.S. soldier to death. The murders of members of the al-Janabi family occurred in 2006 near Yusufiya, about 20 miles south of Baghdad. Steven Green was found guilty earlier this month of raping a 14-year-old Iraqi girl and of murdering her, her parents and her 6-year-old sister.
