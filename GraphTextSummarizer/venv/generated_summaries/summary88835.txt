Italian 14th seed Flavia Pennetta defeated Romania's Sorona Cirstea 6-1 1-6 6-1 to earn a second-round clash with German Andrea Petkovic. In the final first-round ties, world No. 2 Caroline Wozniacki crushed qualifier Petra Kvitova 6-4 6-2, while third-ranked Dinara Safina suffered a shock 7-6 7-6 defeat against Czech qualifier Kara Zakopalova. But 10th seed Victoria Azarenka of Belarus retired while 3-0 down against Chinese wildcard Shuai Peng, who will now play Israel's Shahar Peer or Russian Alisa Kleybanova. In Monday's other second-round clashes, Serena's fourth-seeded older sister Venus beat Russia's Vera Zvonareva 7-5 6-3. Venus next faces Italian 15th seed Francesca Schiavone, who crushed Austria's Sybille Bammer 6-2 6-1.
