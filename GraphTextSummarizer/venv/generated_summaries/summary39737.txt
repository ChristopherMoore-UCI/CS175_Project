Hancock, a longtime volunteer for the organization, and Miller decided John should get involved in sports after they saw a rare sight: Whenever John was running or being active, he was smiling. Sports completely changed John, and Miller said that is because of Hancock. Miller's mind raced: Would John would be OK in front of hundreds of cheering spectators? In May 2014, John's training brought him to the Special Olympics State Summer Games in Orlando. Their relationship evolved when Hancock introduced John to the Special Olympics in Martin County.
