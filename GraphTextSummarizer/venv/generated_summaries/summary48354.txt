On Monday, St. Louis Police Chief Sam Dotson said police are searching for a fourth teen. A third teen is in custody in the death of a man beaten with a hammer on a St. Louis street, and police are looking for a fourth suspect. The victim, 32-year-old Zemir Begic, suffered injuries to his head, abdomen, face and mouth, according to police. Begic was taken to an area hospital, where he was pronounced dead, police said. The third juvenile, a 17-year-old male, turned himself in to police, the St. Louis Police Department said.
