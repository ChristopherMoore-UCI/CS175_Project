The non-profit has been working closely with projects and regulatory bodies to aid experimental green design construction. The LBC was created to encourage the creation of living buildings, sites and communities globally as well as educate people about the importance of going green. Founded by Jason F. McLennan in 2006 through the Cascadia Green Building Council, the Living Building Challenge (LBC) is a green ratings system for design and construction that judges a building based on its actual performance, not just its projected performance at the design stage. We knew in reality buildings typically don't perform the way they are intended so we wanted to rectify this and base our challenge on reality."
