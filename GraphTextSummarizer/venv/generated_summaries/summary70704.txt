-- He claimed he won the prize this week, and now it's official: Pedro Quezada of New Jersey is the winner of Saturday's $338 million Powerball jackpot, the New Jersey Lottery said Tuesday. For winner, unexpected change can come with those dollars One reporter asked whether he planned to stay in New Jersey, while others inquired whether he wanted to return to the Dominican Republic to celebrate with family and friends. Quezada announced on Monday that he held the winning ticket, but the final word from lottery officials didn't come until Tuesday.
