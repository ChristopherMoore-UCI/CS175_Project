As a black family, the Jenkinses understood the rage that seethed and then exploded on the streets of Ferguson after a grand jury decided not to indict Officer Darren Wilson, who is white, in the death of black teenager Michael Brown. After word spread that Wilson would not be indicted, protesters clashed with riot police in Ferguson.
