The Israeli military has said that it has depleted most of Gaza's rocket supply and Israel Defense Forces says more than 3,000 rockets have been fired from Gaza at Israel. Unlike Gaza, Israel has bomb shelters and an advanced warning system to let its population know when and where rockets are coming in. Uneven resources While Hamas has shown itself to be a formidable guerrilla army, Israel's military dwarfs the resources available to the militant Islamic organization, which controls Gaza. In some cases, Israel has said Hamas rockets were launched and fell short, landing on the schools, and stressed that it uses "precision" airstrikes. A goal that could take time One of Israel's top goals is to destroy Hamas rockets and tunnels.
