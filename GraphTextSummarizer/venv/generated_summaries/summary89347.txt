Swiss side FC Basel beat Dnipro Dnipropetrovsk, of Ukraine, 2-0 while VfB Stuttgart drew 1-1 with Belgian side Racing Genk in Germany. Borussia Monchengladbach shared six goals with Italian side Lazio during their first leg in Germany, Libor Kozak scoring a 94th minute equalizer for the visiting side. Two free kick goals from Gareth Bale -- the second in the last ten seconds of the match -- helped Tottenham to a 2-1 victory over French side Lyon in London. Oleg Gusev missed a late penalty as Ukrainian club Dynamo Kiev had to settle for a 1-1 draw with French side Bordeaux. Czech side Viktoria Plzen recorded a shock 3-0 win at Napoli, who are currently second in the Italian league.
