To win over Hispanic voters, both President Obama and the GOP nominee need to smother the kryptonite that the issue of immigration has become with a lead blanket of comprehensive immigration reform, supported by strong majorities of Hispanic swing voters and a majority of independents and the general public. Between January 2008 and November 4, 2008, Hispanic voter registration in North Carolina grew by 62%, from 42,000 to 68,000. Since then, Hispanic voter registration in North Carolina has nearly doubled to 130,615. Three important points about Hispanic swing voters: -- Hispanics lean Democratic, but it's not a base Democratic vote. Any candidate or campaign that ignores Hispanic swing voters does it at their peril.
