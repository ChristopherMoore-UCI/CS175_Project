Android device users will be able to use video and voice chat with any Google Talk contact using another Android devices (phones or tablets) or Google Talk on Gmail. Video chat via Google Talk will be available on other Android 2.3 and higher devices soon. Google is beginning its rollout of Google Talk video chat to Android devices. During voice or video chat, text chats from that contact are overlaid on the device screen. Video and voice chat first came to Gmail late in 2008 and to iGoogle in 2009.
