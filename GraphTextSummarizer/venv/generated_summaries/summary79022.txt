The Oprah plane will fly United customers between cities in the airline's domestic network through May 2011. "As Chicago's hometown airline, United is proud to celebrate The Oprah Winfrey Show's Farewell Season with our customers, employees and 'Oprah' show fans," said Mark Bergsrud, senior vice president of marketing for United, in a statement. -- The Queen of Talk's influence is reaching new heights with the unveiling of a plane marking the 25th and final season of the "Oprah Winfrey Show." An onboard video featuring Oprah Winfrey will greet customers. During its inaugural flight from Chicago, Illinois, one ticketed passenger will receive enough United miles for a trip around the world.
