To be sure, doctors need to be more effective communicators, and we need a health care system that appropriately rewards partnerships with patients. But patients can't afford to wait for doctors to improve and the health care system to be reformed. Devise a strategy with your doctor for narrowing down the list of possible diagnoses. More than 100,000 Americans die from medical error every year, with the majority of error attributed to mistakes in diagnosis. Studies have shown that more than 80% of diagnoses can be made based on history alone.
