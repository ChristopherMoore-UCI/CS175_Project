-- A massive emergency response operation is under way in northern Japan, with world governments and international aid groups coming together to bring relief to the beleaguered island nation. In addition to the response from world governments, humanitarian assistance groups have sent teams to hard-hit areas across Japan. Peace Winds has expert responders on their way to remote areas of northern Japan, where they plan to distribute large emergency tents, water, food and blankets, according to Mercy Corps' website. Mercy Corps International teamed with Peace Winds Japan to rush aid to affected regions. Stephen Valley of U.S. Forces Japan.
