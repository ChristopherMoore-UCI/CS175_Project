-- A suspect is in custody in a case involving the beating of a gay man during Detroit's Motor City Pride festival that was caught on cell phone video, according to Detroit police. Police say they are are still looking for help to identify the other men shown in the video beating Christin Howard, a 20-year-old hairdresser, on June 8. Howard told CNN affiliate WXYZ he was taking a picture with a friend when things suddenly turned violent. At one point I thought they were going to kill me," Howard said.
