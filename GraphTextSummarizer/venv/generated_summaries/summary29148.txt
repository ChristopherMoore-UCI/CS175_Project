Northern white rhinos and southern white rhinos are different subspecies genetically. Of the handful of northern white rhinos left worldwide, a few are at a wildlife conservancy in Kenya. A northern white rhino has died at a San Diego zoo, leaving only five worldwide and bringing the species closer to extinction. Last year, the International Union for Conservation of Nature declared the northern white rhino as "teetering on the brink of extinction. Experts say that rhino horn is becoming more lucrative than drugs.
