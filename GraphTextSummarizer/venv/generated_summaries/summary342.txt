There are two possible ways Apple could open up the Apple TV platform to third-party app developers: It could allow devs to create dedicated TV apps, or it could more heavily leverage (and open up) the AirPlay feature already baked into Apple TV to port existing iPad apps to the big screen. Now, granted, Apple just revamped the Apple TV user interface with the launch of the third-generation iPad. Nonetheless, Apple TV sales are picking up momentum: Even six months ago, Apple TV was the big snoozer in Apple's consumer hardware line-up. On the eve of WWDC 2012 -- Apple's annual developers' conference -- speculation swirls around Apple's positioning as a television manufacturer, and how Apple TV might play into a greater television hardware strategy.
