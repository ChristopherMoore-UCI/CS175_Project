Bachelor's degree programs in fine arts or graphic design are offered at many colleges, universities and private design schools. Career: Interior designer  A bachelor's degree in graphic design is often required for most graphic design positions. Of the 1,601,368 bachelor's degrees awarded by degree-granting institutions, 346,972 were for degree in business, management, marketing and related support services. Business was the most popular degree in 2007-2008, according to the 2010 Digest of Education Statistics from the U.S. Department of Education. SOURCE: U.S. Department of Education, National Center for Education Statistics.
