Blu Bar on 36, Shangri-La Hotel in The Rocks, Sydney, Australia Set on the 36th floor of the Shangri-La Hotel in The Rocks, the New York-inspired bar offers views of Darling Harbour, Sydney Harbour Bridge and the Opera House. The latter features minimalist décor, plush seating, dim lighting and floor-to-ceiling glass windows. (Ayana Resort and Spa, Jimbaran, Bali, tel: +62 361 702222, website: ayanaresort.com/rockbarbali.) Bemelmans Bar, Carlyle Hotel, New York City Dark and decadent, Bemelmans competes for the city's most dapper nightspot. Designed by Chilean Sergio Echeverria and New Yorker Tony Chi, the space puts a modern twist on traditional Chilean décor.
