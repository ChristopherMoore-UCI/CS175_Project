The Russian-made Sukhoi Su-22, a fighter jet, was on a training mission when it crashed in southern Sanaa, Yemeni state TV reported. Journalist Hakim Almasmari in Sanaa and CNN's Yousuf Basil in Atlanta contributed to this report. Last November, a military cargo plane caught fire and crashed in the city, killing 10 people. It was the third fatal air crash in Sanaa in the past year. In February, a military fighter jet on a training mission went down in a residential section of Sanaa, killing at least 10 people and injuring 21.
