The Haehres heard that Dayana and Moise might be among a small group of Haitian children whose adoptions were set that the U.S. military would fly to Florida. Their children were indeed among the small group of children from the orphanage being evacuated to the United States. The flight was heading for an airport in central Florida. The Haehre family looks like any other family on vacation, but this unplanned trip to Florida was where their brood grew from two children to four. -- Dayana and Moise smile as they shovel chips, with a mix of sand, into their mouths on a beach in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.
