"Art Official Age" is being billed as Prince's latest solo effort, while "PlectrumElectrum" is the first release from his band 3rdEyeGirl. Rapper of the moment Kendrick Lamar joined the Purple One on stage, and the event included a rare tour of Paisley Park, Prince's legendary studio complex, where most of his recording over the years has taken place. Prince also sought a greater social media influence, joining Facebook Tuesday to host a live Q&A with fans. The release marks Prince's return to Warner Bros. Records, which released many of the star's early hits. The Grammy winner kicked off the year with a guest star stint on the post-Super Bowl episode of "New Girl," even releasing a duet with the show's star, Zooey Deschanel.
