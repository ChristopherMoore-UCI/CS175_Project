One group of images, taken by Reeva's friend Kerry Smith eight years before her death, show the then 21-year-old law student and modeling wannabe posing on the beach at Port Elizabeth, South Africa, where she grew up -- and where her grieving parents scattered her ashes. "That's Reeva in her natural beauty," Smith said. That's a view of the model shared by another photographer, Gareth Barclay, who took a series of pictures of Reeva in the months before her death. Her dark hair, freckles and innocent good looks are a world away from the shots of a bikini-clad Reeva featured in glossy magazines which millions around the world are now familiar with.
