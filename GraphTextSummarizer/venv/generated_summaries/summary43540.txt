Meet the man behind the laws of power Robert Greene, author of "The 48 Laws," says our fascination with power is rooted in our DNA. The best-selling book offers a collection of 48 laws that show people how to gain power, preserve it, and defend themselves against those powerful people who make their lives miserable. Greene says he received his next lesson in power when he began working as a writer in Hollywood. One of Greene's central claims is that everyone must learn the rules of power.
