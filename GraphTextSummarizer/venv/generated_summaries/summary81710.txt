Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago (March 3 and 4) Officially, carnival in Trinidad's Port of Spain is the two days before Ash Wednesday. It's carnival season, that pre-Lent period of collective frenzy that celebrates overindulgence and transgression before Ash Wednesday ushers in a more sober time of prayer, penance and abstention in the weeks leading up to Easter.
