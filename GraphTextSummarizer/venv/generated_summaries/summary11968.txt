The album debuted at No. 15 on the Billboard Top 200 and No. 1 on both the Billboard Current Electronic Album Chart and iTunes Electronic Chart. "It's hard for me to believe it's real," said Anthony Gonzales, the synth-pop mastermind behind M83. Throughout all the acclaim and spotlight this new album has brought on M83, Gonzales focused on his fans: "I want them to be happy. Gonzales was born in 1980, so being inspired by his childhood also means he's enthralled by the music of the '80s. In songs like "Teen Angst" and albums like "Saturdays=Youth," M83 often incorporates ideas of childhood into its work.
