-- Former ESPN writer Anthony Federico apologized Monday for writing an offensive headline about basketball sensation Jeremy Lin, calling it "an honest mistake." "I'd love to tell Jeremy what happened and explain that this was an honest mistake," Federico told the New York Daily News in an interview published Monday. ESPN apologized Saturday stating, "We again apologize, especially to Mr. Lin. Lin addressed the controversy during a news conference Sunday following the Knicks' 104-97 win over the Dallas Mavericks, the defending national champions.
