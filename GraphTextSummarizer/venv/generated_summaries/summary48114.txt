The tiny country of Bhutan, a very happy country famous for measuring the "Gross National Happiness" of its people, gets the credit for focusing world attention on happiness: Its Prime Minister proposed the idea of a World Happiness Day to the United Nations in 2011. This officially designated happy date marked its fourth year last month. New Zealand New to the top 10 list of happiest countries, New Zealand has plenty of reasons to celebrate.
