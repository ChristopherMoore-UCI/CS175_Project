Fondren's gym is also open to all women every Saturday, and she frequently teaches fitness classes at area schools and churches. So in October 2009, Fondren kicked off Shape Up Vicksburg. As a successful real estate developer, Fondren largely funds the effort herself, but the initiative also receives donations from local businesses.
