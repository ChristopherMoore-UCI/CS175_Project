In addition to the organic forms of underwater and plant life, Howe points to the illustrated books of 19th century author Jules Verne as early inspiration. "My job was mostly putting up steel shelving, so I was surrounded by steel. " Like these creations, Howe's works transform the organic movement of the natural world into cold steel -- yet they retain their own hypnotic mystery. Howe has been making kinetic sculptures since 1990. -- Anthony Howe makes sculptures that refuse to stay still.
