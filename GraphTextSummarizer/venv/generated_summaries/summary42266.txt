It was long time waiting on standby but both the United Arab Emirates and Qatar did get what they were looking for; an upgrade to emerging market status from the more risky rating of frontier market. This process however required some fundamental changes to operations within the Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Doha financial markets. On yet another dismal day for emerging markets, Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Doha rallied again. "They have actually ticked a lot of boxes that previously were stopping the UAE and Qatar," according to Saleem Khokhar of National Bank of Abu Dhabi Asset Management. According to MSCI, $1.5 trillion is earmarked by fund managers into emerging market assets.
