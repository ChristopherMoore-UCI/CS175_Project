Kibbutz caviar Iran, Azerbaijan and Russia may be the global leaders in caviar production, but sturgeon populations in the Caspian Sea have been dropping and commercial fishing has been banned. -- IN FOCUS: Iran's economy Iran hosts a group of 120 developing countries this week, bringing together nations of the Non-Aligned Movement. Iran currently holds the three-year presidency, and at the top of the agenda are the Western sanctions imposed on Iran. Kibbutz Dan in Northern Israel is raising around 70,000 of the fish, with each producing thousands of dollars worth of caviar. The movement was set up in 1961 as a counter-weight to the dominating Cold War powers.
