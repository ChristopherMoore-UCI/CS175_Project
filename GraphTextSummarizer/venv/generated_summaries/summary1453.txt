Teams of five would compete against others from around the country to have the highest percentage of weight loss after 12 weeks. Of course, money isn't the only motivator in weight loss challenges. HealthyWage co-founder David Roddenberry started the company in 2009 after seeing a study in the Journal of the American Medical Association that showed financial incentives could encourage weight loss. Last year, a Mayo Clinic study found people who received financial incentives stuck with healthy behaviors longer -- losing, on average, seven more pounds than their colleagues whose wallets weren't linked to weight loss. Would you lose weight if money were at stake?
