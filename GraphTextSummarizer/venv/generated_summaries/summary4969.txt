The bureau chief for Reuters in Afghanistan and Pakistan, Maria Golovnina, died Monday after falling unconscious, her employer said. She joined the company in 2001 as a foreign correspondent in a number of places, including London, Singapore, Moscow, Afghanistan and Iraq. CNN's Sophia Saifi in Islamabad, Pakistan, and Chieu Luu in Hong Kong contributed to this report. "Maria was a superb journalist with a long and varied career at Reuters," the news agency said. She had been bureau chief for Afghanistan and Pakistan since 2013.
