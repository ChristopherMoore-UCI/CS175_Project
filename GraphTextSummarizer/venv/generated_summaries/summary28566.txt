Click here for a rival view Paul Begala says McCain's VP choice,  Alaska Gov. Sarah Palin, is completely unqualified to be president. Editor's note: Paul Begala, a Democratic strategist and CNN political contributor, was a political consultant for Bill Clinton's presidential campaign in 1992 and was counselor to Clinton in the White House. In his first presidential decision, John McCain has shown that he is willing to endanger his country, potentially leaving it in the hands of someone who simply has no business being a heartbeat away from the most powerful, complicated, difficult job in human history. In choosing Alaska Gov. Sarah Palin to be his running mate, he is not thinking "outside the box," as some have said. He passed over former congressman, trade negotiator and budget director Rob Portman.
