-- Great evil often brings out the best in good men, men like Todd Beamer on Flight 93, Medal of Honor recipient Michael Murphy in Afghanistan and now the Aurora three -- the three young men, each in different parts of theater nine, who gave their lives to protect their girlfriends. This isn't to take anything away from Blunk or the other two heroes, but to illustrate that, in spite of shortcomings, men can still recognize what it means to be a good man and act like one. Remembering the Colorado shooting victims From all accounts, these young men were average, working men in their 20s. Record numbers of men aren't working or even looking for work. It is only by telling their story that this code of honor will survive for future generations of men.
