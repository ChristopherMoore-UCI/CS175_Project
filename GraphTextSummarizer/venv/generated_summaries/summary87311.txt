Reese Witherspoon says she wants to banish the myth that young women are not at risk for breast cancer. We must also banish the myth that young women are not at risk for breast cancer. When breast cancer first develops, there are usually no symptoms, which is why women need to perform self-exams regularly and contact their doctor upon noticing even the smallest change. I fight for my mother, myself, my children and future generations of women, so one day we will not have to be afraid of breast cancer. Of course talking about breast cancer and breast health is a personal thing.
