Yemen State TV announced the death in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, of Abdul Aziz Abdul Ghani, the president of the Shura Council, and announced the start of a three-day mourning period. Ghani's death came as hundreds of security forces were deployed to the streets of the capital, Sanaa, amid fears that the imminent toppling of the regime in Libya could have an effect on Yemen. Along with President Ali Abdullah Saleh, he had been receiving treatment in Saudi Arabia since June 3. Yemen lost a leader and the government holds the Ahmar family responsible for his death.
