In other tennis news on Thursday, tickets for the first match to be played under the new roof on Wimbledon's centre court sold out in just five minutes. Tennis superstar Roger Federer and longtime partner Mirka Vavrinec are expecting their first baby this summer, the Swiss ace revealed on his personal Web site on Thursday. Former world number one Federer is in Indian Wells in California for the first  Masters Series event of the season, his first tournament since his emotional defeat to Rafael Nadal in the final of the Australian Open. Husband-and-wife team Andre Agassi and Steffi Graf, both former Wimbledon champions, will be playing mixed doubles when the roof is officially opened on May 17. "I arrived in Indian Wells earlier this week and I am eager to get back out on court," Federer said.
