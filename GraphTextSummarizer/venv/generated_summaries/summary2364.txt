The consequences of climate change require every community to make changes that will increase safety and survivability. In post-Hurricane Katrina New Orleans, we are building affordable homes designed to use less energy, reduce carbon emissions and run on solar power, as well as weather the next storm. Governments were formed to protect their citizens -- and not just from armed marauders. Climate change is real and happening now. -- Copenhagen, Denmark, is 5,000 miles away from New Orleans, Louisiana.
