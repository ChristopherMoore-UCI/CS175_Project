Slagthuset is Scandinavia's biggest nightclub. A vibrant club scene exists in Malmo, which is home to Slagthuset (Jorgen Kocksgatan 7A) -- the largest nightclub in Scandinavia. Local musicians hang out at hip Tempo Bar and Kok (Sodra Skolgatan 30). -- Whether you're looking for a laid-back evening or want to party the night away, there are plenty of bars and clubs in Malmo to keep you entertained.
