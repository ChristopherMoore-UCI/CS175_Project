The water above Gorilla flows with immense power through a narrow slot that racers call "the notch." In addition to bragging rights, race winners come away with handmade glass trophies. But it's the daring required to participate that earns Green River Narrows Race competitors a badge of honor in boating circles. The athletic skill and white-knuckled bravery possessed by Green River Narrows whitewater kayak racers can only be described as impressive. Organization of race logistics and details is homegrown; there are no emergency medical services available, and the entire affair is basically DIY.
