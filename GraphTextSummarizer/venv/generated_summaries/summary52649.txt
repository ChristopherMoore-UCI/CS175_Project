What if we took some of this soap and recycled it, made brand new soap from it and then sent it home to people who couldn't afford soap?' Derreck Kayongo and his Atlanta-based Global Soap Project collect used hotel soap from across the United States. Last summer, Kayongo personally delivered 5,000 bars of soap to Kenya Relief's Brittney's Home of Grace orphanage. " For Kayongo, collecting soap is "a first line of defense" mission to combat child-mortality around the world. Each year, hundreds of millions of soap bars are discarded in North America alone.
