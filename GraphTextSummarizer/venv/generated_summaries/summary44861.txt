The club also accused Karimi, 31,  of insulting Iranian Football Federation officials. According to Steel Azin Football Club's website, star player Ali Karimi was fired from the club after dismissing several warnings by a club official. According to Iran's semi-official ISNA news agency, Karimi denied any wrongdoing of that he had insulted Iran's Football Federation. After a four-year stint with UAE-based side Al-Ahli Karimi moved to Bundesliga side Bayern Munich where he played in the title-winning side led by Felix Magath. Karimi -- dubbed the 'Maradona of Asia' and the 'Wizard of Tehran' -- is one of the most recognizable faces in Iranian sports.
