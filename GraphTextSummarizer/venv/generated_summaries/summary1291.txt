-- Gunmen who arrived by boats launched attacks on at least seven locations in Mumbai, India, on Wednesday night, killing more than 120 people, authorities say. The following is what is known about the attacks: • Gunmen arrived by boats at the Mumbai waterfront near the Gateway of India monument on Wednesday night, police said. As police rushed to the scene of the attacks, gunmen attacked the Cama Hospital for women and infants. At least nine gunmen were killed in fighting with police by Friday morning. • Gunmen also took hostages at the Chabad House, where several Jewish families live, police said.
