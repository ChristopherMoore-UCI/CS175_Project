"The study is not to point fingers at people, it wasn't to focus on the countries,"  said lead author Jenna Jambeck. Low-income countries were less likely to end up on the list, because of less waste, Jambeck said. Eight of the top 10 countries with mismanaged plastic waste are in Asia. Meanwhile, plastic waste keeps on growing with more people and more consumption, the report authors wrote. However, some countries lack infrastructure for waste management and recycling.
