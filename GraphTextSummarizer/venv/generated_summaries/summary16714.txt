Relatives of passengers of the plane that crashed arrive at Marseille airport in southern France. On June 1, an Air France Airbus A330 crashed off Brazil while en route from Rio de Janeiro to Paris, France. The plane, carrying more than 150 people, was en route to Moroni, the capital of Comoros, from Yemen's capital, Sanaa. There were 142 passengers and 11 crew members aboard, Yemenia Air officials said. An official at Charles De Gaulle airport in Paris said there were 66 French passengers aboard.
