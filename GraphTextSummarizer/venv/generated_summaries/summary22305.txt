Fox has ordered a new Ramsay series where the reality kingpin will partner with a team of hospitality experts, traveling across the country to try and fix every aspect of struggling hotels, motels and bed & breakfasts. Ramsay has hotel experience, having studied hotel management as a young man, and having worked and owned hotel-based restaurants throughout his career. The series is both familiar territory for Ramsay and a real departure, combining the business makeover format of "Kitchen Nightmares" with the internationally known chef moving beyond a cooking-centric show on Fox for the first time.
