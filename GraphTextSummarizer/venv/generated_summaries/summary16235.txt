In the first incident in Kaduna, a man drove a vehicle into a two-story building that houses three national newspapers: ThisDay, The Moment and The Sun. The third blast, in the northern state of Kaduna, was confirmed by emergency management officials, but no information on casualties or damage was available. The targets of the first two bombs were newspaper offices, the country's president said. Within 30 minutes of the first blast in Kaduna, a suicide bomber in the central city of Abuja detonated a bomb that killed four people, including himself, said the Red Cross, which described the explosions as "the first direct attack on the media by (an) unknown armed group. Other bystanders caught the suspected bomber and turned him over to police.
