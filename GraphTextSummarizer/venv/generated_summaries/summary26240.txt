Lead poisoning can cause severe developmental problems in children ranging from anemia to stomach pains and permanent brain damage. In the case of lead and other metals, contamination may be more widespread than many local villagers can anticipate. Lead poisoning is a devastating side effect of industrial pollution. According to doctors, pregnant women can also pass lead poisoning on to their unborn children.
