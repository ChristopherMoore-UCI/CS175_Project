Novara moved out of the bottom three with a 2-1 win over Parma, as striker Raffaele and midfielder Marco Rigoni scored in the final 20 minutes after veteran captain Matteo Centurioni put through his own net in the first half. It put the Turin club one point clear of Udinese, who beat Roma 2-0 on Friday, and three ahead of Rome side Lazio. Midfielder Simone Pepe netted the only goal from close range in the 34th minute after a cross by striker Alessandro Matri.
