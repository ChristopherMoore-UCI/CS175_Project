Tours teach you how to travel Tour guides are old hands at travel. Tours are floaties for world travelers -- they allow you to dip your toes in the pool without the fear of getting in over your head. There are a number of reasons even seasoned travelers shouldn't be afraid of tours. While independent travel is great, many places in the world can't be accessed by solo travelers due to license and government restrictions. Tour guides create a more meaningful experience I've had some amazing guides in my time.
