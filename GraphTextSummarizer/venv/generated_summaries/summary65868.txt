No. 2 Oregon routed No. 3 Florida State 59-20 in the Rose Bowl in the first semifinal, while No. 4 Ohio State upset No. 1 Alabama 42-35 in the Sugar Bowl, the second semifinal. The top four seeds were, in order, Alabama, Oregon, Florida State and Ohio State. No. 2 Oregon will square off against No. 4 Ohio State for the national championship on Monday night. The city of Oregon, which is near Toledo, announced it would be known as "Oregon, Ohio Buckeyes on the Bay, City of Duck Hunters." Oregon and Ohio State will play for the national championship at AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas.
