Gazelles These are classic entrepreneurs -- those who start new businesses and aim to become hot phenomena. The message: entrepreneurs operating within large corporations go out of their way to stink up the joint. Nonprofits need to employ more business techniques. Job security these days depends on the same qualities that make good entrepreneurs -- agility, imagination, persistence and execution. The last group that needs to be more entrepreneurial are small-scale or lifestyle entrepreneurs -- plumbers, yoga instructors, bakers, craft makers.
