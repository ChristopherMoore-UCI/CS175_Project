Drivers' standings after second F1 race of season in Malaysia: 1. -- World champion Sebastian Vettel has taken an early stranglehold on this year's championship after his second straight win in the Malaysian Grand Prix at Sepang. Formula One teams, drivers and circuits The German has 50 points, 24 ahead of Button, with Lewis Hamilton and Mark Webber, who was fourth in Malaysia, four further points adrift. Hamilton, who had a difficult afternoon, crossed the line in seventh, but was relegated to eighth after being penalized for a coming together with Ferrari's Fernando Alonso. Red Bull's Vettel led from start to finish to come home ahead of McLaren's Jenson Button with Nick Heidfeld giving Lotus Renault their second podium of the season.
