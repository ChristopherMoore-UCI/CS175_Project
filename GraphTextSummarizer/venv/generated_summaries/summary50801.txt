Environmental awareness has pushed denim laundries to improve techniques for bleaching and coating jeans to give them different looks, Corrente said. "They're the most unique piece of clothing everyone owns because they keep changing as you wear them," said Angelika Corrente, who runs Denimhead, global trend forecaster WGSN's denim division. This year's trends are marked by a hybrid appreciation for fads of other eras. May 20, 1873, is considered the birthday of blue jeans. By the 1980s, as high fashion brands began to introduce the concept of designer jeans, the shape and fit began to slim down.
