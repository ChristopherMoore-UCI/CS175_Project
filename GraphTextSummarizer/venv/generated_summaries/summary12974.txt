Mine roofs collapse for many reasons, such as fault lines in the strata, inadequate bolting or other mechanical controls, inadequate pillar size or improper mining methods. In underground mines, stored energy in the form of  earth surrounds the worker. Mining exposes workers to lethal amounts of energy. The rescue of 33 trapped men in Chile brings home vividly the hazards of mining. The truth is this: Mining is hazardous but essential to the lives we want.
