Undeterred, she played ball in high school despite rounds of chemotherapy for her inoperable tumor, CNN affiliate WKRC reported. A few weeks before she found out, she'd signed up to play basketball at Mount Saint Joseph. Hill will join her new teammates for her first college game against Hiram College. The 19-year-old freshman at Mount Saint Joseph University was diagnosed with a brain tumor in 2013. After doctors told her she doesn't have much time left, the university asked the NCAA if it can move its season opener by two weeks to November 2, according to the affiliate.
