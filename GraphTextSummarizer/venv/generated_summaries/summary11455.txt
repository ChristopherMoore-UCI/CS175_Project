One tornado caused "about a mile of destruction," said Sheriff Bill Pribil of Coconino County. None of the injuries in Coconino County were believed to be serious, Pribil said. Radar continued to show several thunderstorms in central and north-central Arizona capable of producing tornadoes with little or no advance notice, CNN Meteorologist Sean Morris said. Both tornadoes touched down in the town of Bellemont, the National Weather Service reported.
