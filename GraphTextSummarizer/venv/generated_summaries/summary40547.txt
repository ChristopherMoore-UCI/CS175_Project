Thulo is one of the subjects featured in "Staff Riding," a five-minute digital documentary by Italian filmmaker Marco Casino about a subculture of train surfers in Katlehong -- one of South Africa's largest and poorest townships ("staff riding" is the local slang for train surfing). After stumbling upon an online video of the world's 10 most dangerous sports that listed train surfing as near the top, Casino became curious about the practice. The illegal "sport" attracts many of the community's young men, who perform tricks and dance moves on top of moving trains.
