Aside from serving as instant fodder for the news media, quizzing the public tells us little about the state of science literacy in the United States. Science literacy isn't remembering a bunch of facts. Still, one can't simply dismiss the Indicators data, in light of the very real problem the country faces: The state of science literacy has been in steep decline for a half-century. Only then will we cultivate a culture of science literacy. More recently, budgetary constraints and the transforming media environment led to major cuts in science reporting.
