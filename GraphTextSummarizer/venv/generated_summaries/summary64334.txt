Malema was expelled from South Africa's ruling African National Congress this year in the wake of a speech he made that was critical of President Jacob Zuma. "It is under President Zuma that we have seen the youth of the ANC being traumatized, being expelled from their own home," Malema said, according to an ANC statement. We have seen, under President Zuma, democracy being replaced with dictatorship. "It is under President Zuma we have seen a critical voice being suppressed. In his comments, Malema suggested that Zuma was running the party in a dictatorial and intolerant way.
