Improving communication Community participation and engagement in risk reduction is vital and the tsunami cast a spotlight on the need to improve accountability and two-way communication with crisis affected communities. Working with communities to help them be better prepared to face future disasters was the thread that ran through the IFRC's recovery efforts after the tsunami. This represents a unique opportunity to re-focus attention on the need for greater investment in community level disaster risk reduction. One clear lesson reinforced by the tsunami has been the importance of investing in disaster risk reduction (DRR) at both global and local levels.
