The infographic, which was created in response the lack of female representation in "The Evolution of the Geek" -- a flowchart that was passed around the web last year -- caught some flak from sites like TechCrunch and ComputerWeekly.com. The flow chart comes complete with arrows, cartoons and five possible answers in the form of female tech influencers. "Female success has been reduced to a series of key decisions concerning shoes ... and white wine spritzers," Jenny Williams of ComputerWeekly.com wrote. The last option, "Chicago Cubs fan," points directly to Evans, who, ironically, supports the rival St. Louis Cardinals.
