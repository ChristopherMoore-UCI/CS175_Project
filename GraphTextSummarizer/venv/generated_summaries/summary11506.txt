Justice Louis Brandeis, in a 1927 Supreme Court case, extolled the virtues of "counter-speech" to address objectionable speech. Discussions about privacy usually revolve around consumer privacy and protection. Hate speech can harden low self-esteem and intimidate its targets -- and even lead people to commit suicide. Speak up: Clicking to report hate speech to an online host is not all we can do. Speak up to counter the lies of hate speech or the inappropriate online conduct directed at minorities.
