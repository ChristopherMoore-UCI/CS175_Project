-- Tropical storm Ida could regain hurricane status Saturday night as it heads toward the Gulf Coast. Ida made landfall as a hurricane Thursday morning over east central Nicaragua, pelting the Central American nation with heavy rain before weakening to a tropical storm. Track Ida's projected path Forecasters expect the storm, with winds of 70 mph (110 kph), to reach hurricane status by Saturday night or early Sunday.
