Identical twins and breakdancers-turned-graffiti artist duo Os Gemeos used 1,200 cans of spray paint to emblazon their signature colorful style over a Boeing 737. Brazilian low-cost carrier GOL commissioned the plane, which will fly the Brazilian national soccer team around the country during the FIFA World Cup starting June 12. More photos and a video of the twins at work on the plane can be seen on GOL's blog. They take their national soccer team's plane for the World Cup and turn it into a work of brilliant graffiti art.
