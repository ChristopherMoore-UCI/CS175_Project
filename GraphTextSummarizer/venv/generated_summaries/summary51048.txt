Russia's finance minister has played down the impact of a dialing-back of the U.S. Federal Reserve's stimulus program, saying the country will weather it better than other developing economies. "Russia, as opposed to other developing countries, will be less impacted by that occurrence," he said. The country's economy is already under pressure from investment outflows, lower commodity prices and a sinking ruble.
