Other dog shelters and re-homing centers across the country are also at bursting point as a result of an increase in requests for re-homing and a decrease in people wanting to take on the responsibility of looking after a dog. Another concern for dog charities is that animals are given as Christmas presents. At this time last year, we had half as many dogs," says manager Richard Moore. On top of overcrowding, animal rescue charities face another problem; they rely on charity donations and are likely to suffer in those terms. We have more dogs coming into the center than going out.
