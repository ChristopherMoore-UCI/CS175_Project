Mai grew up in a small village in the Punjab region of Pakistan, where she never went to school and was forced into marriage at the age 13. While the majority of rapes go unreported in Pakistan, according to Pakistani national newspaper The Express Tribune, Mai was determined not to stay silent. Convinced that lack of education contributed to the poor treatment of women, Mai established a girls' school, initially in a single room of her family home with a just one teacher and three students, including herself. However Mai said the school has received no government funding for the last three years and is  struggling for income.
