Mike Hollman is an award winning photographer based in Auckland, New Zealand. Named New Zealand Travel Photographer of the Year in 2013 by the New Zealand Institute of Professional Photography, Hollman grew up on the South Island and says the landscape has had a tremendous impact on his work. Glenorchy-Queenstown Road The 45-kilometer road between Glenorchy and Queenstown may be the most scenic in New Zealand. Church of the Good Shepherd, Tekapo Located in Mackenzie Country in central South Island, Tekapo is renowned for its stunning clear skies and was declared an International Dark Sky Reserve in 2012. A Fellow and Master of Photography with The New Zealand Institute of Professional Photography, he was named New Zealand Travel Photographer of the Year in 2013.
