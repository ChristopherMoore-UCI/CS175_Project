She became just the fourth women's player in the Open Era to claim the same tournament at least seven times, joining retired legends Martina Navratilova, Steffi Graf and Chris Evert. World No. 1 Rafael Nadal meets Novak Djokovic -- a day after both received walkovers into the finale. Kei Nishikori couldn't take on Djokovic because of a groin injury while Tomas Berdych was suffering from a stomach illness and withdrew against Nadal. Playing close to her home in Florida, Williams started slowly before picking things up and beating Li Na 75 61 for a record seventh title at the Sony Open. "It was a really important victory, especially playing Li Na, who has been playing so well," Williams told the crowd.
