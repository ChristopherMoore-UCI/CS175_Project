Morsy, backed by the Muslim Brotherhood and the nation's first democratically elected president, was forced out of office in July, 2013, with detractors saying he was a tyrant trying to impose conservative values. The military and police have detained large numbers of Brotherhood officials and supporters since the Morsy's ouster. Morsy is in custody, facing charges of incitement to murder in connection with protests against his rule last December.
