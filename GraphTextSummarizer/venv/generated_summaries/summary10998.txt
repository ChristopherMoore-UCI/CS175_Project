-- A gunman went on a shooting spree in the Serbian town of Velika Ivanca on Tuesday, killing at least 13 people, including a small child, according to Police Chief Milorad Veljovic. Two people remained hospitalized, one of them likely the suspected shooter, Tanjug reported, citing Veljovic. Veljovic said the gunman attempted to kill his wife and take his own life, according to Tanjug. Twelve of the victims died immediately, another died at a Belgrade hospital, Serbian news agency Tanjug reported. Investigators don't know what sparked the killings, Veljovic said.
