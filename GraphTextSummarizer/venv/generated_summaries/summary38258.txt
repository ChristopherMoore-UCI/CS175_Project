In 2010, the year that saw the highest number of drone strikes, according to TBIJ, Pakistan government figures suggest the country witnessed its highest number of suicide attacks -- more than 2,100 civilians were killed without distinction between women, children, law enforcement or military. Pakistan has also experienced a spike in suicide attacks when there has been a surge in drone attacks. Drones decimating Taliban in Pakistan What astounds me is the belief expressed by some scholars and politicians that drones are the only viable option for combating terrorism or militancy in the tribal areas of Pakistan. The government of Pakistan has consistently maintained that drone attacks are a violation of its territorial integrity and sovereignty. Under the authority of the U.S. President, drone attacks on Pakistani territory have been carried out by the CIA and U.S. military since 2004.
