In 2006, Damon co-founded H2O Africa Foundation, which served to bring attention to clean water initiatives in Africa. The nonprofit provides affordable access to safe water and proper lavatory facilities through microfinance loans. Without the loans, they are forced to turn to the water mafia, who, according to Damon, dramatically mark up the cost of water.
