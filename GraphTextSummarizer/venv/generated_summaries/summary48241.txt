Iranian sailors helped scare off armed pirates who attacked an American cargo ship in the Gulf of Oman, Iranian state media reported Thursday. EU forces attack pirate targets on Somali shore The incident occurred northeast of Fujairah, a port for refueling oil tankers, the Fars News Agency said. The United States, Iran and other nations have been intent on repelling pirates operating in the Gulf of Aden and other bodies of water. Iranian navy vessels received a distress signal from the U.S. cargo ship Maersk Texas during patrols. The U.S. ship crew thanked the Iranian naval force and continued on its way, state media reported.
