Almost every village on Cape Hatteras National Seashore rents kayaks, and he recommends paddling along the shore to "bliss out. If you go: There is no admission to enter Cape Hatteras National Seashore, and there are visitor centers on Cape Hatteras and on Bodie and Ocracoke islands. Gamman says rip currents are common at Hatteras because of small sand dunes that build up in shallow water. Cape Hatteras and Bodie Island lighthouses are open from the third Friday in April through Columbus Day. The Ocracoke, Hatteras and Bodie Island lighthouses were built and rebuilt throughout the 19th century to help ships avoid the "Graveyard of the Atlantic," and the Wright Brothers made history's first recorded flight in Kill Devil Hills at the beginning of the 20th century.
