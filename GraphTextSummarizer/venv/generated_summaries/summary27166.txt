In a recent New York Times interview, von Furstenberg stated: "Everything I do now is for the empowerment of women." Though von Furstenberg was only in her 20s, an awkward time for many young women, she proved to have perfect timing when she unleashed her little dress onto the American fashion landscape. Diane von Furstenberg gave this new woman something new to wear for her new career and new sex life. Perhaps she foresaw herself and other American women making graceful global moves. It liberated women from the stuffy and stiff male-imagined "working gal" get-ups; it was easy to put on and take off; it was smart, affordable and traveled well.
