While Janson has made a new life and family for herself in Thailand, her daughters -- whose remains eventually were found in Khao Lak -- are never far from her mind. For the first year, the facility cared for children and families directly affected by the tsunami. "It's a home for children and ... families actually in need," she said. And whenever possible, Janson works to keep families together. Although Barnhem means "orphanage" in Swedish, Janson says Barnhem is no longer an orphanage in the traditional sense.
