Mississippi Speaker of the House Philip Gunn described Upshaw as "an outstanding legislator and friend" and said "our hearts break for her family. The friend said she killed herself outside the house of former State Rep. Clint Rotenberry. Rotenberry and Upshaw, both single and divorced for a number of years, had been in a relationship, the friend said. Upshaw had been battling depression for several years and had been taking medication while under a doctor's care. The victim is Jessica Upshaw, 53, of Diamondhead, Mississippi.
