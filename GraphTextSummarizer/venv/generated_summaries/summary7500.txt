Fiscal policy would be mostly on automatic pilot and debt levels should decline continuously. Most other member countries, especially France, will have to undertake a major fiscal adjustment if they are to adhere to the limits of the new fiscal compact. The French and German leaders felt vindicated by the fact that their proposal had been accepted not only all euro area countries but also a number of other EU member countries. In the old Stability and Growth Pact, member countries had already solemnly declared their commitment to achieve a balanced budget over the cycle. Member countries from now have to observe a limit on their cyclically adjusted fiscal deficits of 0.5 % of GDP.
