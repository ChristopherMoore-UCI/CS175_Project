John McCain the attacks could come on two fronts - confined not just to his current spouse, the striking, elegant heiress Cindy Hensley McCain, but those rooted in his own marital history and the other Mrs. McCain before her - a woman of more modest means who is now virtually invisible. This is life - not for the presumptive presidential nominees Sen. Barack Obama and Sen. John McCain - but for their respective wives. In his memoirs, McCain writes that their break-up was caused by his "selfishness and immaturity"...."The blame was entirely mine." Carol doesn't often talk to the media but she doesn't think her accident or injuries ended her marriage. In 1969, while John McCain was a prisoner of war in Vietnam, Carol was badly disfigured in a car accident. The first Mrs. McCain was born Carol Shepp.
