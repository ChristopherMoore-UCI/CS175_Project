Abaaoud is believed to have joined ISIS in Syria in early 2014 after traveling from Belgium, according to Guy Van Vlierden, the editor of "emmejihad," a blog on Belgian foreign fighters. Abaaoud's family in Belgium, after hearing nothing from Abaaoud after he traveled to Syria, received word in October he had been killed in fighting, they revealed in interviews with the Belgian media. In a report Huver filed for the Belgian news channel RTBF,  Abaaoud is seen driving a car dragging the corpses of Free Syria army fighters. Other intelligence gathering indicated Abaaoud was linked to several senior ISIS operatives in Syria. Within hours, Belgian officials announced they had thwarted a major imminent terrorist attack by fighters who had returned from Syria.
