This is advance care planning, and it is a good thing for seniors, their families and health care professionals. Advance care planning is about examining options, planning and communicating the choices that the individual wants -- either to limit treatments, accept all treatments or something in between. Health care reform turned the welcome visit into an annual wellness visit. Advance care planning includes completing a living will and appointing a health care proxy. An individual's personal wishes, beliefs and values are among the most important factors when making care decisions brought about by a serious or life-limiting illness.
