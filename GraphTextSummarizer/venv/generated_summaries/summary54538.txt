For Frisina, taking a chance on players like Sunderland's Kenwyn Jones and Ryan Taylor of Wigan paid off -- and he found himself still around the top 100 by mid-season. -- "Incredible" is how John Frisina remembers the moment he logged on to Fantasy Premier League and discovered he was beating nearly two million other competitors. I was nowhere near the leaders before that." Frisina felt striking a balance between superstars and cheaper players was crucial to his fantasy football success -- as was a bit of luck. It's the stuff dreams are made of for the millions of people managing fantasy football teams in online leagues around the world every year. Frisina, who is Australian, went on to win the Fantasy Premier League in 2007/2008, beating hundreds of thousands of would-be managers both from England and around the globe in the process.
