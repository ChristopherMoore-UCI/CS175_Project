Single-hearted unity North Koreans seem to have an innate ability to form a line -- not a queue, but dead straight lines. People love to sing Most North Koreans can carry a tune and if you ask someone -- politely -- to sing a song they will probably oblige -- on the spot, with no accompaniment. There is a story that North Koreans will be punished if they give a Kim pin to a foreigner, but that's a myth and they are occasionally given to visitors. The pins show a portrait of Kim Il Sung or his son, Kim Jong Il -- and sometimes both. Kim pins All North Koreans wear a "Kim pin" on the left breast of whatever they are wearing.
