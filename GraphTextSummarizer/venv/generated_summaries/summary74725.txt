Google ads are a sprawling business and a person's face could show up on any of the 2 million sites that are part of the Google ad network. Read through and if you're still uncomfortable with the idea, uncheck the box next to "Based upon my activity, Google may show my name and profile photo in shared endorsements that appear in ads. If you are really determined not to show up anywhere, just don't hit +1 or leave reviews on Google products or in the Google Play store. As with Google+ reviews, the key to not appearing in these types of ads is not endorsing brands. Starting November 11, Google will be able to include Google+ users' faces, names and comments in ads.
