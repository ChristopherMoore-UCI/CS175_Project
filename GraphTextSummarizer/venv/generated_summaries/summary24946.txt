-- The last chapter in the story of the space shuttle Discovery ended Wednesday as the shuttle landed at Kennedy Space Center in Florida. Discovery's first launch was August 30, 1984. Discovery touched down just before noon ET as planned. The announcer on NASA TV called it "the end of a historic journey" and added, "To the ship that has led the way time and time again we say, 'Farewell, Discovery.' " Discovery retires having logged more than 148 million miles in orbit during 39 flights.
