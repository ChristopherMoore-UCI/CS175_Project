Even though the exterior looks the same as it did during integration -- the interior would be almost unrecognizable to LaNier and the other Little Rock Nine. -- Carlotta Walls LaNier points out the only two African-Americans in her senior class as she flips through her high school yearbook. It's been nearly five decades since LaNier graduated from Little Rock Central High School in Arkansas. Today, many students at Central High see themselves as the beneficiaries of an Obama presidency. "I had hoped to see something like that in the next 10 or 15 years when I was in high school but that didn't happen," says LaNier.
