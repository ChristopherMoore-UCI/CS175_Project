Clients and customers of banks not capable of meeting their financial obligations due to the toxic state of their balance sheets must be protected, with their assets and liabilities being transferred to safe havens -- regardless of the potential damage to existing shareholders. The EU should use its powers to force distressed banks into the arms of those banks with solid balance sheets. The large banks will be focused on corporate and investment banking and investment management, and retail banking will be dominated by large cash rich companies. The markets are now waiting for results from other banks, including JPMorgan's on Thursday. -- So finally, after long drawn out negotiations, German Chancellor Angela Merkel and French President Nicolas Sarkozy seem to have reached some consensus on the recapitalization of European banks.
