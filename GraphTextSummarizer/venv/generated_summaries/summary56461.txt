The center's importance in Islam is such that in June when U.S. President Barack Obama gave his one-and-only speech solely directed to Muslims, he delivered it from Al-Azhar. Tantawi was appointed by Egypt's President Hosni Mubarak in 1996. He condemned suicide bombing during speech in 2003 at a conference in the Malaysian capital of Kuala Lumpur. Tantawi, considered a moderate, was the grand imam at Al-Azhar -- an Islamic center and university in Cairo, Egypt.
