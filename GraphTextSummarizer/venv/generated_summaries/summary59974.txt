Here's a full list of which series will still be available on Netflix in February, according to Variety: Classic "Doctor Who" "Doctor Who" seasons 1 through 7 "Copper" seasons 1 and 2 "House of Cards" trilogy "Keeping Up Appearances" season 1 "Luther" seasons 1 through 3 "Monarch of the Glen" seasons 1 through 6 "North & South" "Robin Hood" seasons 1 through 3 "The Buccaneers" "The Office" U.K. seasons 1 and 2 "Top Gear" seasons 17 though 20 "Torchwood" seasons 1 through 4 "Wallander" seasons 1 through 3 "Sherlock," "Happy Valley," "The Honorable Woman" and "Call the Midwife" were not up for renewal and will remain on Netflix as well. Netflix confirmed on Friday that it's renewed the contracts of several BBC shows, including several fan favorites. Binge-watching fans of "Doctor Who" and "Luther" can breathe a sigh of relief. Fans of "Doctor Who" had launched an online petition to keep the series available. Last week, there were concerns that the shows might not return to Netflix because of an expiring contract.
