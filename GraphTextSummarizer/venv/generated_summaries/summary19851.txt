Medvedev said ahead of the meeting China was Russia's "first foreign trade partner," adding the volume of bilateral trade between the two countries doubled in the last six years. Under the 30-year gas deal, Russian state-owned energy giant Gazprom will supply 38 billion cubic meters of gas to China each year starting 2019. Europe is dependent on Russian energy, as around 30% of Europe's gas comes from Russia. Sanctions imposed on Russia by Western countries in the light of the conflict in Ukraine have slowed down its economic growth. -- As its relationship with the West worsen, Russia is pivoting east in an attempt to secure business with China.
