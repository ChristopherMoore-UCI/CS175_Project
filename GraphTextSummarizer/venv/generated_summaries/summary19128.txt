The TSA also found more than 1,400 firearm components, replica firearms, stun guns and other "dangerous objects" in carry-on luggage. Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport came in second place with 109 gun seizures, and Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport came in third place with 78 gun seizures. Fake guns aren't OK, either. Houston George Bush Intercontinental Airport had 77 gun seizures, while  Denver International Airport had 70.
